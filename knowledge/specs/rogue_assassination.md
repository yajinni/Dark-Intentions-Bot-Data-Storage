# Assassination Rogue Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Assassination Rogue is a master of poisons and bleeds. The gameplay revolves around maintaining powerful Damage-over-Time (DoT) effects on multiple targets to generate energy via **Venomous Wounds** and then spending that energy on high-impact finishers. The spec is defined by its strong sustained damage and explosive burst windows using **Deathmark** and **Kingsbane**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Assassination is about keeping your target(s) bleeding and poisoned while ensuring you don't over-cap Energy or Combo Points.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Venomous Wounds:** You regain Energy whenever a target bleeds from your Rupture or Garrote.
*   **Poisons:** Maintain **Deadly Poison** (or Amplifying) and **Atrophic Poison** on your weapons at all times.
*   **Master Assassin:** (If talented) Increases crit chance when attacking from Stealth or for a short time after Vanishing.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Garrote:** Maintain 100% uptime on your target.
2.  **Rupture:** Maintain with 5+ Combo Points.
3.  **Deathmark:** Your primary burst cooldown. Use on high-health targets.
4.  **Kingsbane:** Use on cooldown, ideally during Deathmark.
5.  **Envenom:** Spend 5+ Combo Points to deal damage and increase poison application chance.
6.  **Mutilate:** Primary Combo Point builder.
7.  **Thistle Tea:** Use to restore Energy during burst windows.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Deathmark:** Sync with all other burst cooldowns.
2.  **Kingsbane:** Use on cooldown (ensure target has bleeds/poisons).
3.  **Garrote:** Maintain uptime.
4.  **Rupture:** Maintain with 5+ CP.
5.  **Envenom:** Spend 5+ CP.
6.  **Tea:** Use if < 50 Energy.
7.  **Mutilate:** builder.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Crimson Tempest:** maintain on all targets (2+ targets).
2.  **Garrote & Rupture:** Multi-dot as many targets as possible for Energy regen.
3.  **Fan of Knives:** Primary builder for 3+ targets.
4.  **Envenom:** Filler spender if Crimson Tempest is active.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Deathmark:** 2-minute cooldown. Duplicates your poisons and bleeds on the target for 16 seconds. **Usage:** Your "big" button. Use on pull and every 2 minutes thereafter.
*   **Kingsbane:** 1-minute cooldown. Deals nature damage that increases with each poison application. **Usage:** Your "mini-burst". Sync with Thistle Tea for maximum poison procs.
*   **Vanish:** 2-minute cooldown. Use offensively to apply an empowered Garrote or reset Master Assassin.
*   **Thistle Tea:** 1-minute recharge (3 charges). Restores 100 Energy and increases Mastery. **Usage:** Use to fuel Kingsbane or avoid energy starvation.
*   **Cloak of Shadows:** 2-minute cooldown. Magic immunity and dispel. **Usage:** Defensive.
*   **Evasion:** 2-minute cooldown. 100% dodge chance. **Usage:** Defensive against physical damage.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Agility**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of all your poisons and bleeds.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Increases chance for double damage and additional CP.
4.  **Haste:** Increases energy regeneration and DoT tick frequency.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage increase and reduction.

**Note:** For Assassination, **Mastery** is typically the most important secondary stat as it powers your signature bleed and poison damage.
