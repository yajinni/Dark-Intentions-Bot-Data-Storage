# Affliction Warlock Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Affliction Warlock is a ranged DPS spec that focuses on managing multiple **Damage-over-Time (DoT)** effects and spending **Soul Shards** on powerful spells like **Unstable Affliction** and **Seed of Corruption**. The spec is defined by its sustained damage and its ability to deal massive damage through its primary cooldown, **Summon Darkglare**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Affliction is about maintaining your DoTs and ensure you are always building toward your next burst window.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Agony:** Your primary resource generator. Maintain 100% uptime to generate Soul Shards.
*   **Corruption:** A steady damage DoT.
*   **Unstable Affliction:** Your primary Soul Shard spender for single-target damage.
*   **Soul Shards:** Your primary resource. Build with Agony and spend on high-impact abilities.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Agony:** Maintain 100% uptime on all targets.
2.  **Corruption:** Maintain 100% uptime on all targets.
3.  **Haunt:** Use on cooldown (increases all DoT damage).
4.  **Unstable Affliction:** Spend Soul Shards (don't cap at 5).
5.  **Dark Harvest:** Use on cooldown (with DoTs active).
6.  **Drain Soul:** Your primary filler and execute tool.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Summon Darkglare:** Primary burst cooldown. Pool shards before use.
2.  **Haunt:** Use on cooldown.
3.  **Unstable Affliction:** Spend 5 Soul Shards immediately.
4.  **Agony & Corruption:** Maintain 100% uptime.
5.  **Dark Harvest:** Use on cooldown.
6.  **Drain Soul:** Primary filler. Use to snipe dying targets for shards.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Seed of Corruption:** Primary AoE burst and Corruption application for 3+ targets.
2.  **Agony:** Multi-dot as many targets as possible (up to 5-8).
3.  **Vile Taint / Phantom Singularity:** Use on cooldown for massive AoE damage.
4.  **Dark Harvest:** High priority AoE damage.
5.  **Unstable Affliction:** maintain on priority targets within the pack.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Summon Darkglare:** 2-minute cooldown. Extends the duration of all active DoTs. **Usage:** Your "big" button. Use on pull and every 2 minutes. Ensure all DoTs are active and refreshed before use.
*   **Dark Harvest:** 1-minute cooldown. Increases damage based on the number of periodic effects active. **Usage:** Use on cooldown.
*   **Phantom Singularity / Vile Taint:** 45-second or 1-minute cooldown. **Usage:** Essential for AoE burst and DoT maintenance.
*   **Unending Resolve:** 3-minute cooldown. 40% damage reduction and interrupt immunity. **Usage:** Primary defensive.
*   **Soul Rot:** (If talented) Primary burst generator.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Haste:** Critical for reducing cast times and increasing DoT tick frequency.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Increases chance for double damage.
4.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of all your periodic effects.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Affliction, **Haste** and **Crit** are the two stats that define the spec's pace and sustained damage potential.
