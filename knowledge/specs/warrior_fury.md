# Fury Warrior Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Fury Warrior is a frenetic, dual-wielding melee DPS spec that revolves around building **Rage** to spend on **Rampage**, which triggers **Enrage**. The spec is defined by its extremely high APM and constant damage. The gameplay focuses on maintaining Enrage uptime and using powerful builder abilities to fuel a relentless assault.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Fury is about staying active and ensuring you are always Enraged while maximizing your Rage efficiency.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Enrage:** Increases your damage dealt by 11%, Haste by 25%, and movement speed by 10%. This is the lynchpin of the spec.
*   **Improved Whirlwind:** After using Whirlwind or Thunder Clap, your next two single-target attacks hit up to 4 additional targets.
*   **Reckless Abandon:** (If talented) Recklessness and Avatar empower your Bloodthirst and Raging Blow into **Bloodbath** and **Crushing Blow**.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Avatar:** Use on cooldown (align with Recklessness).
2.  **Recklessness:** Your primary burst cooldown. Use on pull and every 1.5-2 minutes.
3.  **Rampage:** Spend Rage to maintain Enrage. Use at 80+ Rage.
4.  **Execute:** Use whenever the target is below 20% health or on **Sudden Death** procs.
5.  **Odyn's Fury:** Use for burst AoE and to instantly trigger Enrage.
6.  **Bloodthirst:** Use to generate Rage and potentially trigger Enrage.
7.  **Raging Blow:** Primary filler to generate Rage.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Slayer Priority)
1.  **Recklessness:** Sync with Avatar.
2.  **Rampage:** Spend Rage to trigger Enrage.
3.  **Bloodbath:** Use during Recklessness.
4.  **Execute:** Use with Sudden Death or in execute range.
5.  **Crushing Blow:** Use during Recklessness.
6.  **Raging Blow:** Maintain 0-1 charges.
7.  **Bloodthirst:** filler.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Whirlwind / Thunder Clap:** Must be active for the cleave buff (Improved Whirlwind).
2.  **Odyn's Fury:** Massive AoE burst.
3.  **Rampage:** Use to spend Rage and cleave.
4.  **Bladestorm:** Use during Recklessness for massive AoE damage.
5.  **Thunder Blast:** (Mountain Thane) Spend procs immediately.
6.  **Execute:** Cleaves via Reap the Storm (Slayer).

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Recklessness:** 1.5-minute or 2-minute cooldown. Increases Rage generation and Crit. **Usage:** Your primary throughput cooldown.
*   **Avatar:** 1.5-minute or 2-minute cooldown. 20% damage increase. **Usage:** Always pair with Recklessness.
*   **Odyn's Fury:** 45-second cooldown. Massive AoE and applies a bleed. **Usage:** Use on cooldown for burst and Enrage.
*   **Bladestorm:** 1.5-minute cooldown. High-damage AoE and CC immunity. **Usage:** Use for burst AoE and resource generation.
*   **Enraged Regeneration:** 2-minute cooldown. 30% damage reduction and massive self-healing via Bloodthirst. **Usage:** Primary defensive.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Strength**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases damage dealt while Enraged.
3.  **Haste:** Essential for reducing the GCD and increasing Rage generation.
4.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.
5.  **Critical Strike:** Generally the lowest priority as Recklessness provides a large amount of Crit.

**Note:** For Fury, **Mastery** and **Haste** are the two stats that define your sustained damage and rotational speed.
