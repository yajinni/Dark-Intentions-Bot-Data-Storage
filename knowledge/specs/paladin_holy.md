# Holy Paladin Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Holy Paladin is a "battle-healer" that thrives in melee range. It utilizes **Holy Power** (HP) to cast powerful instant heals. The spec is defined by its strong single-target throughput and the ability to mitigate massive damage via **Aura Mastery**. The gameplay revolves around generating HP with **Holy Shock** and spending it efficiently on **Eternal Flame** or **Light of Dawn**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Holy Paladin is about keeping Holy Power flowing and using your big heals when they are most needed.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Holy Power:** Generate with Holy Shock, Judgment, and Crusader Strike; spend on Eternal Flame or Light of Dawn.
*   **Beacons:** Place **Beacon of Light** on the tank or low-health allies to mirror a portion of your healing to them.
*   **Infusion of Light:** Procs from Holy Shock crits; empowers your next Flash of Light or Holy Light.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Holy Shock:** Use on cooldown to generate Holy Power.
2.  **Eternal Flame / Word of Glory:** Spend 3 Holy Power on single-target healing.
3.  **Light of Dawn:** Spend 3 Holy Power for group/AoE healing.
4.  **Judgment:** Use to generate Holy Power (if talented) and for minor damage.
5.  **Flash of Light:** Use as a fast filler with **Infusion of Light** procs.
6.  **Hammer of Wrath:** Use during Wings or on low-health targets for Holy Power.

## Rotation Priority

### Healing Priority
1.  **Divine Toll:** Use for massive burst group stabilization.
2.  **Holy Shock:** Maintain 100% cooldown efficiency.
3.  **Holy Power Spenders:** Use at 3-5 HP. Do not cap.
4.  **Holy Prism:** Use for supplemental AoE healing.
5.  **Flash of Light:** Primary filler for emergency stabilization.
6.  **Holy Light:** Efficient but slow filler for low-damage phases.

### Damage Priority
1.  **Consecration:** Keep active under the enemies.
2.  **Hammer of Wrath:** High priority for damage and HP generation.
3.  **Judgment:** Use on cooldown.
4.  **Crusader Strike:** Filler to generate HP when healing is not needed.
5.  **Shield of the Righteous:** Spend excess HP for damage and armor.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Avenging Wrath (Wings):** 2-minute cooldown. Increases all healing and crit chance. **Usage:** Your primary throughput cooldown. Use for heavy healing phases.
*   **Aura Mastery:** 3-minute cooldown. Empowers your Aura (e.g., Devotion Aura for 20% DR). **Usage:** Coordinate with the raid for massive group-wide damage events.
*   **Divine Toll:** 1-minute cooldown. Casts 5 Holy Shocks instantly. **Usage:** Excellent for reactive burst healing or quick HP generation.
*   **Lay on Hands:** Long cooldown. 100% health heal. **Usage:** Emergency save.
*   **Blessing of Protection (BoP) / Sacrifice:** Utility cooldowns to protect allies.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases healing based on proximity to the target (or Beacon).
3.  **Haste:** Reduces cooldown of Holy Shock and reduces the GCD.
4.  **Critical Strike:** Increases the frequency of Infusion of Light procs.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage reduction and healing increase.

**Note:** For Holy Paladin, **Mastery** and **Haste** are typically the top secondaries for throughput and rotation smoothness respectively.
