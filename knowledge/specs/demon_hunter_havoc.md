# Havoc Demon Hunter Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Havoc Demon Hunter is a high-mobility, fast-paced melee DPS spec. It focuses on generating Fury and spending it on powerful abilities like **Blade Dance** and **Eye Beam**. The spec thrives on high uptime and strategic use of its burst window, **Metamorphosis**, which is frequently triggered through the **Demonic** talent.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Havoc is a GCD-locked spec, meaning you should always be pressing an ability. The primary goal is to maintain Fury and use your high-impact abilities as often as possible.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Fury Management:** Build with generators like **Felblade** or **Immolation Aura**; spend with **Blade Dance** and **Chaos Strike**.
*   **Demonic:** Using **Eye Beam** puts you into Demon Form for a short duration. Maximize damage during this window.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Immolation Aura:** Keep active as much as possible for passive Fury and damage.
2.  **Eye Beam:** Use on cooldown to trigger Demon Form.
3.  **Death Sweep:** (Demon Form version of Blade Dance) Use immediately after Eye Beam.
4.  **Blade Dance:** Use on cooldown as your primary spender.
5.  **The Hunt:** Use on cooldown (setup for massive Eye Beam damage via Eternal Hunt).
6.  **Felblade:** Use to generate Fury and close gaps.
7.  **Chaos Strike / Annihilation:** Use as your primary Fury spender/filler.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Felblade:** If **Inertia** is ready.
2.  **The Hunt:** On cooldown.
3.  **Death Sweep:** Priority spender in Demon Form.
4.  **Immolation Aura:** If at 2 charges to avoid capping.
5.  **Vengeful Retreat:** To trigger **Initiative**.
6.  **Eye Beam / Abyssal Gaze:** On cooldown.
7.  **Metamorphosis:** Use when Eye Beam and Death Sweep are on cooldown to maximize the reset.
8.  **Blade Dance:** On cooldown.
9.  **Annihilation / Chaos Strike:** Filler.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Immolation Aura:** Maintain 100% uptime.
2.  **Eye Beam:** Massive AoE burst and triggers Demon Form.
3.  **Death Sweep / Blade Dance:** Primary cleave damage.
4.  **The Hunt:** Use on priority targets within the pack.
5.  **Sigil of Flame:** If talented, use for additional AoE damage and Fury.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Metamorphosis:** 2-minute cooldown. The ultimate burst tool. Resets Eye Beam and Blade Dance. **Usage:** Use when you need peak damage, ensuring your core abilities are on cooldown first to get the most from the reset.
*   **The Hunt:** 1-minute cooldown. Deals massive damage and grants a leech buff. **Usage:** In current builds, it empowers the next Eye Beam (+100% damage), making it a key part of your burst sequence.
*   **Eye Beam:** 30-second cooldown (approx). Your most important "mini-cooldown". Triggers Demon Form and deals heavy Chaos damage.
*   **Blur:** 1-minute cooldown. Increases Dodge by 50% and reduces all damage taken by 20%. **Usage:** Use frequently to mitigate predictable damage or when at low health.
*   **Netherwalk:** 3-minute cooldown (if talented). 100% damage immunity but prevents attacking. **Usage:** Use to ignore one-shot mechanics or critical dangerous phases.

## Stat Priority
1.  **Agility**
2.  **Critical Strike:** Highly valuable due to **Know Your Enemy** (crit damage bonus).
3.  **Mastery:** Strong multiplier for almost all core damage.
4.  **Haste:** Generally lower priority due to the massive Haste gained in Metamorphosis.
5.  **Versatility:** Good for flat damage and defense, but usually trails behind Crit/Mastery.

**Note:** For Havoc, **Crit** is usually the king of secondary stats because it fuels multiple talent synergies.
