# Arcane Mage Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Arcane Mage is a high-burst ranged DPS spec that revolves around mana management and heavy-hitting spell windows. It uses **Arcane Charges** to empower its spells, primarily **Arcane Blast** and **Arcane Barrage**. The gameplay follows a cycle of building charges and spending them, while aligning massive cooldowns like **Arcane Surge** and **Touch of the Magi** for explosive damage.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Modern Arcane focuses on using powerful cooldowns as soon as they are available rather than the strict mana-based "Burn and Conserve" cycles of the past.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Arcane Charges:** Build up to 4 to maximize damage.
*   **Arcane Salvo:** Build stacks (up to 20) through your rotation; spend with Arcane Barrage for massive damage.
*   **Clearcasting:** Procs from spending mana; makes your next **Arcane Missiles** free and channel faster.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Arcane Surge + Touch of the Magi:** Your primary burst window. Always use together.
2.  **Arcane Orb:** Use to instantly gain 4 Arcane Charges.
3.  **Arcane Barrage:** Use at 4 Arcane Charges when you have 20 stacks of **Arcane Salvo**.
4.  **Arcane Blast:** Your primary filler and charge builder.
5.  **Arcane Missiles:** Use only when **Clearcasting** is active.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Arcane Surge:** Use when at high mana.
2.  **Touch of the Magi:** Always use during Arcane Surge or on cooldown.
3.  **Arcane Orb:** Use after Arcane Barrage to reset charges.
4.  **Arcane Barrage:** At 4 charges with 20 stacks of **Arcane Salvo**.
5.  **Arcane Blast:** Spend mana to build charges.
6.  **Arcane Missiles:** Use with **Clearcasting** procs.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Arcane Orb:** High priority for charge generation.
2.  **Arcane Barrage:** Use at 4 charges for cleave damage.
3.  **Arcane Explosion / Arcane Pulse:** Primary AoE fillers to build charges.
4.  **Touch of the Magi:** Use on a priority target within the pack.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Arcane Surge:** 1.5-minute cooldown. Spends all mana to deal massive damage and grants a 35% damage buff. Increases mana regen for its duration. **Usage:** Your "big" cooldown.
*   **Touch of the Magi:** 45-second cooldown. Stores damage dealt for 12 seconds and then explodes. Generates 4 Arcane Charges. **Usage:** Sync with Arcane Surge or use as a mini-burst.
*   **Evocation:** 2-minute cooldown. Restores 100% mana. **Usage:** Use just before Arcane Surge to ensure you are at full mana for the burst.
*   **Time Warp:** 5-minute cooldown. 30% Haste for 40 seconds.
*   **Greater Invisibility:** 2-minute cooldown. Immediate threat drop and 60% damage reduction. **Usage:** Defensive and survival.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases Arcane Charge damage bonus and mana regeneration.
3.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.
4.  **Critical Strike:** Increases chance for double damage.
5.  **Haste:** Generally the lowest priority as Arcane has many built-in haste buffs.

**Note:** **Mastery** is the strongest secondary stat for Arcane due to its direct impact on mana and charge efficiency.
