# Retribution Paladin Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Retribution Paladin is a burst-heavy melee DPS spec that focuses on generating **Holy Power** (HP) and spending it on powerful finishers like **Final Verdict** and **Divine Storm**. The spec is characterized by its high survivability and strong utility. The gameplay revolves around a straightforward priority system of building and spending HP, while aligning massive burst windows like **Avenging Wrath**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Retribution is a highly accessible spec with a clear build-and-spend cycle.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Holy Power:** Generate with abilities like Judgment, Blade of Justice, and Hammer of Wrath; spend on Final Verdict or Divine Storm.
*   **Art of War:** Procs that reset the cooldown of **Blade of Justice**.
*   **Final Reckoning / Execution Sentence:** (If talented) Debuffs that increase the damage the target takes from your Holy Power spenders.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Avenging Wrath:** Use on cooldown for massive burst.
2.  **Wake of Ashes:** Use on cooldown (generates 3 HP and deals massive damage).
3.  **Final Verdict:** Spend 3-5 Holy Power on single-target.
4.  **Divine Storm:** Spend 3-5 Holy Power for 2+ targets.
5.  **Blade of Justice:** Primary generator.
6.  **Judgment:** secondary generator.
7.  **Hammer of Wrath:** Use on targets < 20% HP or during Wings.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Wake of Ashes:** On cooldown.
2.  **Hammer of Light:** (Templar Hero Talent) Highest priority spender/generator.
3.  **Divine Toll:** On cooldown.
4.  **Final Verdict:** Primary Holy Power spender.
5.  **Blade of Justice:** Use with **Art of War** procs.
6.  **Judgment:** Generate Holy Power.
7.  **Hammer of Wrath:** High priority in execute or Wings.
8.  **Blade of Justice:** Regular generator.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Wake of Ashes:** Massive AoE burst and HP.
2.  **Divine Storm:** Primary HP spender for 2+ targets.
3.  **Divine Toll:** Spreads Judgment to multiple targets.
4.  **Blade of Justice:** Prioritize targets without the debuff.
5.  **Judgment:** Use to build HP.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Avenging Wrath (Wings):** 2-minute cooldown. Increases damage, healing, and crit chance by 20%. **Usage:** Your primary burst. Always use on cooldown.
*   **Wake of Ashes:** 45-second cooldown. Massive burst AoE and generates 3 HP. **Usage:** Essential for both AoE and single-target resource generation.
*   **Divine Toll:** 1-minute cooldown. Casts Judgment on up to 5 targets. **Usage:** Great for quick HP generation and burst.
*   **Shield of Vengeance:** 1.5-minute cooldown. Absorb shield that explodes for damage. **Usage:** Proactive defensive/offensive tool.
*   **Divine Shield (Bubble):** 5-minute cooldown. Total immunity. **Usage:** Your ultimate panic button.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Strength**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases all Holy damage dealt. Strongest stat for sustained and burst damage.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Highly valuable for damage and talent procs.
4.  **Haste:** Reduces cooldowns and increases attack speed.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Retribution, **Mastery** and **Crit** are typically the most important secondaries for maximizing their signature Holy damage.
