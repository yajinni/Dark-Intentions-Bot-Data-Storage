# Vengeance Demon Hunter Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Vengeance Demon Hunter is a highly mobile tank spec that relies on a combination of high armor (via **Demon Spikes**), self-healing (via **Soul Fragments**), and proactive damage reduction. It excels in crowd control and utility through its various **Sigils**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Vengeance focuses on maintaining high uptime on active mitigation while managing Fury and Soul Fragments to heal back damage.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Demon Spikes:** Your primary active mitigation. Increases armor significantly. Try to keep one charge rolling when actively taking physical damage.
*   **Soul Fragments:** Generated by abilities like **Fracture** and **Sigil of Flame**. Consumed for healing and to power **Spirit Bomb**.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Immolation Aura:** Keep active for Fury generation and passive damage.
2.  **Sigil of Flame:** Keep active on the target for damage and resource generation.
3.  **Fracture:** Use to generate Soul Fragments and Fury.
4.  **Spirit Bomb:** Use whenever you have 5+ Soul Fragments for burst healing and damage.
5.  **Soul Cleave:** Use to spend excess Fury or for immediate healing if Spirit Bomb isn't ready.
6.  **Felblade:** Use to generate Fury and close gaps.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target & AoE (General Priority)
1.  **Metamorphosis:** Use as a primary proactive defensive/offensive burst.
2.  **Fiery Brand:** Primary single-target damage reduction (40%). Use on the most dangerous target.
3.  **Soul Carver:** Use on cooldown for high damage and fragment generation.
4.  **Sigil of Spite:** Use on cooldown for damage and 3 Soul Fragments.
5.  **Sigil of Flame:** Maintain DoT uptime.
6.  **Spirit Bomb:** Use at 5+ Soul Fragments.
7.  **Fracture:** Primary resource generator. Use to build up to 5 fragments.
8.  **Soul Cleave:** secondary spender for immediate healing or to dump Fury.
9.  **Immolation Aura:** Use on cooldown.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Metamorphosis:** 2-minute cooldown. Increases current and maximum health by 50% and armor by 200%. **Usage:** Use proactively before heavy damage phases or to stabilize during large pulls.
*   **Fiery Brand:** 1-minute cooldown. Reduces all damage the target deals to you by 40% for 8 seconds. **Usage:** Your strongest single-target defensive. Use against boss "tank-buster" abilities.
*   **Demon Spikes:** 2 charges, 20-second recharge. Increases armor based on Agility. **Usage:** Your bread-and-butter mitigation. Avoid "capping" at 2 charges; try to have one active during combat.
*   **Sigil of Silence:** 1-minute cooldown. Silences all enemies in the area. **Usage:** Essential for managing caster mobs.
*   **Sigil of Chains:** 1.5-minute cooldown (if talented). Pulls all enemies to the center of the sigil. **Usage:** Great for grouping mobs for AoE and interrupts.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Agility**
2.  **Haste:** Reduces cooldowns of Demon Spikes and Fracture, and lowers the GCD. Crucial for smooth play.
3.  **Versatility / Critical Strike:** Versatility for flat damage reduction; Crit for damage and Parry chance.
4.  **Mastery:** Increases the armor bonus from Demon Spikes. Generally the weakest defensive stat compared to Haste/Vers.

**Note:** **Haste** is the most important secondary stat for Vengeance as it directly increases the frequency of your active mitigation and resource generation.
