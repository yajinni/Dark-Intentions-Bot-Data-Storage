# Elemental Shaman Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Elemental Shaman is a ranged DPS spec that focuses on managing **Maelstrom** and maintaining powerful elemental effects. The spec is defined by its strong burst potential and its ability to deal massive damage through **Stormkeeper** and **Ascendance**. The gameplay involves a priority system of building Maelstrom with **Lightning Bolt** and **Lava Burst**, and then spending it on **Earth Shock** or **Earthquake**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Elemental is about maintaining your DoTs and ensure you are always building toward your next burst window.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Flame Shock:** Your primary DoT. Maintain 100% uptime on your target(s).
*   **Maelstrom:** Your primary resource. Build it to spend on heavy-hitting abilities.
*   **Tempest:** (If talented) Replaces Lightning Bolt/Chain Lightning with a more powerful version.
*   **Lava Surge:** Procs from Flame Shock ticks; makes your next Lava Burst instant and deal more damage.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Flame Shock:** Maintain 100% uptime.
2.  **Stormkeeper:** Use on cooldown (with Ascendance).
3.  **Ascendance:** Your primary burst cooldown. Sync with Power Infusion or Bloodlust.
4.  **Tempest:** Cast as soon as available.
5.  **Earth Shock:** Spend Maelstrom (don't cap at 100).
6.  **Lava Burst:** Use on cooldown or with **Lava Surge** procs.
7.  **Lightning Bolt:** Primary filler to generate Maelstrom.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Stormkeeper:** On cooldown.
2.  **Ascendance:** Primary burst window.
3.  **Flame Shock:** Maintain uptime.
4.  **Tempest:** Highest priority generator.
5.  **Earth Shock:** Primary Maelstrom spender.
6.  **Lava Burst:** Use with procs or on cooldown.
7.  **Lightning Bolt:** main filler.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Voltaic Blaze:** Use to spread Flame Shock.
2.  **Stormkeeper:** Enhance Chain Lightning for massive AoE burst.
3.  **Tempest:** Massive AoE damage.
4.  **Earthquake:** Primary Maelstrom spender for 3+ targets.
5.  **Chain Lightning:** Primary builder for 3+ targets.

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Stormkeeper:** 1-minute cooldown. Enhances your next two Lightning Bolts or Chain Lightnings. **Usage:** Your "mini-burst". In AoE, wait for 5+ targets if possible.
*   **Ascendance:** 2-minute or 3-minute cooldown (depending on talents). Transforms you into an elemental ascendant for 15 seconds. **Usage:** Your "big" cooldown. Sync with all other burst tools.
*   **Fire Elemental / Storm Elemental:** 2.5-minute cooldown. Summons an elemental to assist you. **Usage:** Use on cooldown for passive damage and resource generation.
*   **Spiritwalker's Grace:** 2-minute cooldown. Allows casting while moving for 15 seconds. **Usage:** Essential for maintaining uptime during movement-heavy phases.
*   **Astral Shift:** 2-minute cooldown. 40% damage reduction. **Usage:** Primary defensive.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of your signature abilities and resource generation. (Aim for ~50%)
3.  **Haste:** Increases cast speed and resource generation. (Aim for ~25%)
4.  **Critical Strike:** Increases chance for double damage. (Aim for ~25%)
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Elemental, **Mastery** is typically the most important secondary stat for maximizing their elemental damage.
