# Survival Hunter Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Survival Hunter is a melee-oriented DPS spec that focuses on managing **Focus** and leveraging the **Tip of the Spear** mechanic. Unlike other hunters, Survival fights in melee range but retains significant ranged utility through **Wildfire Bomb** and **Kill Command**. The spec thrives on high-uptime and strategic ability sequencing to maximize damage multipliers.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Survival is about building stacks of **Tip of the Spear** with Kill Command and spending them on your harder-hitting abilities.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Tip of the Spear:** Kill Command generates stacks (up to 3). Each stack increases the damage of your next non-Kill Command ability. **The Golden Rule:** Never waste a stack; always empower your big hits.
*   **Focus Management:** Kill Command generates Focus; Raptor Strike and Wildfire Bomb spend it.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Kill Command:** Use to generate **Tip of the Spear** stacks (try to have at least 1-2).
2.  **Takedown:** Use on cooldown (with stacks).
3.  **Boomstick:** Use on cooldown (with stacks).
4.  **Wildfire Bomb:** Use on cooldown (with stacks).
5.  **Moonlight Chakram:** (If talented) Use on cooldown (with stacks).
6.  **Raptor Strike:** Use as a filler to spend excess Focus and stacks.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Pack Leader Priority)
1.  **Kill Command:** Generate and maintain Tip of the Spear stacks.
2.  **Takedown:** Use on cooldown (preferably with 2+ stacks).
3.  **Wildfire Bomb:** Use on cooldown (always with stacks).
4.  **Boomstick:** Use on cooldown (always with stacks).
5.  **Kill Command:** If you have 0 stacks or a "Howl of the Pack Leader" proc.
6.  **Raptor Strike:** Use to spend excess Focus.

### Multi-Target (AoE) (Sentinel Priority)
1.  **Kill Command:** Generate stacks.
2.  **Boomstick:** Primary AoE burst. Use on cooldown.
3.  **Wildfire Bomb:** Prioritize targets with **Sentinel's Mark**.
4.  **Takedown:** Use on cooldown for the 20% damage buff.
5.  **Moonlight Chakram:** Use on cooldown.
6.  **Butchery / Carve:** Use for additional AoE burst and to reduce the cooldown of Wildfire Bomb.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Takedown:** 1.5-minute cooldown. Deals massive damage and increases all damage done by 20% for 10 seconds. **Usage:** Your primary offensive burst tool.
*   **Spearhead:** 1.5-minute cooldown (if talented). Charges the target, increases Kill Command damage, and makes Kill Command have a chance to reset its own cooldown. **Usage:** Use for high-intensity burst windows.
*   **Flamefang Pitch:** 1-minute cooldown. Ground-targeted AoE.
*   **Exhilaration:** 2-minute cooldown. 30% self-heal.
*   **Aspect of the Turtle:** 3-minute cooldown. 8-second immunity.
*   **Survival of the Fittest:** 3-minute cooldown (2 charges). 20% damage reduction.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Mastery:** Increases all damage and provides minor damage reduction. Strongest stat for Survival.
2.  **Critical Strike:** Highly valuable for damage and talent procs.
3.  **Haste:** Increases Focus regen and reduces GCD. Interchangeable with Crit for some builds.
4.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** **Mastery** is consistently the top stat for Survival across almost all scenarios.
