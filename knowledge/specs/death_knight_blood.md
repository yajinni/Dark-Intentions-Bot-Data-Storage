# Blood Death Knight Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Blood Death Knight is a reactive tank spec that relies on high self-healing through **Death Strike** and damage mitigation via **Bone Shield**. Key gameplay revolves around managing Runic Power to heal back damage taken and keeping Bone Shield stacks high to maintain armor and throughput.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
The "Easy Mode" for Blood Death Knight focuses on a simplified priority to maintain survival while dealing steady damage.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Death and Decay:** Always stand in it to enable **Cleaving Strikes** (Heart Strike hits more targets).
2.  **Death Strike:** Use if low on health or to dump Runic Power (if > 75 RP).
3.  **Blood Boil:** Use to apply **Blood Plague**, or if you have 2 charges, or during a **Boiling Point** proc.
4.  **Reaper's Mark:** Use on cooldown (if available).
5.  **Exterminate:** Use charges generated via Marrowrend.
6.  **Dancing Rune Weapon:** Use on cooldown for high resource generation and parry.
7.  **Marrowrend:** Use if Bone Shield is about to expire or if you have fewer than 6 stacks.
8.  **Heart Strike:** Use as a filler if you have more than 2 Runes.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Sustained)
1.  **Marrowrend:** If Bone Shield is below 3 stacks or about to expire.
2.  **Death Strike:** If Runic Power is above 80 or if significant healing is required.
3.  **Death and Decay:** If **Crimson Scourge** procs.
4.  **Heart Strike:** If you have 3 or more runes available.
5.  **Blood Boil:** If you have 2 charges.

### Multi-Target (Sustained)
1.  **Death and Decay:** Ensure it is active and you are standing in it to cleave with Heart Strike.
2.  **Blood Boil:** Prioritize keeping **Blood Plague** active on all targets.
3.  **Death Strike:** Use for healing and to spend Runic Power.
4.  **Heart Strike:** Use while in Death and Decay to hit multiple targets.
5.  **Consumption:** Use on cooldown if talented for burst AOE healing.

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Dancing Rune Weapon (DRW):** 2-minute cooldown. Increases Parry by 40% and mimics your attacks. **Usage:** Use offensively for massive Runic Power generation and defensively for the parry. Marrowrend gives 6+ stacks of Bone Shield during DRW.
*   **Vampiric Blood:** 1.5-minute cooldown. Increases maximum health by 30% and all healing received by 30%. **Usage:** Use frequently (short CD) when expecting moderate damage or to help healers.
*   **Anti-Magic Shell (AMS):** 1-minute cooldown. Absorbs magic damage and prevents the application of harmful magical debuffs. **Usage:** Use to ignore specific magic-based mechanics or during heavy magic damage phases.
*   **Icebound Fortitude:** 3-minute cooldown. Reduces all damage taken by 30% and makes you immune to stuns. **Usage:** Save for predictable major physical hits or to break out of a stun.
*   **Lichborne:** 2-minute cooldown. Grants 10% Leech and immunity to Fear, Sleep, and Charm. **Usage:** Use for a minor defensive boost via Leech or to counter specific CC mechanics.
*   **Tombstone:** 1-minute cooldown. Consumes up to 5 Bone Shield stacks to grant Runic Power and a damage absorption shield. **Usage:** Use to generate emergency Runic Power or to cycle cooldowns via talent synergies.
