# Restoration Shaman Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Restoration Shaman is a powerful reactive healer that excels at group-wide stabilization and utility. The spec is defined by its strong totem-based utility, high-throughput cooldowns like **Healing Tide Totem**, and unique damage reduction through **Spirit Link Totem**. The gameplay revolves around managing **Earth Shield** and **Water Shield** while using a priority system of reactive heals and proactive totems.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Restoration is about maintaining your core buffs and using your powerful totems to manage group health during high-damage periods.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Earth Shield:** Maintain 100% uptime on the tank and yourself for increased healing received and passive stabilization.
*   **Water Shield:** Always active on yourself for mana regeneration.
*   **Farseer Synergy:** (If talented) **Ancestral Swiftness** and **Unleash Life** can spawn Ancestors to assist in healing.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Earth Shield & Water Shield:** Maintain at all times.
2.  **Riptide:** Use on cooldown to stabilize and trigger buffs.
3.  **Healing Stream / Stormstream Totem:** Use whenever you have charges or during moderate damage.
4.  **Healing Rain:** Place on the ground for stacked group healing (also deals damage via **Acid Rain**).
5.  **Chain Heal:** Primary group triage tool.
6.  **Healing Wave:** Primary single-target efficiency filler.
7.  **Flash Flood:** (Proc) Use to cast faster heals.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Healing Priority
1.  **Divine Toll / Divine Favor:** (If talented) Use for massive burst group stabilization.
2.  **Riptide:** Maintain maximum uptime.
3.  **Healing Stream / Stormstream Totem:** On cooldown.
4.  **Healing Rain:** Place if 3+ players are stacked.
5.  **Chain Heal:** Spend procs or use for group damage.
6.  **Healing Wave:** Single-target triage.
7.  **Lava Burst:** Use for free (if talented) for damage and minor healing.

### Damage Priority
1.  **Healing Rain (Acid Rain):** Keep active on enemies.
2.  **Flame Shock:** Maintain DoT on primary targets.
3.  **Lava Burst:** Use on cooldown.
4.  **Chain Lightning:** Primary AoE builder.
5.  **Lightning Bolt:** Single-target filler.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Spirit Link Totem:** 3-minute cooldown. Redistributes health and provides 10% damage reduction. **Usage:** Your "raid save". Use for massive burst damage that threatens to kill individual players.
*   **Healing Tide Totem:** 3-minute cooldown. Massive raid-wide healing. **Usage:** Use during heavy spread damage.
*   **Ascendance:** 3-minute cooldown. Massive throughput boost. **Usage:** Use proactively for extreme damage windows.
*   **Mana Tide Totem:** 3-minute cooldown. Increases mana regen for nearby healers. **Usage:** Use at ~60-70% mana for the group.
*   **Astral Shift:** 2-minute cooldown. 40% damage reduction. **Usage:** Primary defensive.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Critical Strike:** Critical for throughput and mana returns via **Resurgence**.
3.  **Versatility = Mastery:**
    *   **Mastery:** Increases healing on low-health targets. Essential for progression.
    *   **Versatility:** Recommended for high Mythic+ for flat damage reduction.
4.  **Haste:** Generally the lowest priority unless focusing on a specific DPS-heavy build.

**Note:** For Restoration, **Crit** is the lynchpin of both your throughput and your mana sustainability.
