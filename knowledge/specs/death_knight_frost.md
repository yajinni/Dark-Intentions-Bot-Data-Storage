# Frost Death Knight Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Frost Death Knight is a fast-paced DPS spec focused on dual-wielding or two-handed weapon combat. It revolves around managing two primary resources: Runes and Runic Power. The spec is known for its heavy burst damage windows and the choice between two distinct playstyles: the bursty **Obliteration** build and the sustained **Breath of Sindragosa** build.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
The "Easy Mode" for Frost Death Knight focuses on a simplified rotation to maximize the value of **Pillar of Frost** and **Reaper's Mark** while maintaining high uptime on key damage abilities.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Killing Machine:** Makes your next **Obliterate** a guaranteed Critical Strike and deal Frost damage.
*   **Rime:** Makes your next **Howling Blast** free and deal significantly increased damage.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Empower Rune Weapon:** Use to kick off your burst.
2.  **Reaper's Mark:** Align with Pillar of Frost.
3.  **Remorseless Winter:** Keep active on cooldown.
4.  **Howling Blast:** Use whenever a **Rime** proc is active.
5.  **Frostwyrm's Fury:** Use during Pillar of Frost for massive burst.
6.  **Breath of Sindragosa:** If talented, prioritize generating Runic Power to keep it active as long as possible.
7.  **Obliterate:** Use as your primary Rune spender (prioritize with Killing Machine).
8.  **Frost Strike:** Use as your Runic Power spender (if not using Breath).

---

## Rotation Priority

### Obliteration Build (Single-Target)
1.  **Obliterate:** If **Killing Machine** is active.
2.  **Frost Strike / Frostbane:** If you have 5 stacks of **Razorice**.
3.  **Howling Blast:** If **Rime** is active.
4.  **Frost Strike:** To prevent capping Runic Power.
5.  **Empower Rune Weapon:** To generate resources and Killing Machine procs.
6.  **Obliterate:** Even without Killing Machine, used as a builder.

### Breath of Sindragosa Build (Single-Target)
1.  **Breath of Sindragosa:** Prioritize maintaining the channel. Cast at ~70+ Runic Power.
2.  **Obliterate:** With **Killing Machine** for high damage and RP generation.
3.  **Howling Blast:** With **Rime** for resources.
4.  **Remorseless Winter:** Keep active on cooldown.
5.  **Obliterate:** Use to generate Runic Power to extend Breath.
6.  **Empower Rune Weapon / Horn of Winter:** Use reactively if Runic Power is dangerously low.

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Pillar of Frost:** 1-minute cooldown. Increases Strength by 25%. Always align with Reaper's Mark and other burst windows. This is the heart of your burst.
*   **Reaper's Mark:** 1-minute cooldown. Heavy-hitting Hero Talent ability. Triggers **Soul Wave** and generates **Killing Machine** procs. Align with Pillar of Frost.
*   **Empower Rune Weapon:** 2-minute cooldown. Grants 15% Haste and generates resources. Recharges faster with current tier sets when casting Pillar of Frost.
*   **Frostwyrm's Fury:** 3-minute cooldown. Massive AoE burst. Cooldown is reduced by **Icecap**. Best used during Pillar of Frost.
*   **Breath of Sindragosa:** 2-minute cooldown. Sustained DPS cooldown that drains Runic Power. Aim for 40-60+ second durations.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Strength**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases all Frost damage (the most valuable secondary for most builds).
3.  **Critical Strike:** Synergizes with Killing Machine.
4.  **Haste:** Faster Rune regeneration and attack speed.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage increase and reduction.

**Note:** Mastery is typically prioritized because almost all Frost DK damage is Frost-based.
