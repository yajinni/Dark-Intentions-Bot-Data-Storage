# Fire Mage Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Fire Mage is a reactive, burst-heavy ranged DPS spec that focuses on critical strikes to trigger instant-cast spells. The core gameplay revolves around the **Hot Streak** mechanic, where two consecutive critical strikes allow you to cast a free, instant **Pyroblast** or **Flamestrike**. The spec is defined by its massive burst window, **Combustion**, during which all spells are guaranteed to crit.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Fire Mage is about chaining critical strikes to maximize the number of instant Pyroblasts.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Heating Up:** One critical strike.
*   **Hot Streak:** Two consecutive critical strikes. Makes your next Pyroblast or Flamestrike instant and free.
*   **Ignite:** A passive burn that deals a percentage of your direct damage over time. Spread via Phoenix Flames or Combustion.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Combustion:** Use on cooldown (ideally when the target is below 90% health).
2.  **Pyroblast:** Use whenever you have a **Hot Streak**.
3.  **Fire Blast:** Use while casting (off the GCD) to convert a **Heating Up** into a **Hot Streak**.
4.  **Phoenix Flames:** Use to generate a **Heating Up** or spread Ignite.
5.  **Fireball / Frostfire Bolt:** Your primary filler to generate procs.
6.  **Scorch:** Use as a filler when the target is < 30% HP or while moving.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Meteor:** Use on cooldown, preferably during Combustion.
2.  **Pyroblast:** Cast with **Hot Streak**.
3.  **Fire Blast:** Use to force a **Hot Streak** from a **Heating Up**.
4.  **Phoenix Flames:** Use if you have charges and no Heating Up.
5.  **Fireball / Frostfire Bolt:** Main filler.
6.  **Scorch:** Execute filler (< 30% HP).

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Combustion:** High priority for massive Ignite spreading.
2.  **Flamestrike:** Replace Pyroblast as your **Hot Streak** spender for 3+ targets.
3.  **Living Bomb:** (If talented) Maintain on targets.
4.  **Dragon's Breath:** Use for burst damage and utility.
5.  **Phoenix Flames:** Crucial for spreading Ignite to nearby enemies.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Combustion:** 2-minute cooldown. Grants 100% crit and increases Mastery. **Usage:** Your primary burst. The goal of the entire spec is to reduce this cooldown (via Kindling) and maximize uptime.
*   **Meteor:** 45-second cooldown. Massive burst damage in a small area. **Usage:** Always use on cooldown, ideally inside a Combustion window.
*   **Phoenix Flames:** 3 charges, 25-second recharge. **Usage:** Use to chain procs and maintain the flow of your rotation.
*   **Time Warp:** 5-minute cooldown. 30% Haste.
*   **Alter Time:** 1-minute cooldown. Returns you to your previous health and location. **Usage:** Powerful defensive and mobility tool.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Haste:** Reduces cast times and increases the speed of your rotation.
3.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of your **Ignite** burn.
4.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.
5.  **Critical Strike:** Valuable for generating procs outside of Combustion, but loses value during Combustion.

**Note:** For Fire, **Haste** and **Mastery** are typically the most important secondaries for maximizing sustained and burst damage respectively.
