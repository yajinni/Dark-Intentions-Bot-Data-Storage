### 7. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary divergence between Ganbrew and the Reference is **GCD uptime and ability priority**. 

1.  **Downtime:** Ganbrew's 15.5% downtime is over double that of the Reference. In a short 2.5-minute encounter, leaving 23 seconds of inactivity severely impacts DPS and resource generation.
2.  **Core Ability Priority:** The Reference cast **Keg Smash** 39 times vs the Player's 32. This is the most critical Brewmaster ability for both damage and Brew cooldown reduction. Furthermore, the Reference cast **Breath of Fire** 39 times (matching Keg Smash) while the Player only managed 28. This suggests the Player is not consistently applying the Breath of Fire dot/debuff following Keg Smashes.
3.  **Cooldown Mismanagement:** Missing a second usage of **Invoke Niuzao** and **Touch of Death** (which the Reference used twice) significantly lowered the Player's overall DPS ceiling. Additionally, the failure to use **Fortifying Brew** indicates a missed opportunity for damage mitigation during high-pressure phases.
4.  **Stat Weighting:** The Player is heavily over-budgeted in Mastery (8.37%) compared to the Reference (3.91%). The Reference favors **Versatility (12.57%)**, which provides more consistent throughput and flat damage reduction, directly contributing to the higher "Per Cast" values observed in the rotational table.
