### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary driver of the performance gap is **Cooldown Neglect**. The Player failed to cast **Invoke Yu'lon, the Jade Serpent** and **Restoral** entirely. In a 2.5-minute encounter, missing two full windows of Yu'lon (the Mistweaver's primary throughput engine) accounts for the majority of the HPS deficit.

Additionally, the Player's reliance on **Soothing Mist** channeling (7 casts vs 0 for the Reference) contributed to lower overall CPM (Casts Per Minute). The Reference utilized a much higher frequency of **Vivify** (33 vs 17) and **Enveloping Mist** (36 vs 22), taking advantage of the additional Renewing Mist spread generated by their higher Haste and cooldown usage. To improve, Fenmonkka must prioritize casting major throughput cooldowns early and often, and focus on filling every available GCD to match the Reference's activity level.
