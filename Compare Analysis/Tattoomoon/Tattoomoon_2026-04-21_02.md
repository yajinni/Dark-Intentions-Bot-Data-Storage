### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The HPS disparity is driven by two main factors: activity levels and rotational synergy.
1. **Downtime:** Tattoomoon's 36% downtime is unacceptably high. The Reference casts almost 1.5 times as many spells by using downtime for minor utility and damage, which keeps procs active.
2. **Efficiency Per Cast:** Tattoomoon’s **Holy Word: Serenity** and **Prayer of Healing** are significantly weaker per cast than the Reference. While the Reference has 9% more Intellect, the Per Cast values are nearly double (117% difference in Serenity). This suggests Tattoomoon is not effectively utilizing the **Divine Image** proc windows or is failing to align throughput with the **Apotheosis** windows as effectively as the Reference.
3. **Defensive Utility:** Tattoomoon completely neglected **Fade**, which in modern Priest builds often provides significant damage reduction. The Reference utilized this 16 times to smooth incoming damage.
4. **Talent/Proc Management:** The massive difference in **Benediction** and **Echo of Light** (Mastery) efficiency indicates the Reference is much more effective at spreading and maintaining heals-over-time through better spell prioritization and position-based cooldown usage (Halo).
