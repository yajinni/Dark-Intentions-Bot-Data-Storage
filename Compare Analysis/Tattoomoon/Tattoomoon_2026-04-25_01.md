### 7. Final Audit Conclusions:
The HPS gap is primarily a product of **extreme downtime** and **poor Holy Word economy**. Tattoomoon spent 60% of the encounter inactive. To improve, the player must focus on the following:
1.  **ABC (Always Be Casting):** Reduce downtime by using filler spells (Smite/Holy Fire) during low-damage windows to reset Holy Word cooldowns.
2.  **Holy Word Prioritization:** Increase the frequency of Holy Word: Serenity and Prayer of Healing. The Reference cast nearly double the amount of high-impact spells.
3.  **Modifier Stacking:** Ensure Holy Words are used to trigger and maintain **Divine Image** uptime, as this accounted for nearly 1M more healing for the Reference.
4.  **Haste Scaling:** The player is currently over-budgeted in Crit/Mastery. Shifting secondary stats toward Haste (aiming for ~18%) will lower the **GCD Duration** and allow for faster response to burst damage on Imperator Averzian.
