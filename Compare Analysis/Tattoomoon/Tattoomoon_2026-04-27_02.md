### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
1. **Mastery Deficiency:** The Player is significantly behind on Mastery (11.96% vs 17.11%). This is the primary reason **Echo of Light** (Holy's most important raid healing mechanic) performed so poorly for Tattoomoon. The Reference's Echo of Light did nearly double the total healing.
2. **Filler Selection:** Tattoomoon relied heavily on **Flash Heal** (24 casts). In a Mythic raid environment, Flash Heal is generally inefficient compared to using **Prayer of Healing** to reduce Holy Word cooldowns via Serendipity. The Reference cast 88 Prayer of Healings vs the Player's 46.
3. **Holy Word Efficiency:** The Reference's **Holy Word: Serenity** had a much higher "Per Cast" value (108K vs 62K). This suggests the Reference is better at timing Holy Words for high-damage windows or utilizing procs/buffs (like Divine Image) to amplify them.
4. **Lack of Offensive Contribution:** The Reference contributed 24 casts of Smite and Holy Fire during downtime. Tattoomoon remained idle during these periods, resulting in 0 damage and lost potential for "surge of light" procs.
