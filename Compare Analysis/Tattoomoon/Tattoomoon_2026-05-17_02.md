### 4. Resource Management (Energy/Combo Points/Mana)
The player demonstrated good mana management but suffered from a lack of "filler" activity. In Archon build, the synergy between [Surge of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=114255) and [Prayer of Healing](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=596) (Spiritwell) is critical. Tattoomoon had 39 Surge of Light applications compared to the reference's 59. This suggests the player is not fishing for procs with Smite or keeping the GCD rolling during low-damage phases, leading to fewer free, high-throughput Prayer of Healing casts. Missing [Holy Word: Serenity](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=2050) casts also indicates missed [Serendipity](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=373456) CDR opportunities.
