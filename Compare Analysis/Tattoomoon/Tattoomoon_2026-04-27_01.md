### 8. Final Audit Conclusions.
The primary driver of the 16% parse is a massive deficiency in **Spell Value (Per Cast)** rather than a lack of activity.
1. **Divine Image Synergy:** The Reference's Divine Image did 3.3x more healing. This suggests the Reference is timing their Holy Words more effectively to trigger and maintain the Image during high-damage windows.
2. **Stat Optimization:** Despite being the same item level, the Player is missing 217 Intellect. This is a critical gap. The missing Wrist and Off-Hand enchants, along with suboptimal trinket selection, are contributing to this.
3. **Utility Gaps:** The Player failed to use **Fade** a single time. In Heroic, using Fade (with the damage reduction talent) is vital for survivability and reducing healer strain.
4. **Haste vs. Mastery:** The Player has high Haste (12.61%) but low throughput. For Holy Priests in raid environments, Crit and Mastery typically provide better raw HPS per point than Haste, which can lead to mana depletion if not carefully managed.
