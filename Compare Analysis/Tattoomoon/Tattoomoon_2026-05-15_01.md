### 4. Resource Management (Energy/Combo Points/Mana)
Tattoomoon failed to utilize [Smite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=585) entirely (0 casts). In a Mythic encounter, [Smite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=585) is not just damage; it is the primary method for proc-fishing for [Surge of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=114255). As an Archon, [Surge of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=114255) makes your [Prayer of Healing](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=596) significantly more powerful. By not casting Smite during low-damage windows, you starved yourself of these free, high-throughput procs and delayed your [Holy Word: Chastise](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=88625) resets via Serendipity.
