### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary divergence in performance stems from **Rotational Priority** and **Buff Alignment**.
1.  **Value per Cast:** The Reference achieved 233.3K per **Prayer of Healing** cast, compared to the Player's 68.1K. This suggests the Player is frequently "over-healing" or casting into low-damage periods, whereas the Reference aligns these casts with the **Divine Hymn** healing increase buff and **Divine Image** procs.
2.  **Holy Word Efficiency:** The Player cast **Holy Word: Serenity** 30 times but only yielded 1.4M healing. The Reference cast it 37 times for 4.2M. This indicates the Player is missing resets (via Flash Heal/Binding Heal) and failing to use Serenity on targets with significant missing health.
3.  **Active Downtime:** The Player's 21.4% downtime is excessive for Heroic Vorasius. The Reference maintains a much higher "Active" time by casting Smite and Holy Fire when healing is not required. For Holy Priests, these offensive casts are vital for maintaining **Divine Image** uptime, which accounts for a 2M healing difference between the two logs.
4.  **Apotheosis Usage:** The Player missed a full usage of Apotheosis. In a 5-minute fight, three uses are expected. Missing a cooldown window significantly reduces the number of Holy Words available for burst healing.
