### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The performance gap between Kwamps and the Reference is primarily driven by **Mastery utilization** and **cleave efficiency**. While the Player actually outperformed the Reference in average damage per cast for Rising Sun Kick and Whirling Dragon Punch, the Reference's massive throughput on **Fists of Fury** (10.2M vs 5.5M) created an insurmountable lead. The Reference successfully channeled FoF into a higher density of targets. Additionally, the Reference utilized **Spinning Crane Kick** as a filler during multi-target phases, whereas the Player completely ignored this ability. Tightening GCD downtime from 15.8% down to the sub-10% range seen in top-tier logs will be the most direct path to increasing overall DPS.
