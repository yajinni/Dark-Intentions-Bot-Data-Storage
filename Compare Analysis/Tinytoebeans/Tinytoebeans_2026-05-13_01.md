### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Tinytoebeans performed significantly below the reference, primarily due to a lack of cast efficiency and a misunderstanding of the Archon hero spec's core synergies. While the player maintained a relatively high uptime on some core buffs, a 37.2% downtime rate (compared to the reference's 30.4%) led to a major throughput deficit.
       - The primary driver for the parse difference is the underutilization of the [Spiritwell](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=453726) mechanic. As an Archon, [Surge of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=114255) procs should be used on [Prayer of Healing](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=596) rather than [Flash Heal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=2061). Tinytoebeans cast very few [Flash Heal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=2061) to trigger [Lightweaver](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=390911) and failed to leverage the increased value of [Prayer of Healing](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=596) during high-damage windows.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Prayer of Healing](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=596):** Casts (63 vs 49) and Per Cast (102.8K vs 215.9K). **Advice:** Your per-cast value is 52.4% lower because you are not consistently using [Surge of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=114255) procs to power this spell via the [Archon](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=453726) spec's [Spiritwell](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=453726) talent. Ensure you prioritize PoH over Flash Heal when [Surge of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=114255) is active during group damage.
       - **[Holy Word: Serenity](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=2050):** Casts (37 vs 43) and Per Cast (123.7K vs 215.2K). **Advice:** The 42.5% value gap is partially due to the reference's significantly higher Crit (27.2%) and Intellect. You also missed roughly 14% of possible casts; use Serenity more aggressively to prevent healing waste on low-priority fillers.
       - **[Halo](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=120517):** Per Cast (608.4K vs 1.53M). **Advice:** Your [Halo](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=120517) is doing 60% less healing per cast. This indicates you are likely casting it outside of high-damage windows or failing to align it with Archon's [Power Surge](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=453112) or [Apotheosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=200183) windows. 
       - **[Flash Heal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=2061):** Casts (6 vs 27). **Advice:** You are severely under-casting [Flash Heal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=2061). Even in Archon builds, [Flash Heal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=2061) is required to trigger [Lightweaver](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=390911) and [Surge of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=114255).
       - **[Benediction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1262763):** Casts (10 vs 18). **Advice:** Lower cast counts suggest you are not utilizing this passive throughput engine enough; ensure [Prayer of Mending](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=33110) is cast strictly on cooldown.
       - **[Holy Nova](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=281265):** Per Cast (8.5K vs 48.8K). **Advice:** Large difference due to the reference utilizing procs or target density more effectively during movement phases.
       - **[Divine Hymn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=64844):** Per Cast (285.6K vs 472.1K). **Advice:** Your Hymn value is 39.5% lower per cast. Ensure you are channeling the full duration during periods of maximum group damage and after [Halo](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=120517) has triggered its healing increase.
       - **[Desperate Prayer](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=19236):** Casts (2 vs 3). **Advice:** You missed a potential usage of your primary personal defensive; align this with heavy mechanics like *Chimaeric Breath*.
