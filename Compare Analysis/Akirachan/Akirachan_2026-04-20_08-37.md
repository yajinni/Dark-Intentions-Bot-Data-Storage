### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary driver of the performance gap is rotational priority and resource management. The Player is "filler-locked," spending far too many GCDs on **Mind Flay** (105 casts) while allowing **Mind Blast** (only 20 casts) to sit unused. In Shadow's current kit, Mind Blast is a critical generator and the primary engine for **Psychic Link** cleave damage.

Secondly, the Player's dot management is inefficient. Hard-casting **Vampiric Touch** 24 times suggests a failure to use automated application tools or a failure to maintain dot pandemic windows, leading to a loss of 22 GCDs that should have been used on damaging abilities. Lastly, the lack of **Shadow Word: Death** usage (only 2 casts vs 11) indicates the Player is missing the execute phase or the "Death and Madness" reset windows entirely. To improve, the Player must prioritize Mind Blast above all else and reduce reliance on channeled fillers.
