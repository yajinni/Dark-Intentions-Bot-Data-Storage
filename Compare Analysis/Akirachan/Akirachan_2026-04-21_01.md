The Reference’s higher downtime suggests more calculated movement or waiting for optimal priority windows rather than filling GCDs with inefficient manual DoT refreshes.

### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The performance gap of 58.5K DPS is primarily attributed to rotational priority and efficiency rather than gear alone. The Player’s reliance on hard-casting *Vampiric Touch* (61 casts) represents a massive loss of high-value GCDs. By comparison, the Reference utilized talents to maintain DoTs, allowing them to cast *Mind Blast* four times as often. Additionally, the Reference maximized their burst windows by using *Power Infusion* and *Voidform* more frequently. To improve, the Player must prioritize *Mind Blast* on cooldown and investigate talent choices that automate DoT application to free up GCDs for the core rotation.
