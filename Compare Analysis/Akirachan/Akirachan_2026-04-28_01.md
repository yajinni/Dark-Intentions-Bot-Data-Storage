### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary cause of the performance gap is **Rotational Priority** and **Action Efficiency**. While the Player possesses higher Haste, they are not converting that speed into meaningful output. 

**Top 3 Action Items:**
1. **Prioritize Mind Blast:** You are missing nearly 65% of your potential Mind Blast casts. This is your primary engine for Insanity.
2. **Stop Manual DoT Refreshing:** Use **Tentacle Slam** to apply and spread DoTs. Manually casting Vampiric Touch 40 times is a massive waste of GCDs that should be spent on Mind Blast or Shadow Word: Madness.
3. **Reduce Downtime:** 32.8% downtime on a fight with 27% Haste is unacceptable. You must always be casting, even if it is a filler Mind Flay, to ensure you are constantly generating resources for your spender.
