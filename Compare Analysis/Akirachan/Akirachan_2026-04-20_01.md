### 7. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary driver of the performance gap is **Rotational Priority Divergence**. 
1. **Filler Management:** The Player is excessively relying on Mind Flay (105 casts). In the Shadow rotation, Mind Flay should only be used when all charges of Mind Blast and Shadow Word: Madness are exhausted. The Reference cast Mind Blast **175% more often** than the Player.
2. **Resource & Talent Utilization:** The Player cast Vampiric Touch manually 24 times, whereas the Reference only cast it twice. This suggests the Reference is effectively using **Shadow Crash** or **Void Bolt** refreshes to maintain DoTs, freeing up 22 GCDs for higher-impact damage spells.
3. **Cooldown Alignment:** The Player missed 50% of potential **Power Infusion** windows (3 vs 6). PI should be used on cooldown or strictly aligned with Voidform to maximize the Haste scaling of Shadow's periodic effects.
4. **Execution Phase:** The Player only used **Shadow Word: Death** 2 times. Even outside of execute range, SW:D is a critical movement tool and resource generator that was largely ignored.
