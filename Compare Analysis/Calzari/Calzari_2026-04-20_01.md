### 5. Final Audit Conclusions.
The primary divergence in performance stems from **Temporal Anomaly** and **Echo** utilization. While Calzari casts **Echo** more frequently (80 vs 59), their value per cast is 54% lower than the Reference. This indicates that the Player is generating Echoes but failing to consume them with high-impact empowered spells like **Dream Breath** or **Spiritbloom**. 

Furthermore, the Player completely neglected **Emerald Blossom** and **Rewind**, two cornerstone abilities for Preservation throughput and raid recovery. The Reference used **Emerald Blossom** 30 times, contributing 4.0M healing that is entirely absent from the Player's profile. Calzari is also over-relying on **Chrono Flames** as a filler, casting it twice as often as the Reference for significantly lower value. 

To improve, Calzari must:
1.  **Increase Temporal Anomaly frequency:** Match the Reference’s usage (approx. every 11 seconds) to maximize shielding and Echo distribution.
2.  **Optimize Echo consumption:** Ensure Echoes are active before casting major empowered spells to replicate high-value healing across the group.
3.  **Active Resource Spending:** Incorporate **Emerald Blossom** into the rotation to utilize Essence effectively during periods of group damage.
4.  **Reduce Downtime:** Despite having more Haste, the Player is casting less. Focus on maintaining a higher "Active %" by better utilizing **Hover** for movement-heavy phases of the Imperator Averzian encounter.
