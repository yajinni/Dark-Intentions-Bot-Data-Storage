### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary driver of the 70K DPS disparity is **Shatter efficiency** and **Frozen Orb utilization**. 
1. **Shatter & Procs:** The Reference triggered 265 Shatter hits compared to the Player's 117. This suggests the Reference is much more effective at applying Winter's Chill (via Flurry) and consuming those stacks with heavy hitters. The Player's higher average Glacial Spike damage indicates they are Shattering that specific spell correctly, but failing to Shatter the rest of the kit.
2. **Frozen Orb & Blizzard:** The Reference achieved 433 hits with Frozen Orb versus the Player's 135, despite casting it the same number of times. This points to the Reference timing Orbs for add spawns. Furthermore, the Reference used **Blizzard** to reduce the cooldown of Frozen Orb and provide additional Shatter opportunities; the Player ignored Blizzard entirely.
3. **Stat Weighting:** The Player is significantly over-capped on Crit for a Shatter-based build while lacking the Haste (11.43%) necessary to smooth out the rotation and maximize the number of Glacial Spikes and Frostbolts cast per encounter.
4. **Active Downtime:** The Player's downtime (9.1%) is nearly triple that of the Reference. Every second not casting as a Frost Mage results in lost procs and lost CDR, compounding the damage loss across the fight.
