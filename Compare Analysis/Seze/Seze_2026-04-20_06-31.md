### 5. Final Audit Conclusions
The Player's 44% parse is primarily attributed to **rotational passivity** and **resource mismanagement**. 

1.  **Execute Neglect:** The Reference manually cast Execute 92 times, while Seze recorded 0 manual casts. In the Slayer hero talent tree, Execute is vital for triggering and extending core damage buffs. By ignoring manual Executes, Seze significantly reduced their overall pressure and failed to capitalize on the spec's execute-range scaling.
2.  **GCD Efficiency:** Seze experienced nearly 25% downtime. Even accounting for lower Haste, the Player is consistently missing GCD windows. For Fury, staying GCD-locked is mandatory for Rage generation and Rampage frequency.
3.  **Ability Priority:** Seze over-utilized standard Raging Blow (49 casts) while failing to prioritize Crushing Blow procs (only 3 casts vs Reference's 11). This suggests the Player is not reacting to ability enhancements provided by their talents.
4.  **Cleave Mechanics:** Despite having identical Whirlwind cast counts (25), The Reference achieved 402 hits compared to Seze's 254. This indicates The Reference is aligning Whirlwind casts more effectively with add spawns to maximize the "Meat Cleaver" buff efficiency.

**Action Plan:** Prioritize manual Execute usage during procs and execute phases. Focus on consuming Crushing Blow charges immediately over standard Raging Blow. Reduce idle time between casts to match the 85%+ GCD utilization seen in the Reference.
