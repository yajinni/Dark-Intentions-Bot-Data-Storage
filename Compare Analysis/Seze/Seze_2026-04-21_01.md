### 7. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary performance gap is driven by **Stat Optimization** and **Rotational Priority**.
1. **Mastery Deficiency:** The Reference holds a massive +6.39% Mastery lead. Since Fury damage is amplified significantly during Enrage, this results in the Reference's abilities (Rampage, Bloodbath, Bladestorm) hitting much harder per cast.
2. **Resource Management:** The Player is manually casting **Rend**, which is an inefficient use of GCDs in the current Heroic meta. Removing this in favor of faster **Rampage** generation is critical.
3. **Enrage Uptime:** The Player's 78% Enrage uptime is significantly lower than the Reference's 94%. This is the result of missing **Rampage** and **Bloodbath** casts. 
4. **Cooldown Alignment:** The Reference’s **Bladestorm** average damage is nearly double the Player's. This confirms the Reference is strictly aligning Bladestorm with **Recklessness** and ensuring Enrage is active before the channel starts.
5. **Downtime:** The Player must reduce the 48s of inactivity. Improving GCD "spam" and reducing reaction lag between casts will naturally increase DPS.
