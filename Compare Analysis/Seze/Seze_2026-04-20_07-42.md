### 5. Final Audit Conclusions.
The primary performance gap between Seze and the Reference stems from **Rotational Priority** and **Stat Optimization**. 

1.  **Rend Usage:** Seze spent 20 GCDs on Rend, while the Reference avoided this ability entirely. For Fury Warriors in this encounter, Rend is a significant DPS loss compared to generating or spending Rage.
2.  **Rampage Frequency:** The Reference managed 135 Rampage casts compared to Seze's 119. This is the core of Fury's damage and the primary driver of Enrage uptime.
3.  **Average Hit Value:** There is a massive discrepancy in the average damage of **Bloodbath** (66.6K vs 104.8K) and **Odyn's Fury** (83.3K vs 114.6K). This suggests the Reference is much more effective at stacking multipliers—specifically ensuring these high-value hits land within Enrage windows and cooldown peaks.
4.  **Mastery Gap:** The Reference has 25.24% Mastery compared to the Player's 18.85%. Since Mastery directly increases damage dealt while Enraged, this accounts for a large portion of the throughput difference. Seze should prioritize Mastery/Haste gear over Versatility.
