### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
- **Execute Phase Neglect:** The most glaring divergence is in **Execute** performance. The Reference generated 228 hits compared to the Player's 106. This indicates the Player is either failing to prioritize Execute during the sub-35% health window or is not reacting to Sudden Death procs efficiently.
- **Cleave Inefficiency:** Both players cast **Whirlwind** 25 times; however, the Reference achieved 402 hits while the Player only achieved 254. This suggests the Player is using Whirlwind when secondary targets are out of range or during periods where the cleave buff is not required, leading to wasted globals.
- **Resource Management:** The Player's lower **Rampage** count (119 vs 131) suggests poor Rage generation. This is tied directly to the 74.2s of downtime where no builders (Bloodthirst/Raging Blow) were being cast.
- **Odyn's Fury Alignment:** The Player missed 6 potential casts of **Odyn's Fury**. On an 8:15 fight, this suggests the ability was held significantly longer than necessary, missing out on massive burst damage and bleed application.
- **Proc Priority:** The Player over-utilized **Bloodbath** relative to the Reference (58 vs 45) but significantly under-utilized **Crushing Blow** (3 vs 11). This indicates a misunderstanding of the priority between Raging Blow procs and Bloodthirst procs within the current Fury rotation.
