### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:

#### Closing the Gap:
*   **[Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455):** Increase usage from 0 to at least 7 casts. This is your primary spender for [Voidfall](https://www.wowhead.com/search?q=Voidfall) stacks and represents a massive missing damage source.
*   **[Eradicate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1226033):** Total damage was 38% lower than the reference. Improve Mastery alignment during proc windows to maximize hit value.
*   **[Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1221150):** Increase casts by 15.7%. Prioritize casting immediately upon reaching 30 Souls to pause Fury drain and maximize Metamorphosis duration.
*   **[Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=473728):** Total contribution was 22% lower. Ensure you are using this at 100+ Fury outside of transformation to bank Souls effectively.
*   **[Devour](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1217610):** Increase cast count by 13% to match the Reference's filler density.

#### Top 3 Overall Priorities:
1.  **Reduce Downtime:** You are currently losing 26% of the fight to inactivity. Minimize movement gaps and maintain the GCD loop to prevent falling behind in Fury generation.
2.  **Spend Voidfall Stacks:** Incorporate [Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455) into your rotation. Failing to use this spender is the single largest technical error in this log.
3.  **Stat Optimization:** Swap Haste enchants and gems for **Mastery** ([Flawless Masterful Garnet](https://www.wowhead.com/search?q=Flawless+Masterful+Garnet)). As a Void-Scarred build, your burst potential scales directly with Mastery, and your current Haste-leaning gear is diluting your [Voidsurge](https://www.wowhead.com/search?q=Voidsurge) efficiency.
