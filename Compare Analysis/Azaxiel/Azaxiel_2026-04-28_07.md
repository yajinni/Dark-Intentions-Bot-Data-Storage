### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
Azaxiel's performance is hampered by three primary factors: **Downtime**, **Haste deficit**, and **Gear preparation**. With 26% downtime, the player is spending more than a quarter of the fight inactive, leading to a substantial loss in **Devour** and **Void Ray** casts. While the Player has a higher Intellect pool, the lack of Haste (17% vs 24%) creates a slower rotational flow that the Reference exploits. Furthermore, the omission of gems in four available sockets and a missing ring enchant represents a significant unforced error in character optimization. Improving GCD uptime and correcting the gear gaps are the most immediate paths to performance parity.
