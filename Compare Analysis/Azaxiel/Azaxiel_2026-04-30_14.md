### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:

#### Closing the Gap:
*   **[Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455):** Increase casts from 0 to 7. This is a 1.2M damage loss (approx 4% of total) and must be integrated into the rotation immediately.
*   **[Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1221150):** Increase casts by 19% (from 16 to 19). Ensure this is used on cooldown to prevent drifting.
*   **[Devour](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1217610):** Increase casts by 15% to match reference uptime. Focus on generating enough Fury to never delay this spender.
*   **[Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=473728):** Increase casts by 5% and focus on full channel completion. Downtime and movement are clipping your rays.
*   **[Eradicate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1226033):** While proc-based, the 39% total damage gap suggests you are not prioritizing spending your Fury on [Chaos Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=165410) during the [Void-Scarred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=451122) windows.

#### Other Priorities:
*   **Haste Threshold:** You are significantly behind in Haste (17% vs 25%). This is causing your [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=473728) to lose ticks. Adjust enchants on rings and your weapon to help bridge this gap.
*   **Downtime Mitigation:** 26% downtime is unacceptably high for Mythic. Use [Glide](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=131347) and [Fel Rush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=195072) more aggressively to minimize time spent out of range.
*   **Gearing:** Apply the missing Ring enchant and socket your Ring 1. Use a [Radiant Jewelbinder](https://www.wowhead.com/item=263897) on your Head and Waist to match the Reference's socket count.
