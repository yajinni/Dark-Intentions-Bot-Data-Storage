### 8. Final Audit Conclusions.

#### **Closing the Gap**
- **[Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455):** You cast this **0 times** while the reference cast it 7 times. This is a mandatory spender for *Voidfall* stacks and accounted for 1.2M damage you missed entirely.
- **[Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1221150):** Increase casts by **18%**. You missed 3 casts. You must use this immediately at 30 Souls to pause the Fury drain and extend your transformation.
- **[Devour](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1217610):** Increase cast count by **15%**. This is your primary filler; the gap is due to high downtime and low Haste.
- **[Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=473728):** You are missing **2 casts** and significant per-cast damage. Focus on maintaining high Fury (100+) before casting to maximize Soul loading.
- **[Eradicate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1226033):** Total damage was **63% lower** than the reference. This dot scales with your uptime and buff windows; focus on staying in range.

#### **Other Priorities**
- **Downtime Management:** Your 26% downtime is the single largest technical barrier to your performance. Focus on positioning to maintain uptime during mechanic transitions.
- **Stat Conflict:** You are currently mixing a Mastery-based Flask with Haste-based Gems. Since you are playing a Mastery-heavy profile (Void-Scarred/Flask of the Magisters), you should swap your gems to [Flawless Masterful Garnet](https://www.wowhead.com/item=213454) and ensure your Ring 1 is enchanted.
- **Sockets:** Apply [Radiant Jewelbinder](https://www.wowhead.com/item=263897/radiant-jewelbinder) to your Neck and Waist to add the missing sockets used by the reference.
