### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
The 35K DPS gap between Azaxiel and Metabatt is primarily attributed to **Gear Preparation** and **Rotational Density**. 
1. **Socket & Enchant Gap:** The Player is missing 3 sockets compared to the Reference and is missing an enchant on Ring 1. Additionally, the Player is using Haste-based ring enchants while their stat profile suggests they are playing the Mastery-heavy **Void-Scarred** build. This creates a stat conflict.
2. **Activity Deficit:** The Player has 26% downtime, which is significantly higher than the Reference's 21.9%. This translated into 30 fewer GCDs cast over the 6-minute fight. 
3. **Mechanical Errors:** The Player completely missed the usage of **Cull**, which is a high-value spender. Furthermore, the Reference achieved a 50% crit rate on **Collapsing Star**, indicating better timing during peak proc/buff windows, whereas the Player's crit rate was only 25%.
4. **Weapon Enchants:** The MH/OH enchants are reversed. While both are present, Arcane Mastery should ideally be on the Main Hand for optimal proc tracking.
