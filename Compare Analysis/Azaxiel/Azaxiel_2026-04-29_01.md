### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
The 34.8K DPS gap is primarily driven by **Rotational Downtime (26.0%)** and **Stat Misalignment**.
1.  **Downtime:** Azaxiel is losing nearly a quarter of the fight to inactivity. This is reflected in the significantly lower cast counts for **Devour** and **Void Ray**.
2.  **Hero Spec Logic:** While playing the **Annihilator** build (evidenced by Collapsing Star usage), the Player has geared heavily into **Mastery (28.15%)** instead of the recommended **Haste** priority. This increases the GCD and reduces the frequency of Soul generation.
3.  **Refinement:** The missing **Cull** cast represents a loss of high-impact burst damage. Additionally, five missed **Void Ray** casts shortened the duration of the Player's Metamorphosis windows, as Void Ray is vital for pausing the Fury drain.
4.  **Gear Prep:** Missing four gem sockets and a ring enchant is a significant loss of raw Haste/Mastery. Aligning enchants with the **Annihilator** profile (Haste) is recommended.
