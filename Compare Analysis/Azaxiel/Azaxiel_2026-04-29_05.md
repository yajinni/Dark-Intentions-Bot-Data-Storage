### 8. Final Audit Conclusions.
Azaxiel's performance was significantly hampered by a "Socket Gap" and rotational inactivity. The Reference possessed 5 total sockets compared to the Player's 2, resulting in a substantial loss of secondary stats (specifically Haste).

**Key Takeaways:**
1. **Downtime:** 26.0% downtime on a 6-minute Mythic encounter is too high. The Player must focus on maintaining GCD activity, particularly using **Devour** as a filler to prevent Soul/Fury overflow.
2. **Haste Deficiency:** The 6.8% Haste gap allowed the Reference to achieve higher Metamorphosis uptime (45.5% vs 38.2%). This is critical for the Annihilator build, which relies on high uptime for sustained DPS.
3. **Gear Preparation:** Missing an enchant on Ring 1 and three empty sockets (Head, Ring 1, Wrist) represent a significant loss in throughput.
4. **Rotational Priority:** Ensure **Collapsing Star** is used immediately at 30 Souls to pause Fury drain and maximize Metamorphosis duration. The Player's lower cast count suggests Souls were either overcapped or not generated efficiently.
