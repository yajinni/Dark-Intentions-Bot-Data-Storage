### 8. Final Audit Conclusions.
1.  **Integrate Cull into Rotation:** The single largest rotational error is the 0% usage of [Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455). You must use this as your primary spender for *Voidfall* stacks.
2.  **Reduce Downtime:** Your 26% downtime is nearly 7% higher than the reference. Focus on maintaining uptime during mechanics; as a Devourer DH, being outside of melee range is less punishing, but you must keep the GCD rolling.
3.  **Haste Scaling:** You are currently playing the **Annihilator** build (based on *Collapsing Star* usage) but have a Mastery-heavy gear profile. For Annihilator, Haste is your most valuable secondary stat. You need to target ~25% Haste to match the reference's resource generation and cast frequency.

**Closing the Gap:** To match Shaksó, you must fix the [Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455) oversight immediately. This alone represents a multi-million damage loss. Secondly, increase your [Devour](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1217610) frequency by staying active and increasing your Haste through gems and Ring enchants.
