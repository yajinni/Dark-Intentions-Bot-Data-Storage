### 8. Final Audit Conclusions
1.  **Integrate Cull Immediately:** The most glaring rotational error is the 0% usage of **Cull**. This spell is essential for spending Voidfall stacks. Failing to use it represents a significant loss in both burst and sustained DPS.
2.  **Stat Re-Alignment:** You are currently itemizing for **Void-Scarred** (Mastery) but playing an **Annihilator** rotation (using Collapsing Star frequently). To close the gap, you must either pivot fully into Haste gems/enchants to support the Annihilator "drain-pause" style or refine the Mastery-based burst rotation.
3.  **Optimize Casting Volume:** You have 26% downtime. To reach the Reference’s 122K DPS, you must reduce this to under 20% by pre-positioning and ensuring every GCD is spent on **Devour** filler when higher-priority spenders like **Collapsing Star** (at 30+ Souls) are not available.
4.  **Gear Completion:** You are missing several sockets and have suboptimal enchants on your Rings and Head. Addressing the "Socket Gap" (2 vs 4) and applying the correct Mastery enchants will provide a measurable increase in baseline throughput.
