### 8. Final Audit Conclusions.

#### **Closing the Gap**
- **[Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455):** You missed 100% of potential Cull casts. This is a mandatory rotational spender for the Void-Scarred hero spec; ensure it is bound and used on cooldown.
- **[Eradicate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1226033):** Your damage contribution from this proc was 38% lower than the reference. Focus on ensuring high-damage spenders are pooled for Eradicate windows.
- **[Devour](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1217610):** Increase casts by 15.6%. This requires better Fury management and ensuring you are not over-capping resources before casting Eye Beam.
- **[Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1221150):** Increase usage by 18% to match the reference's 19 casts.

#### **Other Priorities**
- **Haste Profile:** Your Haste (17.8%) is significantly lower than the reference (25.3%). While Mastery is your primary stat, the "feel" and GCD efficiency of the spec suffer below 20%. Consider swapping a few enchants or gems to reach a more comfortable Haste floor.
- **Downtime:** You spent 26% of the fight inactive. This is nearly 1.5 minutes of lost damage. Even if moving for mechanics, ensure you are utilizing [Throw Glaive](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=185123) or positioning yourself to maintain [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=473728) uptime.
- **Missing Enchant/Socket:** You are missing an enchant on Ring 1 and a secondary socket on your neck. These are small but cumulative throughput losses.
