## 7. Actionable Advice
*   **Correct Weapon Enchants:** Swap your weapon enchants immediately. Per the SPEC KNOWLEDGE BASE, **Arcane Mastery** must be on the Main Hand for Mastery scaling, and **Berserker's Rage** on the Off-Hand for Critical Strike.
*   **Potion Usage:** You missed two windows of **Potion of Recklessness**. These should be synced with Metamorphosis and Heroism to bridge the 37K DPS gap.
*   **Gem Sockets:** You have empty sockets in your Waist and Wrist slots. Fill these with **Flawless Quick Amethyst** to reach the necessary Haste thresholds for Void Ray.
*   **Void-Scarred Maintenance:** Ensure you are not casting Eye Beam or Chaos Strike if it will cause **Void Ray** to fall off. The reference had much higher Voidfall uptime, suggesting you are letting the channel drop.
