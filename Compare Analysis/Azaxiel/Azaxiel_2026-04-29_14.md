- **[Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1221150):** Increase casts by 18.7% (approx. 3 more casts). This is a high-priority spell that should not be delayed.

**Other Priorities:**

- **Stat Optimization:** Your Haste (17.8%) is significantly lower than the Reference (25.3%). Haste is critical for the Void-Scarred build to reduce GCDs and increase the number of [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=473728) ticks. Swap Mastery/Intellect focus for Haste where possible.
- **Gear Preparation:** Add a socket to your Head slot using [Radiant Jewelbinder](https://www.wowhead.com/item=263897) and ensure [Ring 1](https://www.wowhead.com/item=249369) is enchanted and gemmed.
