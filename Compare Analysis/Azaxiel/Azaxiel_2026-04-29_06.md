### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary gap between Azaxiel and the Reference is a combination of **Haste scaling** and **Mechanical Uptime**. 
1. **Socket Gap:** The Reference has 3 additional sockets compared to the Player. This accounts for a significant chunk of missing secondary stats.
2. **Rotational Efficiency:** The Player missed 20 casts of *Devour* and failed to cast *Cull* entirely. This suggests the Player is not maximizing the Metamorphosis window or is losing GCDs to movement.
3. **Stat Misalignment:** While the Player has high Mastery, the Haste gap (7%) is causing a "GCD deficit," where the Reference is simply able to cast more spells in the same time frame.
4. **Gear Optimization:** Weapon enchants are swapped, and head/shoulder enchants do not match the recommended utility/survival profile. Correcting the Ring 1 enchant and adding missing sockets should be the priority.
