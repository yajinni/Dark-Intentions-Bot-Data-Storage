### 8. Final Audit Conclusions.

**Closing the Gap:**
- **[Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455):** You missed 7 casts (100% difference). This ability must be integrated into your rotation as a priority spender.
- **[Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1221150):** Increase casts by 15.7% to match reference uptime; ensure this is used on cooldown to maximize its 567k+ per-cast potential.
- **[Devour](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1217610):** Increase casts by 13.5%. You are losing uptime during movement phases; focus on maintaining your melee distance or using [Glide](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=131347) more effectively to stick to Vorasius.
- **[Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=473728):** Focus on 100% uptime. Your "Drain Pausing" needs improvement; do not let the ray expire to finish a lower-priority cast.

**Other Priorities:**
- **Downtime:** Your 26% downtime is the single largest factor in your DPS loss. You are spending 93 seconds not casting. Work on your "Always Be Casting" (ABC) discipline.
- **Haste Threshold:** You are significantly behind the reference in Haste (17% vs 25%). This is making your rotation significantly less efficient. Prioritize Haste/Mastery gear over pure Mastery pieces to reach the next GCD break-point.
- **Gear Optimization:** Fix the weapon enchant swap (Mastery on Main, Berserker on Off) and fill your empty jewelry sockets to bridge the stat gap.
