### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary performance gap is driven by **Rotational Downtime** and a significant **Socket Gap**. While Azaxiel has higher raw Intellect, the Reference's 5 sockets (compared to Azaxiel's 2) and 7% higher Haste allow for a much more fluid rotation. 

**Key Action Items:**
1. **Socket Up:** The Reference has 3 more sockets than you. Acquiring and filling sockets in your Wrist, Waist, and Neck is a priority.
2. **Haste Optimization:** Your Haste (17.82%) is low for the Annihilator playstyle. Aim for closer to 24% to ensure you can maximize "drain-pausing" via *Void Ray* and *Collapsing Star*.
3. **Minimize Downtime:** 26% downtime is excessive for Mythic Vorasius. Ensure you are pre-positioning to maintain uptime during movement mechanics.
4. **Spell Selection:** You missed 100% of your **Cull** casts. This is a vital spender that must be integrated into your priority list when Voidfall stacks are present.
5. **Unique Gem:** You are missing the **Indecipherable Eversong Diamond**. This is a mandatory power increase for the Devourer spec.
