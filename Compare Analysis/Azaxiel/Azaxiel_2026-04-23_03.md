## 7. Final Audit Conclusions:
The performance gap between Azaxiel and the Reference is primarily driven by three factors: rotational priority, uptime efficiency, and stat optimization.

1.  **Priority Divergence:** The Player is significantly under-utilizing their primary spenders. The Reference cast `Devour` 52 times compared to the Player's 37, despite a shorter fight. Furthermore, the Player missed at least 3 high-value casts of `Collapsing Star`. The "Per Cast" value of `Collapsing Star` for the Reference is nearly double that of the Player (723.3K vs 370.8K), indicating the Reference is aligning this ability with high-stack counts or major cooldowns that the Player is ignoring.
2.  **Mechanical Downtime:** 15.2% downtime on a Heroic encounter is unacceptably high for a DPS role. The Player is losing over 18 seconds of active combat time, which directly correlates to the lower cast counts of `Void Ray` and `Devour`.
3.  **Stat Misallocation:** The Player has prioritized Mastery (58.37%) over Haste (18.41%). While Mastery increases damage per hit, the Reference's Haste-heavy build (25.09%) facilitates a much faster GCD, allowing for more frequent resource spending and higher overall throughput.
4.  **Consumables:** The Player failed to use a `Potion of Recklessness`, which provided a significant opening burst window for the Reference.

**Recommended Action:** Shift gearing toward Haste to lower the GCD, focus on maximizing `Devour` frequency, and ensure `Collapsing Star` is only used during optimal damage windows to increase "Per Cast" efficiency. Reduce downtime by pre-positioning for mechanics to maintain high `Void Ray` uptime.
