### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
The performance gap between Azaxiel and the Reference is primarily rooted in **Cast Frequency** and **Downtime**. Azaxiel had 27.1% downtime, meaning for over 100 seconds of the fight, no meaningful actions were taken. 

1.  **Collapsing Star Optimization:** This is the highest priority spell. The Player missed 8 full casts compared to the Reference. Maximizing the usage of this ability is the fastest way to increase DPS.
2.  **Channeling Efficiency:** While Void Ray cast counts were similar, the Reference landed 237 more hits. This suggests the Player is clipping channels early or moving unnecessarily during the cast.
3.  **Secondary Stat Balance:** The Player is significantly behind in Haste (-2.62%). This contributes to a slower rotation and lower APM. Enchanting Ring 1 and socketing the Waist/Wrist with Haste gems should be a priority.
4.  **Gear Preparation:** Ensure Ring 1 is enchanted and all available sockets (Waist/Wrist) are filled to bridge the stat gap.
