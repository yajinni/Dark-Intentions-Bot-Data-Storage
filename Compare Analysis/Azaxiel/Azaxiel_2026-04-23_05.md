### 5. Final Audit Conclusions.
The 68.6K DPS disparity is driven by three primary technical failures:
1.  **Collapsing Star Execution:** The Reference deals nearly double the damage per cast of Collapsing Star (723.3K vs 370.8K) and maintains higher frequency. This suggests the Player is failing to pool resources or align buffs (like Potion of Recklessness) before entering the star window.
2.  **Downtime and Haste Scaling:** The Player's 15.2% downtime is unacceptable for a high-parse attempt. When combined with the Haste deficit, the Player loses roughly 1.5 casts per 10 seconds of combat relative to the Reference.
3.  **Rotational Priority:** The Player under-utilizes **Devour** (37 vs 52 casts) and completely omitted **Cull**. Conversely, the Player appears to be over-relying on **Consume**, using it more frequently than the Reference despite lower overall throughput. Focus must shift toward maximizing Devour counts and tightening the Collapsing Star priority.
