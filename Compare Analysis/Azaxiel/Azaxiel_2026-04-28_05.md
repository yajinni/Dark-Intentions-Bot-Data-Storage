### 8. Final Audit Conclusions
The Player's 22% parse is the result of significant rotational drift and poor uptime. Despite having a 7.7% primary stat advantage, the Player fell 58.8K DPS behind the Reference. 

**Key Takeaways:**
1.  **Uptime is Critical:** 102.8s of downtime is unacceptable for Mythic progression. You must minimize movement and maintain a higher "Casts Per Minute" (CPM).
2.  **Collapsing Star Priority:** You are missing roughly 33% of your potential Collapsing Star windows. This is your most important damage contributor.
3.  **Resource Management:** The deficit in **Devour** casts suggests you are not generating or spending resources efficiently, leading to empty GCDs.
4.  **Gear Preparation:** Ensure all sockets (Head, Ring 1, Waist, Wrist) are filled and Ring 1 is enchanted. Missing these optimizations results in a measurable loss of throughput.
