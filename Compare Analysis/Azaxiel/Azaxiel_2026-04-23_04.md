### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary performance gap is driven by **Rotational Density** and **Cooldown Alignment**. 

1.  **Downtime:** Azaxiel suffered from 15.2% downtime. On a short 2:28 fight, losing 18.3 seconds of active casting significantly reduces the generation of resources and overall pressure.
2.  **Resource Spending (Devour):** The Reference managed 52 casts of Devour compared to the Player's 37. This 40% difference in cast volume suggests the Reference is generating resources more efficiently and prioritizing this spender.
3.  **Collapsing Star Optimization:** Despite the Player having higher Mastery (58% vs 27%), the Reference’s **Collapsing Star** dealt nearly double the damage per cast (723.3K vs 370.8K). This indicates the Reference is successfully lining up this high-impact spell with temporary buffs or the Potion of Recklessness, whereas the Player is casting it "naked" or outside of optimal windows.
4.  **Stat Prioritization:** The Reference has prioritized Haste over Mastery. For this spec, the increased cast frequency and faster GCD floor from Haste appear to provide a higher ceiling than the raw damage-per-hit scaling of Mastery.
