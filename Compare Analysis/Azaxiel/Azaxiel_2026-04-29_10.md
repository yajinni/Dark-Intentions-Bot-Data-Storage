### 8. Final Audit Conclusions
1.  **Eliminate the "Cull" Gap:** You missed 1.2M damage by failing to cast [Cull](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245455). This is your primary spender for *Voidfall* stacks. You must integrate this into your rotation immediately.
2.  **Fix High Downtime:** You spent 26% of the fight (93 seconds) not casting. On a high-intensity Mythic encounter like Vorasius, this is the single largest contributor to your 30% DPS gap. Focus on staying in range and maintaining GCD uptime.
3.  **Haste Optimization for Annihilator:** You are playing the **Annihilator** build but your Haste (17.8%) is far too low compared to the Reference (25.3%). This prevents you from effectively "pausing" the Fury drain during Metamorphosis. Prioritize Haste on your Ring enchants and gems to reach the ~25% threshold.
