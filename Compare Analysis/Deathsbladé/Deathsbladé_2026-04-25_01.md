### 7. Final Audit Conclusions.
The primary performance gap between Deathsbladé and the Reference is **mechanical throughput** and **defensive neglect**. 

1.  **Rotational CPM Gap:** The Player is missing 43 casts over a 2.5-minute window. This indicates significant hesitation in the rotation. Heart Strike and Blood Boil frequency must increase to generate the Runic Power necessary for more Death Strikes.
2.  **Stat Misallocation:** The Player has a high investment in Versatility (6.22%), whereas the Reference prioritizes Mastery (19.30%). In the San'layn/Deathbringer meta, Mastery is vital for **Blood Shield** scaling and physical damage reduction. The Reference's higher "Per Cast" values are a direct result of this Mastery gap.
3.  **Defensive Vacuum:** The Player utilized zero major defensives (Vampiric Blood, Icebound Fortitude, Lichborne) outside of a single Anti-Magic Shell. A Blood DK's survival and damage are linked to the HP increases from Vampiric Blood. 
4.  **Utility Divergence:** The Reference utilized **Abomination Limb** 5 times for grouping and Runic Power generation, while the Player did not use it at all. This is a significant loss of "free" resources and snap-aggro potential.
5.  **Marrowrend Efficiency:** The Player used 10 Marrowrends compared to the Reference's 15. Maintaining higher **Bone Shield** stacks is essential for Haste (via talents) and armor, which directly impacts rotational fluidness.
