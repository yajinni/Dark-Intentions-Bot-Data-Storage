### 5. Final Audit Conclusions
Deathsbladé's performance is hampered by three primary factors: 
1. **Low Action Priority:** Despite the encounter being active, the Player cast 35% fewer spells than the Reference. This results in significantly lower Runic Power generation, evidenced by having 10 fewer **Death Strike** casts.
2. **Defensive Neglect:** As a Blood Death Knight, the Player failed to use **Vampiric Blood**, **Icebound Fortitude**, and **Lichborne** entirely. These are not just survival tools; they are rotational requirements for managing health spikes and increasing healing intake.
3. **Resource Management:** The lower **Marrowrend** count suggest potential Bone Shield expiration or suboptimal stack maintenance. Furthermore, the Player missed utility and throughput cooldowns like **Abomination Limb**, which provide substantial passive damage and resource generation. 

**Priority Improvement:** Increase APM to match the GCD, use Vampiric Blood on cooldown or during high damage phases, and ensure Bone Shield never drops below 5 stacks.
