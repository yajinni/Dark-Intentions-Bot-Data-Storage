### 7. Final Audit Conclusions
The Player's performance is primarily hindered by low rotational activity and poor resource management.

1.  **Runic Power Generation Gap**: The most significant divergence is found in **Heart Strike** usage (49 vs 86). By failing to use Heart Strike as a primary filler, the Player generated significantly less Runic Power, leading to 26 fewer **Death Strike** casts. This directly impacts both DPS and survivability (Blood Shield uptime).
2.  **Bone Shield Inefficiency**: The Player cast **Marrowrend** 32 times compared to the Reference's 25. Over-casting Marrowrend wastes Runes that should be spent on Heart Strike for Runic Power generation. The Player is likely refreshing Bone Shield stacks too early.
3.  **Downtime**: 66.5 seconds of downtime is excessive for a Mythic encounter. The Player is not maintaining the "Always Be Casting" (ABC) principle, missing roughly 1 out of every 6 potential GCDs.
4.  **Cooldown Underutilization**: **Vampiric Blood** usage was 50% lower than the Reference. As a Blood DK, this is a core rotational defensive that should be used proactively to increase healing received and maximum health.
5.  **Mastery Utilization**: While the Player has significantly higher Mastery (14.74% vs 8.48%), the lower frequency of Death Strike means the Player is not capitalizing on the larger **Blood Shields** their stat profile allows for.
