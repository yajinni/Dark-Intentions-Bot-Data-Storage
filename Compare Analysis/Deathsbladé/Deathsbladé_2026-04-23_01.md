### 7. Final Audit Conclusions:
Deathsbladé’s performance is hindered by two critical factors: extreme rotational downtime and a total absence of defensive cooldown management.
1.  **Downtime:** The 41.2s of idle time resulted in 10 fewer Death Strikes and significantly fewer Heart Strikes. This directly leads to lower Blood Shield uptime and lower damage.
2.  **Resource Management:** Marrowrend usage (10 vs 15) suggests the Player may be struggling to maintain **Bone Shield** stacks, leading to lower Armor and Runic Power generation.
3.  **Defensives:** A Blood DK should utilize **Vampiric Blood** proactively. Zero casts in a Heroic encounter indicates a reactive rather than proactive playstyle.
4.  **Stat Gap:** The Reference has 4.56% more Mastery. Since Mastery directly increases the size of **Blood Shield** from Death Strike, the Reference is significantly tankier per point of Runic Power spent.
