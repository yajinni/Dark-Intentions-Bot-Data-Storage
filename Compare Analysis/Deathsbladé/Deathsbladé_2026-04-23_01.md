### 5. Final Audit Conclusions
The performance gap of 25.1K DPS is primarily driven by rotational density and cooldown neglect. 

1.  **Defensive and Utility Neglect:** The Player failed to use **Vampiric Blood** entirely. In a 2:28 fight, this should be used on cooldown or for major mechanics to boost healing received and maximum health. Additionally, the Player ignored **Icebound Fortitude**, **Lichborne**, and **Anti-Magic Zone**, all of which the Reference used to stabilize and provide group utility.
2.  **Resource & GCD Efficiency:** Deathsbladé is lagging significantly in total casts (122 vs 165). This is evident in **Death Strike** counts (27 vs 37). The Reference is generating and spending Runic Power much more efficiently. 
3.  **Bone Shield & Marrowrend:** The Player's Bone Shield uptime (88.4%) is low for a Blood DK. Marrowrend was only cast 10 times compared to the Reference's 15. Maintaining Bone Shield is non-negotiable for armor and Haste (via Abomination Limb/talents).
4.  **Stat Weighting:** The Player is heavily over-budgeted in **Versatility** (6.22%) while sacrificing **Mastery** (14.74%). The Reference prioritizes Mastery (19.30%), which directly scales the **Blood Shield** from Death Strike, providing significantly more effective health and allowing for more aggressive play.
5.  **Rotational Missteps:** The Player used **Death's Caress** 3 times. This should only be used to pick up mobs at range or if absolutely forced out of melee. Heart Strike should be the primary filler and Rune spender. High **Death and Decay** casts (13) suggest the Player may be refreshing it too early or using it outside of its optimal window.
