### 5. Final Audit Conclusions.
The Player’s performance shows a strong foundational understanding but suffers from significant rotational passivity and defensive neglect.

1.  **Defensive Neglect:** The most glaring issue is the zero usage of **Vampiric Blood**, **Lichborne**, and **Icebound Fortitude**. As a Blood Death Knight, Vampiric Blood is a primary throughput cooldown for both survivability and healing; failing to use it 4 times (as the Reference did) severely limits the kit.
2.  **Resource Bottlenecking:** The Player cast **Death Strike** 10 fewer times. Since Death Strike is the primary Runic Power sink and source of **Blood Shield** (Mastery conversion), this directly impacts both DPS and tankiness. 
3.  **Stat Weights:** The Player is heavily over-budgeted in **Versatility** (6.22%) at the expense of **Mastery** (14.74%). The Reference prioritizes Mastery (19.30%), which scales the physical damage mitigation of Blood Shield and overall damage.
4.  **Button Density:** Despite having 6.8% downtime, the Player's low cast count suggests they are waiting too long between GCDs. Focus on filling every available GCD with **Heart Strike** or **Blood Boil** to maximize Runic Power generation for more **Death Strikes**.
