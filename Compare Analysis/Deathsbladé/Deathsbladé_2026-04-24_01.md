### 7. Final Audit Conclusions:
Deathsbladé's performance at 65% parse is primarily hindered by low rotational frequency and poor defensive integration.
1.  **Defensive Neglect:** The most glaring issue is the zero usage of **Vampiric Blood**, **Icebound Fortitude**, and **Lichborne**. As a Blood Death Knight, Vampiric Blood is a primary throughput and survivability tool. The Reference utilized it 4 times, significantly increasing their effective HP and healing received.
2.  **Resource Bottlenecking:** With 43 fewer casts in a similar duration, the Player is leaving a massive amount of damage on the table. The "Per Cast" value for **Heart Strike** and **Blood Boil** being significantly lower suggests the Player is not effectively utilizing **Death and Decay** for cleave or is failing to pool resources for high-value windows.
3.  **Bone Shield Maintenance:** Marrowrend usage is low. Ensure **Bone Shield** stacks never drop below 7 to maximize the benefits of various passives and the armor value.
4.  **Utility & Talents:** The Player missed usage of **Abomination Limb**, which provides significant grouping and damage. Incorporate this into the opener and subsequent cooldown windows.
