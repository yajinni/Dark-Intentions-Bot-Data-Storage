### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Deathsbladé's performance is significantly hampered by extreme downtime (22.6%) and a passive rotation. By not standing in [Death and Decay](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=188290), the player lost a constant 5% damage and healing modifier.
       - Resource management was poor, with a 19.1% Rune waste rate, indicating long periods of sitting on full resources without casting.
   - **Casting Optimizations (STRICTLY MANDATORY):**
       - **[Death Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=49998):** (35% fewer casts). **Advice:** You are pooling too much Runic Power or failing to use it reactively. Use Death Strike to prevent Runic Power capping (>75) and immediately after taking large hits.
       - **[Blood Boil](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=50842):** (44% fewer casts). **Advice:** You missed 30 potential casts. Blood Boil should be used on cooldown to maintain [Blood Plague](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=55078) and generate threat/damage.
       - **[Heart Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=206930):** (40% fewer casts). **Per Cast Reasoning:** The 23% lower damage per cast is driven by a massive **41.68% Crit gap** and the lack of [Sanguine Ground](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=391459) buff alignment. **Advice:** Use Heart Strike as your primary filler whenever Bone Shield is >7 stacks to generate Runic Power.
       - **[Dancing Rune Weapon](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1264353):** (44% fewer hits/ticks). **Per Tick Reasoning:** The reference achieved 471.2K per tick vs your 186K. This is due to the reference aligning DRW with high-haste windows and the 41.68% higher crit rate. **Advice:** Ensure DRW is used on cooldown and that you are actively attacking while it is active to maximize mirrored hits.
       - **[Death and Decay](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=43265):** (52% higher cast count, but 33% lower average damage). **Advice:** You are recasting this too often outside of procs or moving out of it. Stand in your DnD to gain the [Sanguine Ground](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=391459) bonus.
