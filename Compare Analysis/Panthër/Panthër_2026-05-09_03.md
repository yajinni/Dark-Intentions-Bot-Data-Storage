### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is significantly hindered by fundamental rotational errors, specifically regarding finisher quality and bleed snapshotting. While the player maintains basic uptimes, the "value per cast" of core abilities is far below the reference.
       - Resource management is a major concern; a high rate of [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) casts were used with insufficient Combo Points and Energy, leading to a massive loss in potential throughput. Furthermore, the player's 34.3% downtime suggests significant gaps in activity compared to the reference.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** Per cast value is 28.5% lower than the reference. **Advice:** In Midnight 12.0.5, [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) snapshotting is vital. Ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active before applying or refreshing [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) to lock in the damage bonus for the duration of the bleed.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Per cast value is 10.3% lower, and the player cast this 63 times compared to 73. **Advice:** You are frequently casting this at low Combo Points (13.8% of casts). You MUST pool Energy to at least 50 and wait for 5 Combo Points before casting to maximize the damage multiplier.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Per cast value is 37.1% lower than the reference. **Advice:** Similar to Rip, [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) must be snapshotted with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). Ensure you are not overwriting high-value snapshots with unbuffed applications.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Total casts are 29% lower than the reference. **Advice:** This is your primary filler. High downtime (34.3%) is causing you to miss out on significant [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) volume, which in turn slows down your Combo Point generation.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** Per cast value is 28.4% lower. **Advice:** Ensure you maintain high uptime and only refresh within the Pandemic window to avoid wasting GCDs, while ensuring it benefits from your current stat procs.
       - **[Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838):** Per cast value is 9.9% lower (rounding to 10% threshold). **Advice:** Align this major cooldown with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) and other trinket procs like [Algeth'ar Puzzle](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=383781) to maximize the bleed damage.
