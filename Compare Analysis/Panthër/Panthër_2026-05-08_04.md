### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is significantly hampered by extreme downtime (34.3%) and a failure to maintain consistent pressure on secondary targets. While core bleed snapshotting with Tiger's Fury appears present, the sheer volume of missed Global Cooldowns (GCDs) prevents higher throughput.
       - Resource management could not be fully audited due to data limitations, but the low parse suggests significant energy pooling inefficiencies or overcapping during Berserk windows.
   - **Casting Optimizations (STRICTLY MANDATORY):**
       - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** The Player recorded 457 swings compared to a significantly higher cast volume from the leader. Advice: Improve "Time on Target." Use [Dash](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1850) or [Stampeding Roar](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=77764) to minimize travel time between mechanics and the boss.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** The total damage contribution is lower than expected for a Wildstalker build. Advice: Ensure [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) is snapshotted with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) and maintained with 100% uptime, especially on secondary targets like Blistercreep to proc [Bloodseeker Vines](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=439531).
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Per cast value of 73.0K suggests many bites were cast at low energy. Advice: Pool to at least 50 Energy before casting [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) to ensure the 100% damage bonus is applied.
