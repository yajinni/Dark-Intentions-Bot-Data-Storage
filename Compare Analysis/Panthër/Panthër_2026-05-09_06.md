### 1. Audit Conclusions
- **Performance Overview:**
    Panthër's performance is significantly hindered by fundamental rotational errors and resource mismanagement. While core bleed uptimes are acceptable, the quality of finishers—specifically [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568)—is poor. The player frequently casts finishers at sub-optimal Combo Point counts and fails to pool enough energy to trigger the 100% damage bonus on Bites. Additionally, a 34.3% downtime suggests significant periods of inactivity or mechanical distraction compared to the reference's 28.7%.

- **Casting Optimizations:**
    - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Casts: +192.1% | Per Cast Value: -75.8%
        - **Reason:** The player has a 13.8% Low CP cast rate and 12.1% Under-Energized rate. Additionally, lower stat scaling (specifically Mastery) reduces the ceiling of each Bite.
        - **Advice:** Never cast [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) with fewer than 5 Combo Points. Pool at least 50 Energy before casting to ensure you trigger the bonus damage.
    - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Casts: +261.7% | Per Cast Value: -87.1%
        - **Reason:** Excessive casting of Shred indicates a failure to transition to finishers, leading to significant Combo Point waste (18.2% waste).
        - **Advice:** Use Shred only as a generator. Once at 5 CP, immediately prioritize a finisher ([Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) or [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568)).
    - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Casts: +48.4% | Per Cast Value: -64.6%
        - **Reason:** Lower per-tick value is likely due to lack of [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) snapshotting and refreshing outside of the pandemic window.
        - **Advice:** Ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active before applying or refreshing Rake to benefit from the damage snapshot.
    - **[Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838):** Casts: -53.6%
        - **Advice:** This is a major 45s cooldown. You missed approximately 2 usage windows. Use on cooldown to maximize [Unseen Predator](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1263660) procs.
    - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** Casts: +2019.0% | Per Cast Value: -98.2%
        - **Reason:** This discrepancy often indicates the player is auto-attacking without pressing active abilities (supported by the high downtime) or differences in weapon speed/buff alignment.
