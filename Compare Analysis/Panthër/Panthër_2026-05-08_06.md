### Encounter: Vorasius (Mythic)
### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is characterized by solid core buff maintenance but hampered by significant rotational gaps and snapshotting inefficiencies. The player successfully maintained high uptimes on cooldowns like Tiger's Fury and Berserk, yet the actual damage output per cast for bleed abilities was considerably lower than the reference. This indicates a fundamental issue with aligning damage-increasing buffs before applying DoTs.
       - Resource management was a mixed bag; while energy and combo point waste percentages were statistically similar to the reference, the player's overall activity (CPM) was much lower, leading to a substantial throughput deficit.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** Total damage is 25% lower and damage per tick is 28% lower than the reference. **Advice:** The technical reason for the lower per-cast value is missing [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) alignment. Ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active *before* applying or refreshing [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) to snapshot the damage bonus.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Total damage is 30% lower and damage per tick is 37% lower. **Advice:** Technical reason: missing snapshotting alignment. Like Rip, [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) must be applied while [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active to maximize bleed value throughout its duration.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Player utilized 29% fewer casts. **Advice:** This cast deficit indicates significant downtime or energy pooling without spending. Aim to use [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) more aggressively as your primary generator when finishers are not yet ready.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Player utilized 13% fewer casts. **Advice:** Pool energy and ensure you are at 5 Combo Points before casting. The lower frequency suggests slow resource generation or inefficient spending of Combo Points on other abilities.
       - **[Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838):** Total damage is 25% lower and cast frequency is 16% lower. **Advice:** Technical reason: Lower [Mastery](https://www.wowhead.com/stat=49) stat scaling and potential lack of alignment with [Berserk](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106951) windows. Use this on cooldown to ensure maximum usages per encounter.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** Damage per tick is 28% lower. **Advice:** Technical reason: Significant difference in Mastery and secondary stat scaling. Ensure 100% uptime but prioritize spending Energy on Shred/Finishers if resources are tight.
       - **[Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785):** Player over-utilized this ability (20 casts vs. 5). **Advice:** [Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785) is an AoE filler. On a primarily single-target encounter like Vorasius, [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) should be prioritized for better single-target damage and resource efficiency.
