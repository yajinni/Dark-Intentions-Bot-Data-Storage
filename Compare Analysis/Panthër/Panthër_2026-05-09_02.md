### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is significantly below the benchmark, characterized by major rotational gaps and resource management inefficiencies. The player's high downtime (34.3%) and lower total cast count indicate missed global cooldowns and potential positioning issues during the Mythic Vorasius encounter.

The per-cast difference (-28.5%) indicates that many applications are occurring without the [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) buff active.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Casts are +192.1% higher but total damage is -75.8% lower. **Advice:** You are casting this finisher too often at low power. Each cast deals 10.3% less damage than the reference. You MUST pool energy to at least 50 before casting to trigger the 100% bonus damage multiplier. Stop casting [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) at < 5 Combo Points.
       - **[Unseen Predator](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1263660):** Total damage is -94.7% lower. **Advice:** This indicates poor [Wildstalker](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1263660) hero spec execution. You must ensure [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) and [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) are maintained on the target to generate [Symbiotic Blooms](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=439530) and maximize this passive.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Total damage is 64.6% lower. **Advice:** Similar to Rip, the per-cast damage is 37.1% lower than the reference. This confirms that [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) applications are missing the [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) snapshot. Re-apply [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) only when [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active.
       - **[Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838):** Casts are 53.6% lower. **Advice:** This is a major 45s cooldown. You missed approximately half of your potential windows. Use this on cooldown to generate 5 CP and apply its heavy bleed.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Total damage is 87.1% lower. **Advice:** Despite high cast counts, the throughput is missing. Ensure you are not over-relying on [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) while [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is down or while capping energy.
       - **[Bloodseeker Vines](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=439531):** Damage is 96.0% lower. **Advice:** This is a direct result of low [Wildstalker](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1263660) proc frequency. Improve your bleed snapshotting to increase these procs.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** Total damage is 74.0% lower. **Advice:** Maintenance of the Lunar Inspiration dot is falling off. Maintain 100% uptime for consistent CP generation.
       - **[Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785):** Total damage is 94.9% lower. **Advice:** Per-cast value is 37% lower. Do not use [Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785) in single-target unless you have no other builders and need to refresh a buff; however, the reference utilized it more effectively in specific add windows.
       - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** Per-cast value is 11.5% lower. **Advice:** This is largely due to lower stat scaling (Agility and Versatility deltas) and missed uptime on the target.
       - **[Twilight Barrage](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1281579) & [Wrath](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5176):** Damage is >96% lower. **Advice:** These should generally not be part of your Feral rotation.
