### 7. Final Audit Conclusions:
The performance gap between Panthër and the Reference is primarily mechanical rather than gear-based. While the 8.6% Agility gap provides a baseline advantage for the Reference, the following issues are critical:
1.  **Bleed Quality:** You must prioritize applying **Rip** and **Rake** only when **Tiger's Fury** is active to benefit from snapshotting. Your higher Mastery should theoretically make your bleeds stronger, yet the Reference is outperforming you per-tick.
2.  **Finisher Frequency:** You are losing nearly 40 Ferocious Bites over the course of the fight. Ensure you are not over-capping Combo Points and that you are utilizing **Shred** instead of **Swipe** during single-target or low-cleave priority windows.
3.  **Utility & Movement:** The Reference utilized **Tiger Dash** and **Cat Form** shifts more fluidly to minimize downtime. Your 34.7% downtime is high for a Heroic encounter; focus on maintaining uptime during boss movement phases using **Moonfire** (Lunar Inspiration) to keep generating resources.
