<small>*(Player: 87.4K DPS | Reference: 114.3K DPS)*</small>

### Encounter: Vorasius (Mythic)
### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is currently hindered by significant rotational gaps and poor bleed snapshotting. While the core mechanics of a Wildstalker Feral Druid are present, the player is losing a substantial amount of throughput to high downtime (34.3%) and inefficient finisher usage. 
       - The primary drivers for the 28% parse are the significantly lower per-cast values on primary bleeds (Rip and Rake) compared to the reference, suggesting a failure to align [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) for snapshotting.
       - Resource management (Section 4.1) shows acceptable waste levels, but the quality of finishers (Section 4.2) is suboptimal, with 13.8% of Ferocious Bites cast at low Combo Points and 12.1% cast without the required 50 energy bonus.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** The player’s average damage per cast is 28.5% lower than the reference. 
           - **Advice:** Rip damage is heavily dependent on snapshotting. Ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active **before** applying or refreshing Rip to lock in the damage bonus.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** The per-cast damage is 37.1% lower than the reference.
           - **Advice:** Like Rip, Rake must be snapshotted with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). Additionally, ensure 100% uptime is maintained without excessive refreshing outside of pandemic windows.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Cast frequency is 13.7% lower, and per-cast damage is 10.3% lower.
           - **Analysis:** Technical reason for lower per-cast value is likely the 12.1% "Under-Energized" rate and 4.4% lower Mastery scaling.
           - **Advice:** Pool Energy to at least 50 before casting at 5 Combo Points. Casting [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) with low energy sacrifices the 100% damage multiplier.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Total casts are 29.1% lower than the reference.
           - **Advice:** This is a direct result of high downtime (123.9s). Improve your active time on the boss to increase the frequency of your primary builder.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** Per-cast damage is 28.4% lower than the reference.
           - **Advice:** Ensure [Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625) uptime is maintained and prioritize using it as a filler when at range or during movement, provided it is buffed by your secondary stats and any active procs.
       - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** Per-hit damage is 11.5% lower. 
           - **Analysis:** This is primarily driven by the 4-item level difference and significantly lower Mastery (38.4% vs 42.9%).
