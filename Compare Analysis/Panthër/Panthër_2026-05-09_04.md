### Encounter: Vorasius (Mythic)
### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance was significantly hindered by a combination of high downtime (34.3%) and fundamental resource management errors. While the player maintained decent uptime on core bleeds, the quality of finishers was poor—regularly casting [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) at low combo points and insufficient energy. Furthermore, a total lack of primary consumables (Flask, Food, and Potions) resulted in a lower baseline of primary and secondary stats compared to the reference.
       - Resource management was a primary failure point; 12.1% of [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) casts were "Under-Energized" (< 50 Energy), and 13.8% were cast at low Combo Points, leading to massive throughput losses during peak windows.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** The player's per-cast value was 28.5% lower than the reference. 
           - **Advice:** This indicates poor snapshotting. As a Wildstalker, ensuring [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active *before* applying or refreshing [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) is mandatory. Pool energy to ensure you can reach 5 CP and refresh during the snapshot window.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Shows a +192.1% cast frequency delta but a -75.8% value per cast delta.
           - **Advice:** The player is spamming low-impact Bites. You MUST only cast [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) at 5 Combo Points and with at least 50 Energy to trigger the 100% damage bonus. 
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Shows a +261.7% cast frequency delta but -87.1% value delta.
           - **Advice:** Excessive [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) usage suggests a lack of priority on maintaining high-value bleeds or over-generating when finishers should be prioritized. Ensure [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) and [Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625) are maintained before filling with Shred.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Shows a -64.6% per-cast value difference.
           - **Advice:** Similar to [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079), [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) must be snapshotted with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). The reference log shows significantly higher average hits due to consistent alignment with damage buffs and higher Mastery scaling.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** Value per cast is 74.0% lower.
           - **Advice:** This is likely due to the player refreshing the DoT during low-proc windows or missing the [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) overlap. Ensure 100% uptime but prioritize refreshing during stat-procs or buffs.
