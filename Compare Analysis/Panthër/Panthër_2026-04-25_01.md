### 7. Final Audit Conclusions.
The performance gap between Panthër and the Reference is driven by three critical factors:
1.  **Talent/Ability Selection:** The total absence of **Feral Frenzy** is a catastrophic loss of single-target throughput. This ability provides both massive direct damage and a rapid influx of Combo Points for Ferocious Bite.
2.  **Spending Efficiency:** The Player cast 16 fewer **Ferocious Bites** than the Reference despite a similar fight duration. This indicates that Panthër is likely over-capping energy or failing to convert Combo Points into finishers during high-pressure windows.
3.  **Active Uptime:** With 43.1% downtime, the Player is spending nearly half the fight not casting. Improving GCD uptime and ensuring that Every Tiger's Fury window is maximized will be essential for reaching the 99th percentile. Panthër should prioritize sticking to the boss and ensuring that **Shred** or **Rake** is used whenever energy is available and finishers aren't ready.
