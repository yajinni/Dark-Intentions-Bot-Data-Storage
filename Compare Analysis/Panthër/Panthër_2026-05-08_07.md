### Encounter: Vorasius (Mythic)
### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is significantly hindered by poor "snapshotting" of bleeds and inefficient filler selection. While resource waste percentages are comparable to the reference, the raw damage per cast of core bleeds (Rip/Rake) is drastically lower, suggesting these are being applied without proper buff alignment. Furthermore, a high downtime of 17.1% and the use of Swipe on a primarily single-target encounter resulted in a substantial loss of potential Shred-based generation and damage.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** Per Cast value is 28.5% lower than the reference. This is likely due to applying Rip without [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) active. **Advice:** Always ensure Tiger's Fury is active before applying or refreshing Rip to maximize the snapshot damage bonus.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Per Cast value is 37.1% lower than the reference. Similar to Rip, this indicates a failure to snapshot Tiger's Fury. **Advice:** Pool energy and activate Tiger's Fury before refreshing Rake. 
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** The player cast Shred 78 times compared to the reference's 110 (29% difference). **Advice:** Shred is your primary single-target generator. The lower count stems from high downtime (61.7s) and spending GCDs on Swipe.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** Per Cast value is 28.4% lower than the reference. **Advice:** Ensure maximum uptime and aim to refresh during damage windows; the lower value suggests downtime during the bleed's duration.
       - **[Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838):** Per Cast value is 10% lower than the reference. **Advice:** Align Feral Frenzy with Tiger's Fury and Berserk to ensure the initial hits and the following bleed benefit from all active modifiers.
       - **[Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785):** The player cast Swipe 20 times while the reference only used it 5 times. **Advice:** Stop using Swipe as a filler on single-target encounters like Vorasius. Each Swipe used is a lost [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221), which deals more damage and generates more pressure in a Wildstalker build.
