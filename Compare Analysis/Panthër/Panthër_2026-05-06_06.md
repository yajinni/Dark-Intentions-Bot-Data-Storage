       - Panthër's performance is currently hindered by significant activity gaps and suboptimal resource management. While core cooldowns like Berserk and Tiger's Fury are being utilized, the player suffers from high downtime (34.3%) and inconsistent finisher quality.
       - The primary drivers for the lower parse are a lack of Shred casts (27% fewer than the reference) and executing finishers below the 5 Combo Point/50 Energy thresholds. Additionally, a failure to align bleed refreshes (Rip/Rake) with Tiger's Fury snapshots has led to a major loss in per-tick damage efficiency.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** The player cast Shred 78 times compared to the reference's 108 (27.7% difference). **Advice:** This is your primary filler.        - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Cast frequency was 17% lower than the reference. **Advice:** This is tied to your lower Shred count. More generators lead to more finishers. Ensure you are pooling to 5 Combo Points and 50+ Energy before casting to maximize damage.
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** While the cast count was higher, the **Per Cast** value was 32% lower (215.6K vs 316.8K). **Advice:** This indicates poor snapshotting. You MUST ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active *before* applying or refreshing Rip. The reference achieves much higher tick values by strictly adhering to this alignment.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** The **Per Cast** value was 25.5% lower than the reference. **Advice:** Similar to Rip, your Rake damage is suffering from lack of snapshotting. Always refresh Rake while Tiger's Fury is active to carry the damage bonus through the duration.
