### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The performance gap between Panthër and the Reference is primarily attributed to rotational omissions and resource throughput rather than raw stat differences.

1.  **Missing Feral Frenzy:** The most critical divergence is the total absence of **Feral Frenzy** in the Player's log. The Reference generated 2.2M damage (nearly 15% of total output) from 5 casts of this ability. This suggests a talent build error or a complete rotational oversight.
2.  **Finisher Volume:** The Reference cast **Ferocious Bite** 39 times compared to the Player's 23. This is a 70% increase in finisher volume. While the Player's average damage per bite was higher (due to higher Crit/Mastery), the sheer volume of bites from the Reference, facilitated by higher Haste and better energy management, created a massive lead.
3.  **Haste Disparity:** The Reference prioritized Haste (20.73%) over Mastery, whereas the Player favored Mastery (37.41%). In this encounter, the Reference's higher Haste allowed for faster energy recovery, leading to more Shreds and Bites, which outperformed the Player's higher per-hit damage strategy.
4.  **Activity Rate:** The Player's 26.3% downtime is too high for Heroic-level execution. Ensuring that every available GCD is used for pooling or generating combo points is essential for closing the gap to a 99th percentile performance.
