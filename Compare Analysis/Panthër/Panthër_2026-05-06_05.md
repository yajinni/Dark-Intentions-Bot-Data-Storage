### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is characterized by a solid understanding of the core Feral toolkit but is significantly hampered by high downtime and suboptimal resource spending. While buff maintenance on Tiger's Fury was consistent with the reference, the player suffered from a 27.7% deficit in [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) casts, indicating periods of rotational inactivity.
       - The primary throughput gap stems from **Finisher Quality** (Section 4.2). Panthër cast 13.8% of [Ferocious Bites](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) at sub-5 Combo Points, which is a major violation of the spec's core priority. Additionally, a large discrepancy in [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) per-cast damage suggests a failure to properly snapshot bleeds or align them with high-value buff windows.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** The player's per-cast damage was 31.9% lower than the reference. **Advice:** Ensure [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) is always applied or refreshed while [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active to snapshot the damage bonus.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Per-cast damage was 25.5% lower than the reference. **Advice:** Like Rip, [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) must be snapshotted with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). Avoid refreshing [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) without a damage buff active if the current bleed is already empowered.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Player had 27.7% fewer casts. **Advice:** This is your primary filler. The gap is due to 34.3% downtime. Improve positioning to minimize time away from the boss and use [Dash](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1850) or [Stampeding Roar](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=77764) more aggressively to maintain uptime.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Casts were 17.1% lower than the reference. **Advice:** Stop casting this at < 5 Combo Points. Pool Energy to reach the 50 Energy threshold before casting to ensure the damage is doubled.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** Per-cast damage was 8.6% lower, but total output was matched. **Advice:** Maintain this as a low-priority filler when unable to reach melee range, but prioritize generating Combo Points with [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) whenever possible.
