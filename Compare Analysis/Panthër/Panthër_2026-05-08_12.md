### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is characterized by a solid foundational understanding of the Feral rotation, but is significantly hampered by high downtime and inefficient resource utilization. While bleed maintenance is present, the quality of those bleeds—specifically snapshotting with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217)—lags behind the reference. 
       - Resource management is a major concern; a 76.3% Energy waste indicates that [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) or [Berserk](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106951) windows are not being converted into casts effectively. Furthermore, the player is spending significant GCDs on [Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785) in a primarily single-target encounter, which dilutes total throughput.
   - **Casting Optimizations (STRICTLY MANDATORY):**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** The player's per-cast average is 31.9% lower than the reference (215.6K vs 316.8K). 
           - **Advice:** This gap is likely caused by poor "snapshotting." In 12.0.5, [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) increases the damage of bleeds for their entire duration if active when applied. Always ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active before refreshing [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) at 5 Combo Points.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** There is a 27.7% deficit in cast count (78 vs 108). 
           - **Advice:** [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) is your primary builder. The lower cast count suggests the player is either over-pooling energy or losing GCDs during the encounter. Increase active uptime to generate more Combo Points for [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568).
       - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** Total damage shows a -93.4% delta compared to the reference. 
           - **Advice:** This indicates a severe target uptime issue. As a Feral Druid, your auto-attacks are vital for resource generation and passive procs. Ensure you are in melee range of Vorasius at all times.
       - **[Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785):** The player cast this 20 times, whereas the reference cast it 0 times. 
           - **Advice:** Vorasius is a single-target focused encounter. [Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785) deals significantly less damage than [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) on one target. Replace these casts with [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) to improve CP generation and DPS.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Per-cast average is 10.2% lower than the reference.
           - **Advice:** This suggests the player is occasionally casting [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) with less than 50 Energy. [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) consumes up to 25 additional Energy to double its damage; always pool to 50 Energy before using this finisher.
