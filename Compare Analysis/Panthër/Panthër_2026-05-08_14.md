### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is characterized by significant rotational downtime (34.3%) and a failure to maintain core bleed uptimes compared to the reference. While the player demonstrates high uptime on several passive buffs, the actual execution of the Feral rotation—specifically the maintenance of [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) and [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822)—is far below the required standard for Mythic progression. 
       - Resource management is a major concern; with over 76% of Energy wasted, the player is likely over-pooling or failing to spend resources during high-haste windows like [Berserk](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106951). Furthermore, the player spent significantly more damage on secondary targets compared to the reference, diluting boss pressure.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **Rip:** Casts are 71.9% lower than the reference. **Advice:** This is your highest priority finisher. Ensure 5 Combo Points are used and Tiger's Fury is active before applying.
       - **Rake:** Casts are 59.2% lower. **Advice:** Maintain 100% uptime. Do not let this bleed fall off, as it fuels [Wildstalker](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=441591) procs.
       - **Feral Frenzy:** Casts are 87.2% lower. **Advice:** This is a powerful 45s cooldown. You missed several opportunities to use this to instantly generate 5 Combo Points for a snapshotted Rip.
       - **Ferocious Bite:** Casts are 19.6% lower. **Advice:** Per Cast value is 10.2% lower because the player is likely casting at low energy. Ensure you have 50+ Energy to maximize the damage multiplier.
       - **Moonfire:** Casts are 69.4% lower. **Advice:** Maintain this DoT consistently to facilitate more [Bloodseeker Vines](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=439531) generation.
       - **Swipe:** Casts are 74.5% lower. **Advice:** While Swipe is an AOE tool, ensure it is not used over [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) in single-target unless you are specifically target-swapping to adds.
       - **Melee:** Cast count is +483.0% higher. **Technical Reason:** This is a byproduct of the 34.3% downtime; when the player stops casting, melee becomes a larger portion of the event log relatively, though the total damage remains low due to missing [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) windows.
