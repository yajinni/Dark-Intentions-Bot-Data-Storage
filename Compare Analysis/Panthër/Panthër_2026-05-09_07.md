### Encounter: Vorasius (Mythic)
### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is currently hindered by significant rotational errors and suboptimal resource management. While the player maintains a decent core of buff uptimes, the quality of their finishing moves and the lack of snapshotting on primary bleeds are leading to a large throughput gap.
       - Resource management (Sections 4.1 & 4.2) shows a high rate of [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) casts performed at low Combo Points and insufficient Energy levels, which directly sacrifices the spec's primary damage multipliers.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** Per Cast value is 28.5% lower than the reference. **Advice:** This indicates a failure to snapshot your bleeds. Ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active **before** applying or refreshing Rip to maximize its damage for the entire duration.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Per Cast value is 37.1% lower than the reference. **Advice:** Similar to Rip, Rake must be snapshotted with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). Additionally, prioritize maintaining 100% uptime to ensure [Symbiotic Blooms](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=439530) (Wildstalker) proc efficiency.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Cast count is 13.7% lower and Per Cast damage is 10.4% lower. **Advice:** You are casting too many "Under-Energized" bites. Pool your energy and only cast [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) when you have 5 Combo Points AND at least 50 Energy to trigger the 100% bonus damage multiplier.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Cast count is 29.1% lower. **Advice:** This is your primary filler. The lower count suggests high downtime or over-pooling. Minimize idle GCDs and use Shred to build Combo Points more aggressively when not capping Energy.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** Per Cast damage is 28.4% lower. **Advice:** Ensure Moonfire is maintained for the [Lunar Inspiration](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625) benefit, but prioritize alignment with damage-increasing buffs.
       - **[Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785):** Cast count is 300% higher than the reference. **Advice:** Vorasius is primarily a single-target encounter. You used Swipe 20 times compared to the reference's 5. On single targets, [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) is much more efficient for Combo Point generation.
