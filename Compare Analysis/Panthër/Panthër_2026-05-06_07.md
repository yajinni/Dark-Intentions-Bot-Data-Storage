### Encounter: Vorasius (Mythic)
### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is significantly below the reference, primarily due to excessive downtime (34.3%) and a failure to properly snapshot bleed abilities with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). While core buff uptimes like [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) and [Berserk](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106951) were well-aligned with the reference, the mechanical execution of finishers was flawed. The player cast [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) at low combo points and low energy frequently, resulting in a substantial loss of potential throughput.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** Total damage is 27% lower and damage per cast is 46.9% lower than the reference. 
           - **Advice:** This massive per-cast gap indicates a failure to snapshot [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). Always ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active before applying or refreshing your bleeds to maximize their tick damage.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Total damage is 34.7% lower and casts are 20.6% lower. Damage per cast is 11.3% lower. 
           - **Advice:** The player frequently cast [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) with less than 5 Combo Points and less than 50 Energy (as seen in Section 4.2). Pool Energy to at least 50 and ensure you are at 5 Combo Points before using this finisher to benefit from the 100% damage multiplier.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Total damage is 23.5% lower and damage per cast is 34.3% lower. 
           - **Advice:** Similar to [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079), the per-cast variance stems from missing [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) snapshots. Maintain 100% uptime but only refresh while buffed.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Total damage is 30% lower and casts are 38.4% lower. 
           - **Advice:** This is a direct result of higher downtime. You missed 30 potential [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) GCDs compared to the reference. Improve target uptime and ABC (Always Be Casting) discipline.
       - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** Total damage is 16% lower.
           - **Advice:** The reference maintained significantly better uptime on the boss. Minimize movement out of melee range and use [Dash](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1850) or [Stampeding Roar](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=77764) to return to the target faster after mechanics.
       - **[Bursting Growth](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=440122):** Total damage is 13.3% lower.
           - **Advice:** Damage is tied to the application frequency of [Wildstalker](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=440122) bleeds. Improving your [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) and [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) efficiency will naturally increase this passive output.
   - Gear & Consumable audit found some improvements could be made. [Click Here](#9-gems-enchants-and-consumables) to view.
