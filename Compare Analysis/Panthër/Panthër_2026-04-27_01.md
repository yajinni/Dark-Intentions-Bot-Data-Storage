### 7. Final Audit Conclusions
The 30.2K DPS gap between Panthër and the Reference is driven by three primary technical factors:

1.  **Talent/Ability Omission (Feral Frenzy):** The Reference generated 4.0M damage from **Feral Frenzy**. The Player did not use this ability at all. This is a 45-second cooldown that provides 5 instant Combo Points; missing this severely reduces the frequency of **Ferocious Bite** and **Rip** applications.
2.  **Mastery & Snapshotting Efficiency:** The Reference has a 7% Mastery advantage, but the "Per Cast" value of **Rip** is 78% higher (476K vs 266K). This confirms the Reference is successfully snapshotting **Tiger's Fury** and potentially other damage modifiers into their bleeds, whereas the Player is likely refreshing bleeds without buffs active.
3.  **Active Uptime:** The Player lost 15 seconds of active combat time relative to the Reference. This resulted in 20 fewer **Shred** casts and 6 fewer **Moonfire** casts, leading to lower Combo Point generation and reduced overall pressure.

**Action Plan:** Ensure **Feral Frenzy** is talented and used on cooldown. Prioritize refreshing **Rip** and **Rake** only when **Tiger's Fury** is active to maximize bleed value. Reduce downtime by utilizing **Moonfire** as a filler when forced out of melee range.
