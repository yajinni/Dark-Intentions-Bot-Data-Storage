### 7. Final Audit Conclusions.
The 30.2K DPS deficit is driven by three primary factors: **Mastery Scaling**, **Snapshotting Efficiency**, and **Active Casting Time**.

1.  **Mastery Gap:** The Reference holds a substantial +7.02% Mastery advantage. For Feral, this directly amplifies Bleed and Finisher damage, which is reflected in the Reference's significantly higher "Per Cast" values for Rip and Ferocious Bite.
2.  **Rip Snapshotting:** Despite casting **Rip** more frequently (24 vs 21), Panthër achieved 3.6M less total damage with the ability. The Reference's Rip value per cast is nearly 80% higher (476.2K vs 266.7K), identifying a failure by Panthër to consistently apply or refresh Rip during peak damage windows (Tiger's Fury/Bloodtalons).
3.  **Frenzy Divergence:** The Reference utilized **Feral Frenzy** 13 times, while Panthër used **Frantic Frenzy** only 8 times. This indicates either a talent pathing discrepancy or a failure to utilize this high-impact cooldown on every available window.
4.  **Builder Efficiency:** Panthër over-relied on **Swipe** (34 casts) compared to the Reference (18 casts), likely at the expense of **Shred**. In a single-target focused environment like Averzian, Shred should be the priority builder to maximize individual hit value.

**Action Items:** Focus on tightening the rotation to reduce downtime to <32% and prioritize Mastery over Crit/Versatility in gear selection to close the throughput gap. Focus on Snapshotting Rip only when major buffs are active.
