### Encounter: Vorasius (Mythic)
### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is significantly hindered by fundamental rotational errors, primarily regarding finisher quality and resource management. While activity levels are relatively high, the efficiency of those actions is low. The player is frequently casting finishers at low combo points and, more critically, failing to pool energy for the [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) multiplier. Furthermore, a substantial damage gap in bleed throughput suggests a failure to properly snapshot [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) and [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217).
       - Resource management (Section 3.1) and Finisher Quality (Section 3.2) show that 13.8% of bites were at low CP and over 12% were under-energized, directly leading to the 28% parse.

   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** (Per Cast Difference: -28.5%). **Reason:** Missing buff alignment with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). Feral Druids must snapshot bleeds. **Advice:** Always ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active before applying or refreshing [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) to lock in the 15% damage bonus for its entire duration.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** (Casts Difference: +192.1% | Per Cast Difference: -75.8%). **Reason:** Improper resource pooling and low CP usage. **Advice:** Pool energy to at least 50 before casting [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) and ensure you are at exactly 5 Combo Points. Casting below these thresholds results in a massive damage penalty.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** (Casts Difference: +48.4% | Per Cast Difference: -64.6%). **Reason:** Over-refreshing without snapshotting. **Advice:** Avoid refreshing [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) unless it is in the pandemic window or you have a [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) window to improve the bleed quality.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** (Casts Difference: +261.7%). **Advice:** The high cast count compared to the reference suggests a lack of efficiency in generating combo points. Focus on utilizing [Clearcasting](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135700) procs more effectively to reduce raw energy spend.
       - **[Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838):** (Casts Difference: -53.6%). **Advice:** You missed roughly half of your potential [Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838) casts. This is a primary source of combo points and heavy bleed damage; use it on cooldown.
       - **[Moonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=155625):** (Casts Difference: +11.3% | Per Cast Difference: -74.0%). **Reason:** Lower uptime on secondary stats or missing damage multipliers during dot application. **Advice:** Maintain high uptime but prioritize energy for cat-form abilities.
       - **[Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785):** (Per Cast Difference: -94.9%). **Reason:** Likely used on a single target rather than during add spawns. **Advice:** Only use [Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=106785) when 2+ targets are present; otherwise, [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) is your superior filler.
       - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** (Casts Difference: +2019.0% | Per Cast Difference: -98.2%). **Reason:** Significant target uptime differences or weapon scaling.
