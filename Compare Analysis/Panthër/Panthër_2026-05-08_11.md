### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance is significantly below the reference, primarily due to excessive downtime (34.3%) and a failure to effectively manage bleed snapshotting. While core buff uptimes are stable, the quality of finishers and the frequency of generator casts are insufficient for high-tier Mythic performance.
       - Resource management shows high energy waste, and the value per cast of primary finishers suggests that Panthër is not consistently aligning [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) with bleed applications ([Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) and [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822)).
   - **Casting Optimizations (STRICTLY MANDATORY):**
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** The player's average Rip value is 215.6K vs. the reference's 316.8K (31.9% difference). **Advice:** This is a snapshotting failure. You must ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active at the moment you apply or refresh Rip. Never refresh a strong Rip with a weaker one.
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Panthër cast Shred 78 times compared to Nekonyan's 108 (27.7% difference). **Advice:** Shred is your primary builder. The lower cast count is directly linked to your 34% downtime. Always keep your GCDs active and use Shred to bridge gaps to 5 Combo Points.
       - **[Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568):** Value per cast is 10.2% lower than the reference. **Advice:** You are likely casting Ferocious Bite with less than 50 Energy. Always pool energy to at least 50 before using this finisher to benefit from the 100% damage bonus, and ensure it is cast only at 5 Combo Points.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Total damage is 31.2% lower despite similar cast counts. **Advice:** Similar to Rip, Rake must be snapshotted with [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217). Additionally, ensure you are utilizing [Sudden Ambush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244483) procs to refresh Rake for higher initial damage.
       - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** Total damage is 93.4% lower (2.48M vs 2.88M). **Advice:** This is a direct consequence of lower uptime on the boss and a lack of [Agility](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=Agility) scaling compared to the reference.
       - **[Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838):** Total damage shows a 304.2% gap. **Advice:** Ensure Feral Frenzy is used on cooldown and within [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) windows. It is a massive source of instant combo points and bleed pressure.
