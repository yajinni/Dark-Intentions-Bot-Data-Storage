### 8. Final Audit Conclusions:
The 30.2K DPS gap between Panthër and the Reference is primarily driven by three factors:

1.  **Talent Selection & Spec Synergy:** The Reference utilized **Feral Frenzy** (13 casts, 4.0M damage), while Panthër did not. Feral Frenzy provides critical combo point generation and a powerful bleed that benefits from Feral's mastery.
2.  **Bleed Snapshotting & Mastery Gap:** Panthër's **Rip** "Per Cast" value is 44% lower than the Reference (268.5K vs 478.4K). While the Reference has 7% more Mastery, the magnitude of the difference suggests Panthër is failing to consistently refresh Rip during **Tiger's Fury** windows. Feral bleeds must be "snapshotted" with damage modifiers to achieve 99th-percentile performance.
3.  **Active Uptime:** Panthër lost 136 Melee hits compared to the Reference. In Feral combat, Melee hits are passive resource generators through Omen of Clarity. Lower melee uptime leads to lower energy pooling and fewer Shred/Ferocious Bite opportunities, as evidenced by Panthër's lower Shred count (75 vs 95).

**Priority Improvement:** Talent into Feral Frenzy and focus on high-priority Tiger's Fury snapshotting for Rip.
