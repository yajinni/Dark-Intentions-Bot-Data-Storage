### 1. Audit Conclusions
   - **Performance Overview:**
       - Panthër's performance was significantly hindered by a combination of high downtime (36.0%), poor resource management, and a failure to properly snapshot bleeds. 
       - While core ability usage was present, the frequency of primary generators like [Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221) was nearly 35% lower than the reference, leading to fewer finisher opportunities and lower overall pressure.
       - Resource management was a major concern; 10% of [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) casts and 12% of [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) casts were performed at sub-optimal Combo Point counts, which is a critical throughput error for Feral.
   - **Casting Optimizations:**
       - **[Shred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5221):** Casts (72 Player vs 110 Ref) - **Advice:** Missing 38 casts indicates significant downtime or excessive pooling. Shred is your primary generator; ensure you are filling GCDs when not pooling for specific snapshot windows or 50 Energy [Ferocious Bite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=22568) windows.
       - **[Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822):** Per Cast (108.4K Player vs 169.6K Ref) - **Advice:** A 36% difference in per-cast value indicates poor [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) alignment. Always ensure [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) is active before refreshing [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822) to benefit from snapshotting. The massive gap is also exacerbated by the Player's significantly lower Mastery (21.54% vs 42.87%).
       - **[Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079):** Per Cast (245.9K Player vs 301.7K Ref) - **Advice:** Similar to [Rake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1822), the lower per-tick value stems from missing [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) snapshotting and the 21.3% Mastery deficit. Focus on only casting [Rip](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1079) at 5 Combo Points during a [Tiger's Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5217) window.
       - **[Feral Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=274838):** Total Damage (3.0M Player vs 4.4M Ref) - **Advice:** Missing 2 full casts over the encounter. Ensure this is used on cooldown to maximize the "Unseen Predator" and bleed amplification windows provided by the Wildstalker hero spec.
       - **[Melee](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1):** Casts (395 Player vs 510 Ref) - **Advice:** A difference of 115 swings (22%) confirms poor uptime on the boss. The reference did a significantly better job of sticking to the target during mechanics.
