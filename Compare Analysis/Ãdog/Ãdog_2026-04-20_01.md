**Key Recommendations:**

2. **Minimize GCD Latency:** Reduce downtime (currently at 19.2%) by queueing spells more effectively and using **Hover** to eliminate movement-based pauses in casting.
3. **Deep Breath Alignment:** Ensure Deep Breath is hitting the maximum number of targets possible, especially during add phases, to capitalize on its high damage ceiling.
4. **Empowered Extensions:** Prioritize getting more Fire Breath and Eternity Surge casts inside the Dragonrage window to maximize its duration.
