### 5. Final Audit Conclusions:
The primary performance gap between Faerocity and the Reference stems from **Barbed Shot efficiency** and **Filler Priority**. 

1.  **Barbed Shot Scaling:** The Reference's Barbed Shot value is nearly triple that of the Player. This suggests the Reference is much more effective at maintaining 3 stacks of the Frenzy pet buff and aligning Barbed Shot refreshes with the Bloodshed window. The Player is likely over-capping charges or letting stacks drop entirely.
2.  **Filler Management:** The Player cast Cobra Shot 29 times compared to the Reference’s 43. In Beast Mastery, Cobra Shot reduces the cooldown of Kill Command. By neglecting this filler, the Player experienced higher downtime and fewer Kill Command opportunities during buff windows.
3.  **Missing Throughput:** The Reference benefitted from **Huntmaster's Call**, accounting for over 800k damage. The Player must review their talent selection to ensure they are not opting out of high-performing passive throughput talents.
4.  **Haste Thresholds:** The Reference’s 15.68% Haste allows for a smoother rotation. The Player should look to transition some Crit rating into Haste to narrow the GCD gap and increase the frequency of auto-shot procs for Barbed Shot resets.
