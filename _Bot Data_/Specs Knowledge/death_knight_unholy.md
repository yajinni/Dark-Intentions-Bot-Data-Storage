# Unholy Death Knight Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Unholy Death Knight is a pet-centric DPS spec that focuses on spreading diseases and managing **Festering Wounds**. It excels in sustained multi-target damage and has powerful 45-second and 3-minute burst windows. The spec involves coordinating your own attacks with those of your permanent Ghoul and various temporary minions.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
The "Easy Mode" for Unholy Death Knight simplifies the complex interaction between Festering Wounds, Runic Power, and pet management.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Virulent Plague:** Your primary disease. Keep it active on all targets at all times.
*   **Festering Wounds:** Applied by Festering Strike and consumed by Scourge Strike for burst damage.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Outbreak:** Apply Virulent Plague.
2.  **Dark Transformation:** Use on cooldown to empower your Ghoul.
3.  **Festering Strike:** Use to apply 2-3 Festering Wounds if the target has none.
4.  **Scourge Strike:** Use to consume Festering Wounds.
5.  **Death Coil:** Use to spend Runic Power and trigger **Sudden Doom** procs.
6.  **Soul Reaper:** Use as a filler when the target is below 35% health.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Outbreak:** Maintain 100% uptime on Virulent Plague.
2.  **Festering Scythe:** Use whenever the debuff is missing from the target.
3.  **Soul Reaper:** High priority if target is < 35% HP.
4.  **Death Coil:** Use if **Sudden Doom** is active or Runic Power is > 80.
5.  **Putrefy:** Use when target is > 35% HP during Dark Transformation.
6.  **Festering Strike:** Build Lesser Ghoul stacks (up to 8, but keep at least 3).
7.  **Scourge Strike:** Consume Lesser Ghoul stacks.
8.  **Death Coil:** Filler to spend Runic Power.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Outbreak:** Apply and spread Virulent Plague.
2.  **Festering Scythe:** Apply debuff to multiple targets.
3.  **Putrefy:** Use for burst shadow damage and plague application.
4.  **Epidemic:** Primary Runic Power spender for 3+ targets.
5.  **Death and Decay:** Stand in it to allow **Scourge Strike** to cleave.
6.  **Scourge Strike:** Cleave while inside Death and Decay.
7.  **Festering Strike:** Build stacks on priority targets.

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Dark Transformation:** 45-second cooldown. Empowers your Ghoul and increases your shadow damage. Triggers a free **Soul Reaper**. Use on cooldown.
*   **Army of the Dead:** 1.5-minute effective cycle (with talent CDR). Your primary burst cooldown. Align with every other Dark Transformation.
*   **Putrefy:** 45-second cooldown. Core part of your burst window; deals shadow damage and ensures disease uptime.
*   **Blightfall:** Replaces Outbreak during Dark Transformation (if talented). Consumes plumes for massive burst AoE.
*   **Abomination Limb:** 2-minute cooldown. Pulls enemies in and applies Festering Wounds. Great for setup and AoE burst.
*   **Summon Gargoyle:** 3-minute cooldown. Powerful single-target burst. Best used when you have high Runic Power generation.

## Stat Priority
1.  **Strength**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases Shadow and minion damage. Crucial for scaling.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Synergizes with Mastery and increases minion/disease crit damage.
4.  **Haste:** Increases Rune regen and pet attack speed.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Unholy, Mastery and Crit are often very close in value, but Mastery scales better with more targets and minion-heavy builds.
