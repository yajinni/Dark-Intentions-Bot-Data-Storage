## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Shado-Pan** *(Recommended)*
*The dominant hero tree for almost all content.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Flurry of Blows** — Energy spending builds stacks that trigger a massive burst of physical damage.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Prioritize spending Energy quickly to trigger Flurry windows. Blackout Kick becomes a high-priority spender even at low stacks.

### **Conduit of the Celestials** *(Alternative)*
*Situational alternative focusing on burst windows.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Empowers your primary cooldowns with celestial assistance.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Coordinate primary cooldowns (Serenity/Storm, Earth, and Fire) with celestial procs.

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target**
1.  **Touch of Death:** Use on low health targets for instant kill or massive damage.
2.  **Whirling Dragon Punch:** Use whenever available (requires Fists of Fury and Rising Sun Kick to be on CD).
3.  **Strike of the Windlord:** High damage burst; use on cooldown.
4.  **Fists of Fury:** Primary channel; do not clip unless necessary.
5.  **Rushing Wind Kick:** Use on cooldown (replaces Spinning Crane Kick in ST).
6.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Primary damage builder.
7.  **Blackout Kick:** Use on procs or as a filler.

### **Multi-Target (3+)**
1.  **Touch of Death:** High priority to eliminate priority targets.
2.  **Whirling Dragon Punch:** Primary AoE burst.
3.  **Fists of Fury:** High cleave damage.
4.  **Rushing Wind Kick:** Strong AoE filler.
5.  **Spinning Crane Kick:** Use during **Zenith** or **Dance of Chi-Ji** procs.
