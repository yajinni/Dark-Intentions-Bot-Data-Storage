# Windwalker Monk Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Windwalker Monk is a fast-paced melee DPS spec that revolves around its unique Mastery, **Combo Strikes**, which prevents you from repeating the same ability twice in a row for a significant damage bonus. The gameplay involves managing **Energy** to generate **Chi**, and then spending that Chi on high-impact abilities like **Fists of Fury** and **Rising Sun Kick**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Windwalker is all about variety. If you press the same button twice, you lose a massive amount of damage.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Combo Strikes (Mastery):** Your abilities deal more damage if they are not a repeat of the previous ability.
*   **Mark of the Crane:** Tiger Palm, Blackout Kick, and Rising Sun Kick apply a mark to targets. Your **Spinning Crane Kick** damage increases for every unique target with a mark (up to 5).
*   **Chi & Energy:** Energy is for Tiger Palm; Chi is for almost everything else.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Don't Repeat Abilities:** Always alternate your buttons.
2.  **Touch of Death:** Use as an execute (< 15% HP).
3.  **Strike of the Windlord:** Use on cooldown.
4.  **Fists of Fury:** Primary channeled burst. Use on cooldown.
5.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Use on cooldown.
6.  **Whirling Dragon Punch:** Use whenever it's available (requires FoF and RSK to be on CD).
7.  **Tiger Palm:** Use to generate Chi (don't cap at 5).
8.  **Blackout Kick:** Primary filler to spend Chi and maintain Combo Strikes.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Invoke Xuen, the White Tiger:** Use on cooldown.
2.  **Strike of the Windlord:** Highest priority spender.
3.  **Whirling Dragon Punch:** Use as soon as FoF and RSK are on cooldown.
4.  **Fists of Fury:** Essential channeled damage.
5.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Primary single-target burst.
6.  **Rushing Wind Kick:** (If talented) Use for supplemental damage.
7.  **Blackout Kick:** Use as a filler to spend Chi.
8.  **Tiger Palm:** Use to generate Chi.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Strike of the Windlord:** Massive AoE burst.
2.  **Fists of Fury:** Your most important AoE ability.
3.  **Whirling Dragon Punch:** High AoE burst.
4.  **Spinning Crane Kick:** Use with 3+ targets and multiple **Mark of the Crane** stacks.
5.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Use to apply Mark of the Crane and enable WDP.
6.  **Tiger Palm:** Use to spread Mark of the Crane.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Invoke Xuen, the White Tiger:** 2-minute cooldown. Summons Xuen for 20 seconds. **Usage:** Your main burst window.
*   **Strike of the Windlord:** 45-second cooldown. Heavy-hitting burst that applies a movement slow. **Usage:** Use on cooldown.
*   **Serenity / Storm, Earth, and Fire:** (Depending on talents) Increases damage and reduces Chi costs. **Usage:** Use for major burst phases.
*   **Touch of Karma:** 1.5-minute cooldown. Redirects damage back to the attacker. **Usage:** Use defensively to survive or offensively to gain a tiny damage boost.
*   **Fortifying Brew:** 2-minute cooldown. 15% health and damage reduction. **Usage:** Primary defensive.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Agility**
2.  **Haste:** Increases attack speed and reduces core ability cooldowns.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Highly valuable for all damage.
4.  **Mastery:** Directly increases the damage bonus from Combo Strikes.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Windwalker, **Haste** is increasingly important in the current expansion for reducing the cooldown of Fists of Fury and Rising Sun Kick.
