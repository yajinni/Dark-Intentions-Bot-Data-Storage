## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Templar** *(Recommended)*
*Focuses on high burst and synergy with Wake of Ashes.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Hammer of Light** — Replaces Final Verdict after casting Wake of Ashes.
*   **Rotational Shift:** High priority on Hammer of Light during the Wake of Ashes window.

### **Herald of the Sun** *(Alternative)*
*Focuses on steady damage and synergy with Holy Power spenders.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Dawnlight** — Applies a damage dot to targets when you cast Final Verdict or Divine Storm.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Higher priority on maintaining Holy Power spenders to maximize Dawnlight uptime.

---

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target**
1.  **Execution Sentence:** Open the window (requires 3 Holy Power).
2.  **Wake of Ashes:** High damage builder; use on cooldown.
3.  **Hammer of Light:** Use immediately after Wake of Ashes (Templar).
4.  **Final Verdict:** Primary Holy Power spender.
5.  **Blade of Justice:** Primary builder.
6.  **Judgment:** Builder and debuff application.
7.  **Hammer of Wrath:** Use on low health targets or during Avenging Wrath.

### **AoE / Multi-Target**
1.  **Wake of Ashes:** Primary AoE burst and builder.
2.  **Hammer of Light:** Primary AoE burst.
3.  **Divine Storm:** Primary Holy Power spender for 2+ targets.
4.  **Divine Toll:** Massive builder for AoE windows.
5.  **Final Reckoning:** (If talented) Use before Wake of Ashes for massive burst.

## 5. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **REWORK:** Hammer of Light is now the core burst mechanic for Retribution Paladins.
*   **REMOVED:** Radiant Spark — Fully removed.
*   **REMOVED:** Seraphim — Fully removed.
*   **ADDED:** Dawnlight Synergy — New interaction with Herald of the Sun.
*   **ADDED:** Execution Sentence — Now functions as a 30-second burst window activator.
