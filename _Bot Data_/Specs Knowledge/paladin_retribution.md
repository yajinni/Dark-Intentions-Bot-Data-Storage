## 3. Hero Talents Deep Dive

### **Templar**
*   **Core Focus:** **Hammer of Light** & **Shake the Heavens**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Introduces a massive burst window triggered by *Wake of Ashes*. Replaces *Hammer of Wrath* with *Hammer of Light* (a heavy AoE/ST spender).
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for pure single-target burst and priority target damage in 12.0.5.

### **Herald of the Sun**
*   **Core Mechanic:** **Dawnlight** & **Solar Ray**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Adds a massive radiance DoT to your *Wake of Ashes* and *Final Verdict*. Focuses on consistent throughput and area damage.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for consistent multi-target cleave and target-rich encounters.

## 4. Deep Dive: Raid Rotation
Focus: **Execution Sentence Window** and **Holy Power Efficiency**.

### **A. The Burst Audit (The Execution Window)**
1.  **Execution Sentence:** Use on cooldown (ensure you have at least 3 Holy Power available).
2.  **Wake of Ashes:** Cast immediately after Execution Sentence.
3.  **Hammer of Light (Templar):** Use all available procs within the Execution window.
4.  **Final Verdict:** Primary Holy Power spender. 
5.  **Audit Flag:** Casting **Wake of Ashes** BEFORE **Execution Sentence** is a major throughput error.

### **B. Holy Power Management**
1.  **Final Verdict:** Spend at **4-5 Holy Power**.
2.  **Blade of Justice:** Use on cooldown (triggered by *Art of War*).
3.  **Judgment:** Use on cooldown (applies *Judgment of Light*).
4.  **Hammer of Wrath:** Use on cooldown (during Avenging Wrath or on targets < 20% HP).
5.  **Audit Flag:** Casting *Blade of Justice* or *Judgment* when at **5 Holy Power** is a catastrophic resource waste.

## 5. Major Cooldowns Breakdown (Strict Whitelist)
*   **Avenging Wrath (Crusade):** 2-minute CD. Increases damage and Haste.
*   **Wake of Ashes:** 45s CD. Generates 3 Holy Power and triggers Hero Talent procs.
*   **Execution Sentence:** 30s CD. Increases damage taken by the target.
*   **Divine Toll:** 1-minute CD. Instant high threat/damage and resource generator.
*   **Shield of Vengeance:** Primary defensive absorb.
