## 2. Apex Talent: Master the Darkness
This is the primary 12.0.5 proc to audit.
*   **Mechanism:** Penance casts have a chance to upgrade your next *Power Word: Shield* into a **Void Shield**.
*   **Void Shield:** Absorbs more damage, reflects damage back (Atonement healing), and applies Atonement to 2 additional nearby targets.
*   **Audit Logic:** Always consume the Void Shield proc before your next Penance cast to avoid wasting charges.

## 3. Hero Spec Mechanics (Raid)
### **Voidweaver** (Entropic Rift Focus)
*   **Core Mechanic:** *Mind Blast* summons an **Entropic Rift**.
*   **Void Blast:** During the Rift, Smite is upgraded to Void Blast (massive Atonement healing).
*   **Audit Priority:** Ensure *Void Blast* is used as the primary filler during Rift windows.

### **Oracle** (Pet & Defensive Penance)
*   **Binding Heals:** Flash Heal applies Atonement to both the target and the Priest.
*   **Audit Priority:** Use Penance defensively (on allies) to maximize Atonement uptime and benefit from Twinsight bolts.

## 4. Definitive Raid Ramp Sequences (Audit Ready)

### **Voidweaver Evangelism Ramp**
1.  **Shadow Word: Pain:** Refresh.
2.  **Penance:** Used on an ally (Atonement setup).
3.  **Power Word: Shield / Plea / Flash Heal:** Quick Atonement spread.
4.  **Evangelism:** Extension window.
5.  **Power Word: Radiance:** Large group coverage.
6.  **Mind Blast:** Summons **Entropic Rift**.
7.  **Shadow Word: Death:** Trigger *Expiation*.
8.  **Power Word: Radiance:** Final Atonement coverage.
9.  **Penance:** Used on an enemy (Voidheart bonus).
10. **Void Blast:** Primary filler during Rift.

### **Oracle Evangelism Ramp**
1.  **Shadow Word: Pain:** Refresh.
2.  **Penance:** Used on an ally.
3.  **Power Word: Shield / Plea / Flash Heal:** Setup.
4.  **Evangelism:** Extension.
5.  **Power Word: Radiance x2:** Full raid coverage.
6.  **Shadow Word: Death:** Trigger *Inescapable Torment*.
7.  **Penance:** Used on an ally.
8.  **Mind Blast:** Burst.
9.  **Smite:** Filler.

## 5. Major Cooldown Execution
*   **Ultimate Penitence:** Cast offensively (at enemies) for maximum Atonement throughput. You can cast an instant spell (like SW:Death) while traveling into the air without canceling the channel.
*   **Power Word: Barrier:** Niche alternative to Ultimate Penitence for heavy stack damage.
*   **Pain Suppression:** Use proactively on tanks; talented into *Protector of the Frail* for high uptime.

## 6. Audit Cleanup (12.0.5 Removals)
*   **DO NOT AUDIT:** Symbol of Hope, Power Word: Life, Lightwell, and baseline Power Word: Shield (unless talented into Void Shield/Master the Darkness).
