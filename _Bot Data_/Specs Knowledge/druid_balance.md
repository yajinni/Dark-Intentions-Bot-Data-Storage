# Balance Druid Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Balance Druid (Boomkin) is a ranged DPS spec that focuses on the **Eclipse** mechanic. You cycle between Solar and Lunar Eclipses to empower your nature and arcane spells. The goal is to maximize the time spent in Eclipse while managing **Astral Power** to cast powerful finishers like **Starsurge** and **Starfall**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Balance Druid gameplay revolves around a predictable "cycle". You enter an Eclipse by casting the opposite spell twice.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Solar Eclipse:** Best for 1-3 targets. Empowered spells: **Wrath**.
*   **Lunar Eclipse:** Best for 4+ targets. Empowered spells: **Starfire**.
*   **Astral Power:** Generated by fillers (Wrath/Starfire) and DoTs; spent on finishers (Starsurge/Starfall).

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Maintain DoTs:** Always keep **Sunfire** and **Moonfire** active on all targets.
2.  **Enter Eclipse:** 
    *   Cast **Starfire** x2 to enter **Solar Eclipse** (for Single Target).
    *   Cast **Wrath** x2 to enter **Lunar Eclipse** (for AoE).
3.  **Spend Astral Power:**
    *   Use **Starsurge** for single target (do not cap at 100).
    *   Use **Starfall** for 2+ targets (maintain uptime).
4.  **Filler:** 
    *   Cast **Wrath** during Solar Eclipse.
    *   Cast **Starfire** during Lunar Eclipse.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Sunfire / Moonfire:** Maintain 100% uptime.
2.  **Force of Nature / Fury of Elune:** Use on cooldown for Astral Power generation.
3.  **Enter Solar Eclipse:** Use Starfire x2.
4.  **Starsurge:** Use to spend Astral Power. Avoid capping at 100.
5.  **Wrath:** Main filler during Solar Eclipse.
6.  **New Moon / Half Moon / Full Moon:** If talented, use to generate massive Astral Power; avoid sitting at max charges.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Sunfire:** Apply to the entire pack.
2.  **Moonfire:** Apply to high-health/priority targets.
3.  **Wild Mushroom:** Use on cooldown for burst AoE and Astral Power.
4.  **Enter Lunar Eclipse:** Use Wrath x2.
5.  **Starfall:** Maintain 100% uptime when 2+ targets are present.
6.  **Starfire:** Main filler during Lunar Eclipse (cleaves).

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Celestial Alignment / Incarnation: Chosen of Elune:** 3-minute cooldown. Triggers both Eclipses and increases haste/damage. **Usage:** Your primary burst window. Always use on cooldown or for priority phases.
*   **Convoke the Spirits:** 1-minute or 2-minute cooldown (depending on talents). Fires 16 spells in 4 seconds. **Usage:** Use inside Celestial Alignment for maximum burst.
*   **Fury of Elune:** 1-minute cooldown. Massive Astral Power generation and AoE damage. **Usage:** Sync with your burst windows or use for high-movement phases to keep generating resources.
*   **Warrior of Elune:** 45-second cooldown. Makes your next 3 Starfires instant. **Usage:** Use for burst AoE or to move without losing DPS.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Haste:** Faster casts and more Astral Power generation.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Highly valuable due to **Ascendant Eclipses** synergy.
4.  **Mastery:** Increases damage of spells while in Eclipse.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Balance, **Haste** and **Crit** are currently the top secondary stats, with Mastery close behind.
