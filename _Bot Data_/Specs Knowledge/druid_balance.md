## 2. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Elune's Chosen**
*Focuses on Lunar power, Starfire, and Fury of Elune uptime.*
*   **Rotational Shift:** **Starfire** becomes the primary filler even in single-target situations to maximize Lunar power generation.
*   **Key Combo:** Pair **Fury of Elune** with every **Celestial Alignment** window.

### **Keeper of the Grove**
*Focuses on Force of Nature summons and Solar power.*
*   **Rotational Shift:** **Wrath** remains the primary filler. 
*   **Key Combo:** Use **Force of Nature** immediately before entering **Celestial Alignment** or Solar Eclipse to maximize the buff window.

## 3. Full Rotational Priority

### **The Opener**
1.  **Pre-pull:** Cast 2 **Wraths** or **Starfires** (depending on desired starting Eclipse).
2.  **Pull:** Apply **Sunfire** and **Moonfire**.
3.  **Cooldowns:** **Celestial Alignment** + **Potion** + **Trinkets**.
4.  **Hero Power:** **Force of Nature** (Keeper) or **Fury of Elune** (Elune).
5.  **Spend:** **Starsurge** to dump Astral Power and stack **Starlord**.

### **Single-Target Priority**
1.  **Maintain DoTs:** Keep **Sunfire**, **Moonfire**, and **Stellar Flare** active.
2.  **Spend:** **Starsurge** when at 80+ Astral Power or to consume **Touch the Cosmos** procs.
3.  **Celestial Alignment:** Use on cooldown.
4.  **Enter Eclipse:**
    *   **Keeper:** Use **Wrath** to enter Solar Eclipse.
    *   **Elune:** Use **Starfire** to enter Lunar Eclipse.
5.  **Filler:**
    *   **Keeper:** **Wrath**.
    *   **Elune:** **Starfire**.

## 4. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **REMOVED: Starweaver** — No longer audit for Starfall/Starsurge weaving procs.
*   **SIMPLIFIED: Eclipse** — Entering Eclipse now only requires a single cast of the corresponding filler (**Wrath** for Solar, **Starfire** for Lunar).
*   **ADDED: Apex Talent: Ascendant Eclipses** — Check for instant filler procs upon entering Eclipse.
*   **REMOVED: Adaptive Swarm** — This is no longer part of the standard Balance toolkit in Midnight.
