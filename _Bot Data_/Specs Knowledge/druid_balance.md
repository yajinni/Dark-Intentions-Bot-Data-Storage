## 3. Hero Talents Deep Dive

### **Keeper of the Grove**
*   **Core Focus:** **Force of Nature** & **Dream Burst**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Introduces a burst window centered around your treants. Requires casting *Force of Nature* immediately before *Celestial Alignment*.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for priority target damage and burst windows.

### **Elune's Chosen**
*   **Core Focus:** **Lunar Calling** & **Starfire**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Makes *Starfire* your primary filler during Eclipse. Focuses on Arcane damage amplification.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for consistent multi-target cleave and Arcane-heavy encounters.

## 4. Deep Dive: Raid Rotation
Focus: **Eclipse Uptime** and **Resource Efficiency**.

### **A. Eclipse Management (The Entry Audit)**
1.  **Solar Eclipse (ST):** Cast **Wrath x2** to enter. Use Wrath as filler.
2.  **Lunar Eclipse (AoE):** Cast **Starfire x2** to enter. Use Starfire as filler.
3.  **Audit Flag:** Entering the wrong Eclipse for the target count (e.g., Solar for 3+ targets) is a major efficiency error.

### **B. Spending Logic**
1.  **Starsurge:** Use for single-target spending (1-2 targets).
2.  **Starfall:** Use for multi-target spending (**3+ targets**).
3.  **Audit Flag:** Casting *Starfall* on a single target is a catastrophic resource waste error.

### **C. Maintenance**
1.  **Sunfire / Moonfire / Stellar Flare:** Maintain 100% uptime on all DoTs.
2.  **Fury of Elune:** Use on cooldown (align with Celestial Alignment).

## 5. Major Cooldowns Breakdown (Strict Whitelist)
*   **Celestial Alignment / Incarnation:** 3-minute CD. Major burst window.
*   **Fury of Elune:** 1-minute CD. Massive Astral Power generator.
*   **Force of Nature:** Used to generate Astral Power and trigger *Keeper* buffs.
*   **Warrior of Elune:** Used for instant Starfire casts during high-movement windows.
