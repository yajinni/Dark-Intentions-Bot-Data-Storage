*   **General Priority:** Intellect > Mastery > Critical Strike > Haste > Versatility.
*   *Note: Stat weights are very close; Mastery and Crit are the primary focuses for raid damage.*

## 3. Hero Talents Deep Dive

### **Elune's Chosen**
*   **Core Focus:** **Lunar Calling** & **The Boundless Moonlight**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Significantly enhances *Starfire* and *New Moon*. Focuses on high-intensity astral power generation and burst.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for single-target boss encounters and consistent throughput in 12.0.5.

### **Keeper of the Grove**
*   **Core Mechanic:** **Dreamsurge** & **Force of Nature**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Introduces *Dreamsurge* procs from your *Force of Nature* (Treants). Focuses on summoning and periodic high-impact windows.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for encounters requiring periodic high-burst utility and multi-target stabilization.

## 4. Deep Dive: Raid Rotation
Focus: **Eclipse Uptime** and **Astral Power Management**.

### **A. The Eclipse Audit (The 100% Rule)**
1.  **Rule:** Maintain **100% uptime** on Eclipse (Solar or Lunar) while in combat.
2.  **Audit Flag:** Casting **Wrath** or **Starfire** while outside of an Eclipse window (and Eclipse is available to enter) is a catastrophic failure.

### **B. Primary Priority List (Strict Whitelist)**
1.  **Starsurge:** Primary Astral Power spender for single-target. Use to maintain high damage windows.
2.  **Sunfire / Moonfire:** Maintain 100% uptime on the primary target.
3.  **Wrath:** Primary Solar Eclipse generator.
4.  **Starfire:** Primary Lunar Eclipse generator.
5.  **Audit Flag:** Casting *Starsurge* at **100 Astral Power** is a major resource waste.

---

## 5. Major Cooldowns Breakdown (Strict Whitelist)
*   **Celestial Alignment / Incarnation:** 3-minute CD. Major burst window.
*   **Convoke the Spirits:** 1-minute CD (if talented). Rapid-fire spells.
*   **Force of Nature:** 1-minute CD. Summons Treants (primary Keeper engine).
*   **Warrior of Elune:** 45s CD. Empowers next 3 Starfires to be instant.
*   **Starfall:** Primary AoE Astral Power spender.
