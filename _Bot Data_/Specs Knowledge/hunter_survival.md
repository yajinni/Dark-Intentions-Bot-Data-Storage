## 2. Stat Priority
1. **Mastery** — Increases Wildfire Bomb and melee damage
2. **Critical Strike** — Near-equal to Haste for Pack Leader
3. **Haste** — Reduces cooldowns; higher priority for Pack Leader
4. **Versatility**

*Note: Critical Strike and Haste are interchangeable depending on your hero talent tree.*

---

## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Pack Leader** *(Recommended — Raid & Mythic+)*
*Focuses on coordinated pet attacks and burst through Takedown.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Howl of the Pack Leader** generates free Kill Commands. **Takedown** is a major offensive cooldown providing +20% damage.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Weave Kill Command between melee abilities. Use Takedown with 2+ Tip of the Spear stacks for maximum burst. Boomstick is a new shotgun ability that replaces Flanking Strike as the primary proc spender.

### **Sentinel**
*Focuses on Sentinel Owl lunar damage and Moonlight Chakram.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Sentinel Owls proc during Wildfire Bomb and melee hits, dealing lunar AoE damage.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Maintain Wildfire Bomb on cooldown — it triggers Sentinel procs. Moonlight Chakram is a new cooldown.

---

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target (Pack Leader)**
1.  **Kill Command:** Use to generate **Tip of the Spear** stacks.
2.  **Takedown:** Use on cooldown with 2+ Tip of the Spear stacks.
3.  **Kill Command (Howl proc):** Use free procs immediately.
4.  **Wildfire Bomb:** Use with Tip of the Spear stacks.
5.  **Boomstick:** Use with Tip of the Spear stacks (shotgun proc spender).
6.  **Raptor Strike / Mongoose Bite:** Primary Tip of the Spear consumer.

### **AoE / Mythic+ (Pack Leader)**
1.  **Multi-Shot:** Maintain Beast Cleave if pet is active (2+ targets).
2.  **Wildfire Bomb:** Use on cooldown — primary AoE damage and proc trigger; spend Tip stacks.
3.  **Boomstick:** AoE shotgun ability — use with Tip stacks.
4.  **Kill Command:** Maintain procs.
5.  **Raptor Strike / Flamefang Pitch:** Use Flamefang Pitch for AoE ground DoT on 3+ targets.

---

## 5. Major Cooldowns
*   **Takedown (Pack Leader):** 1.5-minute cooldown. Grants +20% damage for a short window. Use with 2+ Tip of the Spear stacks for maximum burst.
*   **Wildfire Bomb:** Short cooldown — not a traditional "cooldown" but the primary damage and proc-trigger ability. Never delay.
*   **Coordinated Assault:** Major DPS cooldown — increases all damage for 20 seconds. Align with Bloodlust/Heroism.
*   **Moonlight Chakram (Sentinel):** New Sentinel cooldown — throws a lunar chakram through targets.
*   **Stampede (Pack Leader):** Activated through Pack Leader talent during burst windows.

---

## 6. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **NEW:** **Boomstick** — A new shotgun ability that replaces Flanking Strike as the primary proc spender for Pack Leader.
*   **NEW:** **Flamefang Pitch** — New ground AoE DoT ability (Survival spec tree).
*   **CHANGED:** **Takedown** — Reworked into a major offensive cooldown (previously a passive execute modifier).
*   **NEW:** **Raptor Swipe** — New Apex talent version of Raptor Strike for AoE situations.
*   **REMOVED:** **Flanking Strike**, **Mongoose Fury** (stacking buff system), **Serpent Sting** — Do not audit for these legacy abilities.
*   **REMINDER:** Survival is the only melee Hunter spec. Do not audit for ranged-only ability usage.
