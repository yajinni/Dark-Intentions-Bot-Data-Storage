# Marksmanship Hunter Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Marksmanship Hunter is a ranged DPS spec that focuses on high-damage, long-range shots. It is unique in that it often plays without a pet (via **Lone Wolf**) to maximize its own damage. The gameplay revolves around managing **Focus** and **Precise Shots** procs to maximize the value of **Aimed Shot** and **Rapid Fire**, while utilizing **Trick Shots** for heavy AoE burst.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Marksmanship is about setting up your shots and ensuring you don't over-cap Focus while maximizing your burst windows.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Precise Shots:** Aimed Shot and Rapid Fire generate stacks that empower your next Arcane Shots or Multi-Shots.
*   **Trick Shots:** Triggered by hitting 3+ targets with Multi-Shot or using Volley. Makes your next Aimed Shot or Rapid Fire ricochet to additional targets.
*   **Lone Wolf:** Damage increased by 10% when you don't have a pet active.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Aimed Shot:** Use to avoid reaching 2 charges.
2.  **Rapid Fire:** Use on cooldown to generate Focus.
3.  **Arcane Shot:** Use to spend **Precise Shots** stacks (if < 2 targets).
4.  **Multi-Shot:** Use to spend **Precise Shots** stacks (if 2+ targets) and maintain **Trick Shots**.
5.  **Steady Shot:** Use as a filler to generate Focus.
6.  **Trueshot:** Use on cooldown for burst.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Dark Ranger Priority)
1.  **Hunter's Mark:** Maintain on the target.
2.  **Black Arrow:** (If talented) Use on cooldown.
3.  **Rapid Fire:** Use on cooldown.
4.  **Aimed Shot:** Use at 2 charges or to avoid capping.
5.  **Kill Shot:** Use on targets < 20% HP.
6.  **Arcane Shot:** Use to spend **Precise Shots** stacks.
7.  **Steady Shot:** Filler.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Volley:** Use on cooldown for guaranteed **Trick Shots**.
2.  **Multi-Shot:** Use to trigger **Trick Shots** (if Volley is down).
3.  **Rapid Fire:** Use with Trick Shots active.
4.  **Aimed Shot:** Use with Trick Shots active.
5.  **Explosive Shot:** Use on cooldown.
6.  **Deathbind:** (If talented) Use for heavy AoE burst.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Trueshot:** 2-minute cooldown. Increases Haste by 20% and reduces the cooldown of Aimed Shot and Rapid Fire by 60%. **Usage:** Your main burst. Use with Bloodlust or during high-priority phases.
*   **Volley:** 45-second cooldown. Deals AoE damage and grants Trick Shots for 6 seconds. **Usage:** Essential for starting your AoE burst.
*   **Salvo:** 45-second cooldown (passive/active). Enhances your next Multi-Shot or Volley to apply Explosive Shots. **Usage:** Sync with Volley for massive AoE burst.
*   **Exhilaration:** 2-minute cooldown. Self-heal.
*   **Aspect of the Turtle:** 3-minute cooldown. Defensive immunity.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Critical Strike:** Increases the damage of all shots and is required for many talent synergies.
2.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage and range of all abilities.
3.  **Versatility:** Flat damage increase and reduction.
4.  **Haste:** Generally lower priority, but reduces cast times and Focus regen.

**Note:** For Marksmanship, **Crit** and **Mastery** are the two most important stats for maximizing burst potential.
