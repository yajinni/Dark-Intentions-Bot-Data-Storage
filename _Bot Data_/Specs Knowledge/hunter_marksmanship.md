## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Sentinel** *(Recommended — Raid)*
*Focuses on lunar damage through Sentinel Owls and Moonlight Chakram.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Lunar Storm** procs chain lightning from your target, amplified during Trueshot.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Trueshot now synergizes with Sentinel proc windows — align Aimed Shots with Lunar procs. **Moonlight Chakram** is a new major cooldown.

### **Dark Ranger** *(Recommended — Mythic+)*
*Focuses on Black Arrow shadow DoT and sustained single-target pressure.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Black Arrow** applies a stacking DoT enhanced by critical Aimed Shots.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Maintain Black Arrow on the primary target at all times. Critical Aimed Shots extend and amplify the DoT.

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target (Sentinel)**
1.  **Trueshot:** Use on cooldown — primary DPS cooldown; aligns with Sentinel proc windows.
2.  **Rapid Fire:** Use on cooldown.
3.  **Moonlight Chakram (Sentinel):** Use on cooldown during Trueshot.
4.  **Aimed Shot:** Use to avoid capping 2 charges; high priority during Trueshot.
5.  **Kill Shot:** Use when target is below 20% HP.
6.  **Arcane Shot:** Use with **Precise Shots** buff (2 charges).
7.  **Steady Shot:** Filler — use when nothing else is available.

### **Single-Target (Dark Ranger)**
1.  **Trueshot:** On cooldown.
2.  **Black Arrow:** On cooldown — maintain DoT.
3.  **Rapid Fire:** On cooldown.
4.  **Aimed Shot:** Do not cap charges.
5.  **Kill Shot:** Below 20% HP.
6.  **Arcane Shot (Precise Shots):** Spend buff stacks.
7.  **Steady Shot:** Filler.

### **AoE / Mythic+**
1.  **Trueshot:** On cooldown — buffs entire AoE window.
2.  **Multi-Shot:** Maintain **Trick Shots** buff (must be active for AoE Aimed Shot cleave).
3.  **Rapid Fire:** On cooldown — cleaves through Trick Shots.
4.  **Aimed Shot:** Use while Trick Shots is active — cleaves to all nearby targets.
5.  **Multi-Shot:** Spend **Precise Shots** stacks with Multi-Shot on 3+ targets.

---

## 5. Major Cooldowns
*   **Trueshot:** 2-minute cooldown. Increases Aimed Shot and Rapid Fire damage. The primary damage window — align with Bloodlust and trinkets.
*   **Rapid Fire:** Short cooldown. Use on cooldown; critical inside Trueshot windows.
*   **Moonlight Chakram (Sentinel):** New ability — throws a lunar chakram dealing heavy damage; use during Trueshot.
*   **Black Arrow (Dark Ranger):** Short cooldown DoT — maintain on target always.

## 6. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **NEW:** **Black Arrow** — New shadow DoT for Dark Ranger hero talent tree.
*   **NEW:** **Take Aim** — New Apex talent increasing Aimed Shot damage after Steady Shot.
*   **NEW:** **Moonlight Chakram** — Sentinel hero talent cooldown.
*   **CHANGED:** **Trueshot** — Updated with Sentinel proc synergy for lunar damage amplification.
*   **CHANGED:** **Rapid Fire** — Now generates significantly more Focus per cast.
*   **REMOVED:** **Volley**, **Explosive Shot**, **Serpent Sting** — Do not audit for these legacy abilities.
