## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Spellslinger** *(Recommended)*
*Focuses on generating and detonating Splinters via Ice Lance.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Ice Lance and Flurry generate Splinters.
*   **Rotational Shift:** High emphasis on Ice Lance spam to maintain Splinter counts.

### **Frostfire** *(Alternative)*
*Focuses on Frostfire Bolt procs and Glacial Spike integration.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Frostfire Bolt replaces Frostbolt.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Glacial Spike now deals Frostfire damage and scales with both schools.

---

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target (Spellslinger)**
1.  **Flurry:** Use with Brain Freeze (preferably at 2 stacks).
2.  **Glacial Spike:** Use at 5 Icicles when Winter's Chill is active.
3.  **Comet Storm:** Use on cooldown.
4.  **Ice Lance:** Use to consume Fingers of Frost and Shatter stacks.
5.  **Frostbolt:** Filler.

### **AoE / Multi-Target**
1.  **Frozen Orb:** Highest priority; generates Fingers of Frost.
2.  **Blizzard:** Use whenever instant proc is available.
3.  **Ice Lance:** Spend procs; cleaves with Splinters.
4.  **Glacial Spike:** High priority AoE finisher.
