## 3. Hero Talents Deep Dive

### **Herald of the Sun**
*   **Core Focus:** **Dawnlight** & **Eternal Flame**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Introduces *Dawnlight* as a massive raid-wide HoT. Requires precise management of *Holy Shock* to spread radiance damage/healing.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for raid-wide stabilization and overall HPS in 12.0.5.

### **Lightsmith**
*   **Core Mechanic:** **Holy Armaments** & **Avenging Crusader**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Increases the impact of your utility weapons (*Bulwark/Sacred Weapon*). Focuses on high-intensity spot healing windows.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for priority target protection and encounters requiring periodic high-burst utility.

## 4. Deep Dive: Raid Rotation
Focus: **Holy Power Management** and **Glimmer Maintenance**.

### **A. The Holy Power Audit (The "Never Cap" Rule)**
1.  **Rule:** Spend Holy Power immediately at **5 stacks**.
2.  **Audit Flag:** Casting **Holy Shock**, **Judgment**, or **Crusader Strike** while at 5 Holy Power is a catastrophic resource failure.

### **B. Primary Priority List**
1.  **Holy Shock:** Top priority. Use on cooldown to generate Holy Power and maintain *Glimmer of Light*.
2.  **Divine Toll:** Use to instantly apply Glimmer to 5 targets and generate 5 Holy Power.
3.  **Light of Dawn:** Primary Holy Power spender for raid healing.
4.  **Word of Glory / Eternal Flame:** Secondary spender for priority triage.
5.  **Judgment:** Use to generate Holy Power and trigger *Judgment of Light*.
6.  **Audit Flag:** Letting **Glimmer of Light** fall off all targets during combat is a baseline loss error.

## 5. Major Cooldowns Breakdown (Strict Whitelist)
*   **Avenging Wrath:** 2-minute CD. Increases all healing and damage.
*   **Aura Mastery:** 3-minute CD. Major raid-wide damage reduction.
*   **Divine Toll:** 1-minute CD. High-impact burst healing and resource generator.
*   **Blessing of Sacrifice:** Primary external for tanks or low-health allies.
*   **Lay on Hands:** Ultimate emergency heal.
