## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Herald of the Sun** *(Recommended — General)*
*Focuses on high throughput and synergy with Avenging Wrath.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Dawnlight** — Applies a healing/damage dot to targets when you cast Holy Shock or Eternal Flame.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Higher priority on Holy Shock and Eternal Flame to maintain Dawnlight uptime.

### **Lightsmith** *(Recommended — Utility)*
*Focuses on group support and specific burst healing windows.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Sacred Weapon** — Imbues an ally (or yourself) with a holy weapon that provides procs.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Coordinate Sacred Weapon with major damage/healing windows.

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Healing Priority**
1.  **Holy Shock:** Primary builder; use on cooldown.
2.  **Eternal Flame:** Primary Holy Power spender (replaces Word of Glory).
3.  **Light of Dawn:** Use for group healing (spend 3 Holy Power).
4.  **Divine Toll:** Massive group burst; use on cooldown.
5.  **Hammer of Wrath:** Use on low health targets or during Avenging Wrath for Holy Power generation.
6.  **Flash of Light:** Fast spot healing (builder).

### **DPS Priority (Avenging Crusader)**
1.  **Judgment:** Primary builder and debuff application.
2.  **Crusader Strike:** Generates Holy Power.
3.  **Hammer of Wrath:** Use whenever available.
4.  **Holy Shock:** Use on targets to build Holy Power.

## 5. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **REWORK:** Eternal Flame is now the core Holy Power spender, replacing Word of Glory in almost all builds.
*   **REMOVED:** Holy Prism (Base) — Moved into specific talent branches.
*   **ADDED:** Dawnlight Synergy — New interaction with Herald of the Sun.
*   **ADDED:** Sacred Weapon — New utility mechanic for Lightsmith builds.
