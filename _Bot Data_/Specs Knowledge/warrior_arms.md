# Arms Warrior Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Arms Warrior is a methodical, heavy-hitting melee DPS spec that focuses on managing **Rage** and maximizing the value of its primary burst window, **Colossus Smash**. The spec is defined by its strong sustained damage and explosive Execute phase. The gameplay revolves around a strict priority system focused on maintaining **Rend** and using **Overpower** to buff powerful finishers like **Mortal Strike**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Arms is about staying active and ensuring you always have active combat buffs while maximizing your Rage efficiency.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Colossus Smash:** Increases damage taken by the target by 30% for 10 seconds. This is your primary burst window.
*   **Overpower:** Buffs the damage of your next Mortal Strike or Cleave.
*   **Sudden Death:** Procs that make your next **Execute** free and usable on any target.
*   **Sweeping Strikes:** Causes your single-target attacks to hit an additional target for 15 seconds.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Avatar:** Use on cooldown (align with Colossus Smash).
2.  **Colossus Smash:** Your most important cooldown. Use on pull and every 45s.
3.  **Mortal Strike:** Spend Rage on this whenever available.
4.  **Execute:** Use on Sudden Death procs or if the target is < 35% health.
5.  **Overpower:** Use to generate procs and buff Mortal Strike.
6.  **Rend:** Maintain 100% uptime on your target.
7.  **Slam:** Primary filler to spend excess Rage.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Avatar:** Sync with Colossus Smash.
2.  **Colossus Smash:** Primary burst window.
3.  **Mortal Strike:** Highest priority spender.
4.  **Execute:** Use with Sudden Death or in execute range.
5.  **Overpower:** Use to avoid capping charges.
6.  **Thunder Clap:** (If using Colossus Hero Talents) High priority generator.
7.  **Slam:** filler.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Sweeping Strikes:** Must be active for 2 targets.
2.  **Warbreaker:** Use instead of Colossus Smash for AoE.
3.  **Bladestorm / Ravager:** Massive AoE burst during Warbreaker.
4.  **Cleave:** Primary spender for 3+ targets.
5.  **Overpower:** Buffs Cleave.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Colossus Smash / Warbreaker:** 45-second cooldown. Increases damage taken by the target. **Usage:** The core of the spec. Always pool Rage and use your hardest-hitting abilities during this window.
*   **Avatar:** 1.5-minute or 2-minute cooldown. 20% damage increase. **Usage:** Sync with every second Colossus Smash.
*   **Bladestorm / Ravager:** 1.5-minute cooldown. High-damage AoE. **Usage:** In single-target, use during Colossus Smash for burst.
*   **Demolish:** (Colossus Hero Talent) Massive multi-strike ability. **Usage:** Highest priority during Colossus Smash.
*   **Shield Wall:** 3-minute or 4-minute cooldown. 40% damage reduction. **Usage:** Major defensive.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Strength**
2.  **Critical Strike:** Critical for massive Mortal Strike and Execute damage.
3.  **Haste:** Essential for reducing the GCD and increasing Rage generation.
4.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of your deep wounds and other periodic effects.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Arms, **Crit** and **Haste** are the two stats that define the spec's pace and burst potential.
