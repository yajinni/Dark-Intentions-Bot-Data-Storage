# Protection Warrior Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Protection Warrior is a high-mobility, physical-damage-mitigation specialist. The spec revolves around generating **Rage** through attacks and spending it on **Shield Block** and **Ignore Pain**. It excels at smoothing out incoming physical damage and provides significant utility through **Spell Reflection** and **Shockwave**. The gameplay is a fast-paced "active mitigation" cycle.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Protection is about maintaining your core defenses and ensuring you always have enough Rage to react to incoming damage.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Shield Block:** Your primary active mitigation. Keep this active as much as possible (ideally 100% of the time when tanking physical damage).
*   **Ignore Pain:** A damage absorption shield. Spend excess Rage on this to smooth out your health spikes.
*   **Revenge:** Spend Rage on this for damage and threat (especially free procs).
*   **Shield Slam:** Your primary Rage generator. Use on cooldown.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Shield Block:** Maintain 100% uptime when taking physical damage.
2.  **Ignore Pain:** Use to avoid capping Rage or to mitigate large hits.
3.  **Avatar:** Use on cooldown for damage and Rage generation.
4.  **Demoralizing Shout:** Use on cooldown (with Booming Voice for Rage).
5.  **Shield Slam:** Highest priority Rage generator.
6.  **Thunder Clap:** Use to generate threat and Rage (especially in AoE).
7.  **Shield Charge:** Use on cooldown for burst Rage and damage.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Mountain Thane Priority)
1.  **Avatar:** On cooldown.
2.  **Demoralizing Shout:** On cooldown.
3.  **Ravager / Champion's Spear:** On cooldown.
4.  **Shield Charge:** On cooldown.
5.  **Thunder Blast:** Use with 2 stacks of Thunderstruck.
6.  **Shield Slam:** Primary builder.
7.  **Thunder Clap:** filler.
8.  **Execute:** spend excess Rage if survivability is stable.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Thunder Clap:** Primary threat and Rage generator.
2.  **Ravager:** Massive AoE burst and Rage.
3.  **Avatar & Demoralizing Shout:** High priority for group mitigation and threat.
4.  **Thunder Blast:** Massive AoE damage.
5.  **Revenge:** Primary AoE spender for damage.

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Shield Wall:** 4-minute cooldown. 40% damage reduction for 8 seconds. **Usage:** Your strongest defensive. Save for "tank-buster" mechanics or large pulls.
*   **Last Stand:** 3-minute cooldown. Increases max HP by 30% for 15 seconds. **Usage:** Emergency panic button or to help healers catch up.
*   **Spell Reflection:** 25-second cooldown. 25% magic damage reduction and reflects the first spell. **Usage:** Essential for magic-heavy encounters.
*   **Demoralizing Shout:** 45-second cooldown. Reduces enemy damage by 20%. **Usage:** High frequency defensive; use as much as possible.
*   **Shockwave:** 30-second or 1-minute cooldown. 2-second AoE stun. **Usage:** Critical utility for stopping dangerous mob casts.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Strength / Item Level**
2.  **Haste:** Critical for reducing the cooldown of Shield Slam, Thunder Clap, and the GCD. (Aim for ~25%)
3.  **Versatility = Critical Strike:**
    *   **Versatility:** Flat damage reduction and healing increase.
    *   **Critical Strike:** Increases Parry chance via Riposte.
4.  **Mastery:** Increases block chance and attack power.

**Note:** For Protection Warrior, **Haste** is the single most important stat for ensuring you can maintain active mitigation uptime.
