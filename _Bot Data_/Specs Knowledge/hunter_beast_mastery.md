## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Pack Leader** *(Recommended — Raid & Mythic+)*
*Focuses on coordinated pet attacks and Stampede synergy.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Howl of the Pack Leader** procs additional free Kill Commands after Bestial Wrath.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Prioritize Kill Command during Pack Leader proc windows. Stampede is now part of the Bestial Wrath burst window.

### **Dark Ranger**
*Focuses on shadow damage and Black Arrow DoT management.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Black Arrow** applies a stacking shadow DoT that spreads via pet attacks.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Maintain Black Arrow on primary target; it is a high-priority ability on cooldown. Venomous Bite now procs from Barbed Shot crits.

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target (Pack Leader)**
1.  **Bestial Wrath:** Use on cooldown — primary DPS cooldown.
2.  **Stampede (Pack Leader):** Used automatically within Bestial Wrath window.
3.  **Kill Command:** Use on cooldown to maintain **Frenzy** stacks (3 stacks = max pet damage).
4.  **Barbed Shot:** Use before Frenzy drops — never let stacks fall off.
5.  **Kill Command (Howl proc):** Use free procs immediately.
6.  **Cobra Shot:** Filler to avoid Focus capping.

### **Single-Target (Dark Ranger)**
1.  **Bestial Wrath:** Use on cooldown.
2.  **Black Arrow:** Use on cooldown — maintain DoT on target.
3.  **Kill Command:** Maintain Frenzy stacks.
4.  **Barbed Shot:** Maintain Frenzy — do not let stacks fall.
5.  **Cobra Shot:** Filler.

### **AoE / Mythic+**
1.  **Multi-Shot:** Maintain **Beast Cleave** (4-second buff); refresh when < 1s remaining.
2.  **Black Arrow (Dark Ranger):** Apply on cooldown — spreads via pet cleave.
3.  **Bestial Wrath:** On cooldown.
4.  **Kill Command:** Maintain Frenzy.
5.  **Barbed Shot:** Maintain Frenzy.
6.  **Kill Command (procs):** Use free procs.

## 5. Major Cooldowns
*   **Bestial Wrath:** 1.5-minute cooldown. Increases pet damage by 25% and removes pet crowd control. Core DPS cooldown — use on cooldown.
*   **Stampede (Pack Leader):** Activated within Bestial Wrath via Pack Leader talent. Sends a stampede of pets to attack.
*   **Black Arrow (Dark Ranger):** High-priority DoT on a short cooldown. Spreads shadow damage through pet attacks.
*   **Bloodlust/Heroism:** Align Bestial Wrath with Lust whenever possible.

## 6. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **NEW:** **Black Arrow** — A new shadow DoT ability exclusive to the Dark Ranger hero talent tree.
*   **CHANGED:** **Nature's Ally** — New Apex talent providing passive pet utility.
*   **CHANGED:** **Venomous Bite** — Now procs from Barbed Shot critical strikes (previously a passive).
*   **CHANGED:** **Bestial Wrath** — Updated to include Stampede functionality for Pack Leader builds.
*   **REMOVED:** **Dire Beast**, **Spitting Cobra**, **A Murder of Crows** — Do not audit for these legacy abilities.
