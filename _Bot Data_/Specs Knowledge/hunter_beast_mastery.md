# Beast Mastery Hunter Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Beast Mastery Hunter is a ranged DPS spec that relies almost entirely on pets for damage. It is unique in that every single ability can be cast while moving, making it the most mobile ranged spec in the game. The gameplay revolves around maintaining the **Frenzy** buff on your pets via **Barbed Shot** and maximizing the uptime of **Bestial Wrath**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
BM Hunter is about keeping your pets empowered and using your core abilities on cooldown.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Frenzy:** Maintain 3 stacks on your pets by spacing out **Barbed Shot** casts.
*   **Bestial Wrath:** Your primary damage window. Using Barbed Shot reduces its cooldown.
*   **Kill Command:** Your main priority ability.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Bestial Wrath:** Use on cooldown.
2.  **Kill Command:** Use on cooldown as your highest priority.
3.  **Barbed Shot:** Use to maintain Frenzy stacks (don't cap at 2 charges).
4.  **Bloodshed:** (If talented) Use on cooldown.
5.  **Cobra Shot:** Use as a filler when Kill Command is on cooldown and you have excess Focus.
6.  **Multi-Shot:** Use once every 4 seconds in AoE to maintain **Beast Cleave**.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Dark Ranger Priority)
1.  **Hunter's Mark:** Ensure it is active on the target.
2.  **Bestial Wrath:** Use on cooldown.
3.  **Barbed Shot:** Use before Bestial Wrath to ensure no charges are wasted.
4.  **Kill Command:** Use on cooldown (Nature's Ally synergy).
5.  **Black Arrow:** Use during the first 10 seconds of Bestial Wrath (Withering Fire window).
6.  **Wailing Arrow:** Use when ~7 seconds remain on Bestial Wrath.
7.  **Cobra Shot:** Filler.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Multi-Shot:** Use to maintain **Beast Cleave** (100% uptime).
2.  **Bestial Wrath:** Use on cooldown.
3.  **Wild Thrash:** Cast on cooldown for heavy AoE damage.
4.  **Kill Command:** Benefits from **Kill Cleave** while Beast Cleave is active.
5.  **Barbed Shot:** Maintain Frenzy on pets.
6.  **Explosive Shot:** Use on cooldown for burst AoE.

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Bestial Wrath:** 30-second base cooldown. Increases you and your pet's damage by 20% for 15 seconds. **Usage:** Use as often as possible. Its cooldown is reduced by 12 seconds every time you use Barbed Shot.
*   **Call of the Wild:** 2-minute cooldown. Summons all of your active pets at once. **Usage:** Your massive burst cooldown. Use it during Bestial Wrath for maximum effect.
*   **Aspect of the Wild:** 2-minute cooldown (if talented). Generates Focus and increases Crit chance. **Usage:** Sync with Call of the Wild and Bestial Wrath.
*   **Exhilaration:** 2-minute cooldown. Heals you for 30% and your pet for 100%. **Usage:** Your primary self-heal.
*   **Turtle Aspect:** 3-minute cooldown. Deflects all attacks and reduces damage taken by 30%, but you cannot attack. **Usage:** Use to survive otherwise lethal mechanics.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of all your pets and summons. By far the strongest stat for BM.
2.  **Critical Strike:** High value for damage and proc synergy.
3.  **Haste:** Reduces the cooldown of Kill Command and Barbed Shot, and increases Focus regen.
4.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Beast Mastery, **Mastery** scales significantly better than other stats, especially in AoE where your pets do the majority of the work.
