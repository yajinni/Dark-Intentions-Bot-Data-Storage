## 4. Deep Dive: Raid Rotation
This rotation focuses on **Bone Shield Stability** and **Death Strike Timing**.

### **A. The Golden Rule of Bone Shield**
1.  **Marrowrend:** Use if stacks are **< 7**.
2.  **Ossuary Check:** Never let stacks drop below **5**. This reduces the cost of *Death Strike* and is the most common audit error.
3.  **Overcapping:** Do not use Marrowrend if stacks are at 10.

### **B. Rotational Priority**
1.  **Death Strike:** 
    *   **Reactive:** Use after taking a hit > 25% of Max HP.
    *   **Proactive:** Use to avoid Runic Power capping (> 75 RP).
2.  **Reaper's Mark (Deathbringer):** Use on cooldown.
3.  **Death and Decay:** Keep active on the ground at all times for cleave and damage reduction.
4.  **Blood Boil:** Use if 2 charges are available or to maintain *Blood Plague*.
5.  **Heart Strike / Vampiric Strike:** Main Rune spender to generate Runic Power.

## 5. Major Cooldowns Breakdown

### **Defensive Utility**
*   **Vampiric Blood:** Increases Max HP and Healing Received by 30%. Use *proactively* before predicted heavy damage.
*   **Dancing Rune Weapon:** 40% Parry and mirrors your attacks. Doubles Bone Shield generation from Marrowrend.
*   **Icebound Fortitude:** 30% Damage Reduction and Stun Immunity.
*   **Anti-Magic Shell:** Absorbs magic damage and prevents debuff application.
*   **Tombstone:** Consumes Bone Shield for a shield and RP. Synergizes with *Dancing Rune Weapon* CDR.

### **Offensive / Utility**
*   **Empower Rune Weapon:** 15% Haste and instant Rune/RP generation.
*   **Raise Dead:** Summons Ghoul for minor DPS support.
