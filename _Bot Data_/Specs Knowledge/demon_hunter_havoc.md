## 2. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Fel-Scarred (Burst/Metamorphosis Focus)**
*Focuses on empowering Demon Form through the Demonsurge mechanic.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Abyssal Gaze** (replaces *Eye Beam*) and **Consuming Fire** (replaces *Immolation Aura*).
*   **Rotational Shift:** Use **Abyssal Gaze** and **Death Sweep** to consume **Demonsurge** procs during Metamorphosis.
*   **Audit Check:** Ensure **Consuming Fire** uptime is maximized and used on cooldown.

### **Aldrachi Reaver (Priority Target Focus)**
*Focuses on the Reaver's Mark and Art of the Glaive mechanics.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Reaver's Glaive** (replaces *Throw Glaive* procs).
*   **Rotational Shift:** Prioritize applying **Reaver's Mark** with the glaive and then consuming it with **Death Sweep** or **Annihilation**.

## 3. Full Rotational Priority

### **The Opener (Fel-Scarred)**
1.  **Immolation Aura / Consuming Fire:** Use on pull.
2.  **Felblade:** Generate Fury.
3.  **Vengeful Retreat:** Trigger **Inertia** and **Initiative**.
4.  **The Hunt:** Use during the Inertia window.
5.  **Abyssal Gaze:** Enter Metamorphosis.
6.  **Death Sweep:** Use immediately to consume first Demonsurge proc.

### **Single-Target Priority**
1.  **Felblade:** If Inertia is ready.
2.  **Abyssal Gaze / Eye Beam:** Use on cooldown.
3.  **Death Sweep / Blade Dance:** Use on cooldown.
4.  **Consuming Fire / Immolation Aura:** Keep at < 2 charges.
5.  **Vengeful Retreat:** For Initiative crit buff.
6.  **The Hunt:** Use with Inertia/Initiative.
7.  **Chaos Strike / Annihilation:** Fury spender.
8.  **Fel Rush:** Filler (if talented into *Unbound Chaos*).
