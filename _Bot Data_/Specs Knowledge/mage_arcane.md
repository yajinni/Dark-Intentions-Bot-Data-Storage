## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Spellslinger** *(Recommended)*
*Focuses on high uptime of Splinters and Splinterstorm.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Arcane Missiles and Arcane Blast generate Splinters. Splinterstorm detonates automatically.
*   **Rotational Shift:** High emphasis on maintaining Arcane Missiles and using Arcane Orb to generate Splinters quickly.

### **Sunfury** *(Alternative)*
*Focuses on high uptime of Arcane Phoenix and Burden of Power.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Arcane Surge summons the Arcane Phoenix, which mimics your casts.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Prioritize Arcane Barrage when Burden of Power is active to maximize Phoenix damage.

---

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target (Spellslinger)**
1.  **Arcane Orb:** Use whenever you have Clearcasting and are at low Arcane Salvo stacks.
2.  **Presence of Mind:** Use with Arcane Blast for burst.
3.  **Arcane Barrage:** Use at 20 stacks of Arcane Salvo.
4.  **Arcane Missiles:** Use whenever Clearcasting procs.
5.  **Arcane Blast:** Filler to maintain Charges.

### **AoE / Multi-Target**
1.  **Arcane Orb:** High priority; use with Clearcasting if fewer than 14 Arcane Salvo stacks.
2.  **Arcane Barrage:** Primary spender for 4+ targets.
3.  **Arcane Explosion:** Primary builder on 3+ targets.
