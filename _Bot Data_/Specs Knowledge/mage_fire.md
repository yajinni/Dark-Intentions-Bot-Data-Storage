## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Frostfire** *(Recommended)*
*Focuses on Frostfire Bolt procs and Elemental Excess.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Frostfire Bolt replaces Fireball.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Balance Excess Fire and Excess Frost buffs. Frostfire Bolt procs are high priority.

### **Sunfury** *(Alternative)*
*Focuses on high uptime of Arcane Phoenix and Burden of Power.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Combustion summons the Arcane Phoenix.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Prioritize Phoenix Flames and Pyroblast to keep the Phoenix active.

---

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target (Frostfire)**
1.  **Phoenix Flames:** Use to maintain stacks, avoid capping.
2.  **Fire Blast:** Use during Combustion to trigger Hot Streak.
3.  **Pyroblast:** Use whenever you have Hot Streak.
4.  **Frostfire Bolt:** Primary filler and proc generator.

### **AoE / Multi-Target**
1.  **Flamestrike:** Replace Pyroblast on 4+ targets (if targets > 90% HP, check Firestarter logic).
2.  **Phoenix Flames:** High priority for cleave damage.
3.  **Dragon's Breath:** Use with Alexstrasza's Fury for guaranteed crit.
