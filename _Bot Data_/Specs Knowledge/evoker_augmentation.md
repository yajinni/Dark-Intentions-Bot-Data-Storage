## 2. Stat Priority
1. **Critical Strike** — Highest priority; allows buffs to crit (Chronowarden)
2. **Haste** — Reduces GCD and cast times (Scalecommander priority)
3. **Mastery** — Increases duration and power of your buffs
4. **Versatility** — Flat damage/reduction

---

## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Chronowarden** *(Recommended — Raid)*
*Focuses on buff crit chance and Ebon Might extension through time-based synergies.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Increases the chance for your buffs (Prescience, Ebon Might) to critically strike, amplifying ally stat gains.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Critical Strike is the highest priority secondary. Prescience on the two highest DPS.

### **Scalecommander** *(Recommended — Mythic+)*
*Focuses on personal DPS contribution and Ebon Might uptime through combat aggression.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Reduces cast times and GCD — enables faster Eruption casts to extend Ebon Might.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Haste becomes the top secondary. More aggressive Eruption spam.

---

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Single-Target (Raid)**
1.  **Ebon Might:** Maintain as close to 100% uptime as possible — the lynchpin of the spec.
2.  **Prescience:** Keep active on 2 highest-performing allies at all times.
3.  **Breath of Eons:** Coordinate with major raid/party cooldowns (Bloodlust, PI, etc.).
4.  **Tip the Scales:** Use with Fire Breath during Ebon Might.
5.  **Fire Breath:** Level 3 during Ebon Might window.
6.  **Upheaval:** Level 1 during Ebon Might window.
7.  **Eruption:** Primary Essence spender — each cast extends Ebon Might.
8.  **Living Flame:** Primary Essence builder (filler).
9.  **Azure Strike:** Filler on movement.

### **AoE / Multi-Target**
1.  **Breath of Eons:** Primary AoE burst — opens the window.
2.  **Ebon Might:** Apply immediately; group-wide stat buff.
3.  **Upheaval:** Highest rank — massive AoE burst and Ebon Might extension.
4.  **Fire Breath:** AoE damage + extension.
5.  **Eruption:** Cleaves through targets; extends Ebon Might.
6.  **Azure Strike:** Primary builder for 2+ targets.

---

## 5. Major Cooldowns
*   **Breath of Eons:** 2-minute cooldown. Accumulates ally damage for a massive delayed explosion. Always coordinate with raid burst windows (Bloodlust, PI, Trinkets).
*   **Ebon Might:** 30-second base cooldown. Increases primary stats of nearby allies. Goal: 70%+ uptime.
*   **Tip the Scales:** 2-minute cooldown. Makes next Empower spell instant and max level. Best paired with Fire Breath during Ebon Might.
*   **Blistering Scales:** 30-second cooldown. Armor + reflect damage. Keep on the tank permanently.
*   **Zephyr:** 2-minute cooldown. Reduces AoE damage taken for the group — a powerful defensive utility.

## 6. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **REWORK:** Ebon Might extension mechanics updated — Eruption and Empower spells now interact more consistently with extension math.
*   **NEW Hero Talent interactions:** Both Chronowarden and Scalecommander have updated synergies with Ebon Might and Breath of Eons.
*   **Prescience:** Remains a 2-target maintenance buff — do not audit for more than 2 active stacks.
*   **REMINDER:** Augmentation DPS numbers will appear lower than peers — the spec's contribution is measured through **ally damage amplification**, not personal output.
