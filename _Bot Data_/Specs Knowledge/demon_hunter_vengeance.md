## 2. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Aldrachi Reaver (Art of the Glaive Focus)**
*Focuses on high-impact Art of the Glaive combos.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Reaver's Glaive** initiates a 3-part combo.
*   **Rotational Shift:** You must complete the combo (**Glaive → Fracture → Soul Cleave**) to gain the maximum damage and healing buffs. 
*   **Audit Check:** Verify that *Reaver's Glaive* is followed by the correct sequence of builders and spenders.

### **Fel-Scarred / Annihilator (Voidfall Focus)**
*Focuses on managing Voidfall stacks and maximizing Fiery Brand uptime.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Voidfall** stacks (builds via Fracture, spent via Spirit Bomb/Soul Cleave).
*   **Rotational Shift:** You should only spend Fragments with **Spirit Bomb** or **Soul Cleave** once you reach **3 stacks of Voidfall** to maximize efficiency.
*   **Audit Check:** High **Fiery Brand** uptime is now a baseline defensive requirement (40% flat DR).

## 3. Full Rotational Priority

### **The Opener**
1.  **Sigil of Spite:** Use on pull to generate initial fragments and threat.
2.  **Immolation Aura:** Generate initial Fury.
3.  **Fiery Brand:** Apply immediately for the 40% DR.
4.  **Fracture (x2):** Build initial Soul Fragments and **Voidfall** stacks.
5.  **Spirit Bomb:** Spend fragments at 3 stacks of Voidfall.
6.  **Metamorphosis:** Use during high-damage windows to reset **Voidfall** stacks.

### **Priority List (Defensive/Resource Management)**
1.  **Fiery Brand:** Maintain 100% uptime (Priority #1 for survival).
2.  **Sigil of Spite:** Use on cooldown (triggers Hero Talent combos).
3.  **Spirit Bomb:** Use with 5+ Fragments ONLY at 3 stacks of **Voidfall**.
4.  **Soul Cleave:** Use with 3+ Fragments at 3 stacks of **Voidfall**.
5.  **Fracture:** Use to generate fragments and build Voidfall.
6.  **Sigil of Flame:** Use for resource generation.
7.  **Fel Devastation:** Use for burst healing/damage (No longer triggers Meta).

## 4. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **REMOVED: The Hunt** — Fully removed. Do not audit for hunt usage.
*   **REMOVED: Demonic** — *Fel Devastation* no longer triggers Metamorphosis.
*   **CHANGED: Fiery Brand** — Now a flat 40% DR on the player, not a target DoT.
*   **CHANGED: Spirit Bomb** — Now has a **25-second hasted cooldown**.
*   **ADDED: Voidfall System** — New resource management mechanic for spenders.
*   **ADDED: Soul Cleanse / Burn It Out** — New dispel utility (Curse/Disease).
