# Preservation Evoker Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Preservation Evoker is a mid-range reactive healer that specializes in high-throughput burst healing and time-manipulation mechanics. The spec revolves around managing **Essence** and using **Echo** to duplicate powerful healing spells. It excels at stabilizing groups through **Dream Breath** and has the unique ability to "reverse" damage through **Rewind**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Preservation is about preparing for damage with Echo and reacting to health spikes with your powerful Empower spells.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Echo:** Consumes Essence to mark a target; your next non-Echo heal will be duplicated on that target at 70-100% effectiveness.
*   **Empower Spells:** **Dream Breath** (front-cone HoT) and **Spiritbloom** (multi-target burst). Higher ranks increase the number of targets or the initial burst.
*   **Essence:** Your primary resource. Build over time and spend on Echo or Emerald Blossom.
*   **Essence Burst:** Procs that make your next Essence spender free.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Temporal Anomaly:** Use on cooldown to apply shields and free Echoes to the group.
2.  **Echo:** Apply to injured targets before using a major heal.
3.  **Dream Breath:** Use at Rank 1 for efficient group-wide HoT.
4.  **Reversion:** Maintain on targets taking steady damage (especially with Echo).
5.  **Verdant Embrace:** Use for immediate single-target burst or to position yourself.
6.  **Emerald Blossom:** Use when Essence is capping and the group is stacked.
7.  **Living Flame:** Primary filler for both healing and damage.

## Rotation Priority

### Healing Priority
1.  **Rewind:** Use after massive raid-wide damage hits.
2.  **Temporal Anomaly:** Maintain 100% cooldown efficiency.
3.  **Dream Breath:** Rank 1 for sustained group damage.
4.  **Spiritbloom:** Use at appropriate rank for burst group triage.
5.  **Echo + Reversion:** Use for efficient multi-target stabilization.
6.  **Verdant Embrace:** Single-target triage.
7.  **Living Flame:** filler.

### Damage Priority
1.  **Fire Breath:** Use at Rank 1 to apply DoT and trigger **Leaping Flames**.
2.  **Living Flame:** Primary damage filler.
3.  **Azure Strike:** Use for movement or multi-target damage.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Rewind:** 4-minute cooldown. Heals 50% of damage taken in the last 5 seconds. **Usage:** The ultimate "reset" button for the raid.
*   **Dream Flight:** 2-minute cooldown. Fly and heal in a line. **Usage:** Excellent for movement-heavy phases or burst group healing.
*   **Stasis:** 1.5-minute cooldown. Stores your next 3 spells to be released later. **Usage:** Use to double up on powerful combos (e.g., Anomaly -> Dream Breath -> Spiritbloom).
*   **Tip the Scales:** 2-minute cooldown. Makes your next Empower spell instant and max rank. **Usage:** Use with Dream Breath or Spiritbloom for emergency burst.
*   **Zephyr:** 2-minute cooldown. 20% AoE damage reduction for the party. **Usage:** Proactive group defensive.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases healing based on your health vs. target health. Strongest for raw throughput.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Increases chance for double healing and damage.
4.  **Haste:** Reduces cast times and GCD (vital for Echo application speed).
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage reduction and healing increase.

**Note:** For Preservation, **Mastery** is the king of throughput, while **Haste** ensures you can react fast enough to save targets.
