## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Chronowarden** *(Recommended — Raid)*
*Focuses on Temporal Anomaly synergies and Echo generation.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Nozdormu Adept** reduces the cooldown and mana cost of Temporal Anomaly, enabling frequent Echo generation.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Weave Temporal Anomaly on cooldown; it generates shielding and Echo buffs central to the healing loop.

### **Flameshaper** *(Recommended — Mythic+)*
*Focused on Living Flame synergies and Consume Flame for single-target burst healing.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** **Consume Flame** provides strong single-target healing on demand.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Consume Flame now only heals the primary target — do not expect raid-wide burst from it.

## 4. Full Healing Priority

### **Core Healing Rotation (Raid)**
1.  **Echo:** Core of the spec — pre-cast before incoming damage on key targets.
2.  **Reversion:** Maintain on party/raid members; becomes a burst heal via **Merithra's Blessing**.
3.  **Temporal Anomaly:** Use on cooldown for shields and Echo buffs (Chronowarden priority).
4.  **Dream Breath:** Rank 1 for efficient group healing — always triggers **Merithra's Blessing** in Midnight.
5.  **Verdant Embrace:** Burst healing or to trigger **Life-Bind** (note: Life-Bind can no longer be replicated by Echo).
6.  **Emerald Blossom:** Use with **Essence Burst** (Twin Echoes talent) for free Echo casts.
7.  **Living Flame:** Filler healing or damage between major heals.

**Echo Consumption Priority:** Merithra's Blessing > Verdant Embrace > Dream Breath > Reversion

### **Dungeon / Mythic+ Rotation**
1.  Echo → Reversion (maintain on tank).
2.  Echo → Dream Breath (burst heal windows).
3.  Spirit Bloom for emergency burst.
4.  Verdant Embrace to trigger Life-Bind.

---

## 5. Major Cooldowns
*   **Stasis:** Stores next 3 spells. Optimal combo: Dream Breath → Temporal Anomaly → Merithra's Blessing.
*   **Rewind:** Heals 40% of damage taken in the last 5 seconds — best for recovering from burst raid damage.
*   **Dream Flight:** Powerful line heal that leaves a massive HoT — use for predictable raid-wide damage.
*   **Tip the Scales:** Makes next empowered spell instant and max rank — use with Dream Breath for emergency healing.
*   **Zephyr:** Reduces AoE damage taken by 20% — a powerful raid defensive cooldown.

---

## 6. Midnight 12.0.5 Changes (Audit Cleanup)
*   **REMOVED:** Spiritbloom, Emerald Communion, Cycle of Life, Engulf — do not audit for these.
*   **REMOVED:** Lifebind can no longer be replicated via Echo.
*   **REWORK:** Emerald Blossom no longer consumes Echo and has been significantly buffed — use freely with Essence Burst.
*   **REWORK:** Consume Flame (Flameshaper) now only heals the primary target — not a raid-wide cooldown.
*   **NEW Apex Talent:** **Merithra's Blessing** — Dream Breath always triggers this, transforming your next Reversion into a high-throughput burst heal.
*   **NEW Talent:** **Twin Echoes** — Emerald Blossom cast with Essence Burst grants free Echoes.
*   **NEW Talent:** **Dream Simulacrum** — Verdant Embrace no longer flies you to the target; healing is increased.
