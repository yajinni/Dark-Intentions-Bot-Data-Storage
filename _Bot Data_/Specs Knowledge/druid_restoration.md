## 3. Hero Talents Deep Dive

### **Keeper of the Grove**
*   **Core Focus:** **Grove Guardians** (Treants) & **Reforestation**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Increases the importance of the *Swiftmend* cycle to trigger free *Grove Guardians*. Focuses on burst healing windows.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for raid stabilization and burst management in 12.0.5.

### **Wildstalker**
*   **Core Mechanic:** **Symbiotic Blooms**.
*   **Gameplay Effect:** Enhances the efficiency of your HoTs over time. More passive and steady than the Keeper build.
*   **Why Pick?** Superior for low-intensity healing or target-rich Mythic+ environments.

## 4. Deep Dive: Raid Rotation
Focus: **HoT Maintenance** and **Ramp Efficiency**.

### **A. The Maintenance Audit (100% Uptime)**
1.  **Efflorescence:** Maintain 100% uptime on the ground (under the raid cluster).
2.  **Lifebloom:** Maintain on **3 targets** (if talented) or 1 target at all times.
3.  **Audit Flag:** Letting **Efflorescence** or **Lifebloom** fall off is a critical "Baseline Loss" error.

### **B. The Ramp Sequence**
1.  **Rejuvenation:** Pre-apply to 5-10 targets before predicted raid damage.
2.  **Wild Growth:** Use immediately after the damage hit.
3.  **Swiftmend:** Use to trigger **Soul of the Forest** (buffing the next HoT).
4.  **Grove Guardians:** Use treants to bridge medium-damage gaps.
5.  **Audit Flag:** Casting *Wild Growth* without a *Swiftmend* (Soul of the Forest) buff during high-damage windows is a throughput error.

## 5. Major Cooldowns Breakdown (Strict Whitelist)
*   **Tranquility:** 3-minute CD. Major raid-wide healing channel.
*   **Convoke the Spirits:** 1-minute CD (if talented). Burst stabilization.
*   **Incarnation: Tree of Life:** Massive healing buff and instant Regrowths.
*   **Grove Guardians:** Your primary rotational treants for consistent HPS.
*   **Ironbark:** Primary single-target external for tanks or low-health allies.
