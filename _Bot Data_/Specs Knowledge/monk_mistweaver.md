## 3. Hero Talent Breakdown

### **Conduit of the Celestials** *(Recommended)*
*Focuses on high throughput and synergy with August Celestials (Chi-Ji/Yu'lon).*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Increases the healing of your primary cooldowns and adds passive periodic healing.
*   **Rotational Shift:** Higher priority on keeping Invoke Chi-Ji/Yu'lon active.

### **Master of Harmony** *(Alternative)*
*Focuses on specific damage-to-healing conversions and utility.*
*   **Key Mechanic:** Converts a portion of damage dealt into a pool of healing for later use.
*   **Rotational Shift:** More aggressive DPS rotation to build the Harmony pool.

## 4. Full Rotational Priority

### **Healing Priority**
1.  **Renewing Mist:** Maintain on as many targets as possible (primary source of Vivify cleave).
2.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Use on cooldown if running Rising Mist to extend Renewing Mist.
3.  **Vivify:** Primary spot healing and cleave (via Renewing Mist).
4.  **Sheilun's Gift:** Use for massive group burst healing (collects clouds passively).
5.  **Enveloping Mist:** Use for heavy single-target tank healing.

### **DPS Priority (Ancient Teachings)**
1.  **Ancient Teachings:** Maintain the buff by casting Essence Font or Faeline Stomp (if talented).
2.  **Tiger Palm:** Build stacks of Teachings of the Monastery.
3.  **Blackout Kick:** Spend stacks to reset Rising Sun Kick.
4.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Primary damage and healing extension.
