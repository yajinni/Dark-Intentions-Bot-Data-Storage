# Mistweaver Monk Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Mistweaver Monk is a versatile healer that can play as a traditional ranged healer or a melee-based "Fistweaver". The core mechanic revolves around **Renewing Mist**, which allows other spells like **Vivify** to cleave-heal all affected targets. They have unique utility through **Ring of Peace** and powerful group stabilization via **Invoke Yu'lon** or **Chi-Ji**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Mistweaver is about maintaining high uptime on Renewing Mist and using your mana efficiently through Mana Tea.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Renewing Mist:** Your most important maintenance spell. Spread it as much as possible.
*   **Vivify:** Your primary single-target heal; cleaves to everyone with Renewing Mist.
*   **Mana Tea:** Use frequently to reduce the mana cost of your expensive spells.
*   **Ancient Teachings:** (Fistweaving) Dealing damage heals nearby allies.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Renewing Mist:** Use on cooldown (always).
2.  **Vivify:** Use for group damage (cleaves).
3.  **Enveloping Mist:** Use for heavy single-target priority damage.
4.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Use on cooldown (even if not Fistweaving, to extend HoTs via talents).
5.  **Mana Tea:** Use before heavy healing phases.
6.  **Soothing Mist:** Channel on a target for instant-cast Vivify/Enveloping Mist.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Healing Priority
1.  **Renewing Mist:** Maintain maximum stacks/uptime.
2.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Use on cooldown to extend HoTs (Rising Mist talent).
3.  **Vivify:** Primary spender for group damage.
4.  **Enveloping Mist:** Priority single-target stabilization.
5.  **Sheilun's Gift:** Use at high stacks for massive group burst.
6.  **Thunder Focus Tea:** Use with Renewing Mist for duration or Enveloping Mist for instant cast.

### Damage Priority (Fistweaving)
1.  **Jadefire Stomp:** Maintain uptime for Ancient Teachings.
2.  **Rising Sun Kick:** Primary damage and healing trigger.
3.  **Blackout Kick:** Use at 3-4 stacks of **Teachings of the Monastery**.
4.  **Tiger Palm:** Use to build stacks.
5.  **Spinning Crane Kick:** AoE damage and healing.

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Invoke Yu'lon, the Jade Serpent / Invoke Chi-Ji, the Red Crane:** 1-minute or 2-minute cooldown. **Usage:** Your primary throughput burst. Use proactively for heavy damage windows.
*   **Revival / Restoral:** 3-minute cooldown. Instant raid-wide heal and mass dispel. **Usage:** Panic button or for specific dispel mechanics.
*   **Life Cocoon:** 2-minute cooldown. External shield and 50% healing bonus. **Usage:** Use on the tank or a player about to take a massive hit.
*   **Sheilun's Gift:** 1-minute (approx) recharge. **Usage:** Use for predictable group-wide bursts.
*   **Mana Tea:** 1-minute cooldown. Reduces mana costs by 50%. **Usage:** Essential for mana management.

## Stat Priority

### Raiding
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Haste:** Faster GCD and more Renewing Mist procs.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Powerful for all healing and damage.
4.  **Versatility:** Flat healing boost and damage reduction.
5.  **Mastery:** Historically the weakest stat for Mistweaver in raids, but strong for single-target.

### Mythic+ (Dungeons)
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Haste**
3.  **Critical Strike >= Versatility**
4.  **Mastery**

**Note:** **Haste** is the engine that drives Mistweaver's throughput and resource generation.
