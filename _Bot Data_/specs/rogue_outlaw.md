# Outlaw Rogue Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Outlaw Rogue is a high-action, high-energy melee DPS spec that revolves around the **Roll the Bones** (RtB) mechanic. The spec is defined by its extremely high Actions Per Minute (CPM) and the constant reduction of its own cooldowns via **Restless Blades**. The gameplay is a fast-paced "build and spend" cycle where you must react to various combat buffs and procs.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Outlaw is about staying active and ensuring you always have active combat buffs while maximizing your Energy and Combo Point efficiency.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Roll the Bones:** Grants random combat buffs. You must maintain these at all times.
*   **Restless Blades:** Every Combo Point spent reduces the cooldown of several major abilities (Adrenaline Rush, Between the Eyes, etc.).
*   **Opportunity:** Sinister Strike has a chance to make your next **Pistol Shot** deal double damage and cost half energy.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Blade Flurry:** Maintain 100% uptime for 2+ targets.
2.  **Roll the Bones:** Use if you have no buffs or only 1 "weak" buff.
3.  **Adrenaline Rush:** Use on cooldown (your most important cooldown).
4.  **Between the Eyes:** Spend 6+ Combo Points on this whenever available.
5.  **Dispatch:** Your primary finisher at 6+ Combo Points.
6.  **Sinister Strike:** Primary builder.
7.  **Pistol Shot:** Only use with **Opportunity** procs.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Fatebound Priority)
1.  **Roll the Bones:** Use on cooldown unless you are in **Stage 2** or higher (Fatebound synergy).
2.  **Keep It Rolling:** Use when in Stage 2 or higher to lock in powerful buffs.
3.  **Adrenaline Rush:** Maintain 100% cooldown efficiency.
4.  **Blade Rush:** Use on cooldown as a builder.
5.  **Between the Eyes:** Cast on cooldown with 6+ Combo Points.
6.  **Dispatch:** Spend 6+ CP.
7.  **Pistol Shot:** Use with 6 stacks of **Opportunity**.
8.  **Sinister Strike:** main builder.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Blade Flurry:** Must be active for 2+ targets.
2.  **ST Priority:** Once Blade Flurry is active, follow the Single-Target priority. The damage will automatically cleave.
3.  **Deft Maneuvers:** Cast Blade Flurry at low Combo Points to trigger the buff.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Adrenaline Rush:** 3-minute base cooldown (reduced by Restless Blades). Increases Energy regen, Energy cap, and attack speed. **Usage:** The core of the spec. Use as often as possible.
*   **Keep It Rolling:** 7-minute base cooldown. Extends RtB buffs by 15s. **Usage:** Essential for maintaining "good" buffs or high Fatebound stages.
*   **Killing Spree:** 2-minute base cooldown. Rapidly strikes targets. **Usage:** Use at high CP for maximum damage.
*   **Blade Rush:** 45-second base cooldown. Charges and generates Energy. **Usage:** Use on cooldown for resource generation.
*   **Vanish:** Use offensively to benefit from stealth-related talents or to generate CP.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Agility**
2.  **Critical Strike:** Highly valuable for procs and maximizing Between the Eyes damage.
3.  **Haste:** Essential for Energy regeneration and reducing the GCD (aim for ~20-25%).
4.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.
5.  **Mastery:** Generally the lowest priority as it only impacts off-hand procs.

**Note:** For Outlaw, **Crit** and **Haste** are the two stats that define the spec's pace and burst potential.
