# Frost Mage Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Frost Mage is a control-oriented ranged DPS spec that focuses on freezing enemies and shattering them with heavy critical strikes. The gameplay revolves around managing **Icicles**, **Fingers of Frost**, and **Brain Freeze** procs. It is known for its high consistency and powerful defensive utility.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Frost Mage is about reacting to procs while maintaining a constant stream of frost damage.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Fingers of Frost:** Makes your next **Ice Lance** deal damage as if the target were frozen.
*   **Brain Freeze:** Makes your next **Flurry** instant and apply **Winter's Chill**, allowing subsequent spells to "Shatter".
*   **Icicles:** Generated by Frostbolt and Flurry; stored and then fired automatically or consumed by **Glacial Spike**.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Frozen Orb:** Use on cooldown to generate Fingers of Frost.
2.  **Ice Lance:** Use whenever you have a **Fingers of Frost** proc.
3.  **Flurry:** Use whenever you have a **Brain Freeze** proc.
4.  **Glacial Spike:** Use as soon as you have 5 Icicles.
5.  **Comet Storm:** Use on cooldown.
6.  **Frostbolt:** Your primary filler to generate Icicles and procs.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Spellslinger Priority)
1.  **Comet Storm:** On cooldown.
2.  **Flurry:** With **Brain Freeze** proc.
3.  **Frozen Orb:** On cooldown.
4.  **Ice Lance:** With **Fingers of Frost**.
5.  **Glacial Spike:** At 5 Icicles.
6.  **Ice Lance:** When target has 6+ **Freezing** stacks (Spellslinger synergy).
7.  **Ray of Frost:** Use as a primary mini-cooldown.
8.  **Frostbolt:** Main filler.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Blizzard:** Maintain 100% uptime.
2.  **Frozen Orb:** High priority for AoE damage and Fingers of Frost generation.
3.  **Comet Storm:** Use on cooldown.
4.  **Flurry:** With procs to apply Winter's Chill for cleave.
5.  **Ice Lance:** Use with Fingers of Frost procs (cleaves while Blizzard is active if talented).

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Icy Veins:** 3-minute cooldown. Increases Haste by 30% and provides other buffs based on talents. **Usage:** Your primary burst window. Often extended by spending procs (Thermal Void).
*   **Ray of Frost:** 1-minute cooldown. High-damage channel that generates Freezing stacks. **Usage:** Best used during Icy Veins or for priority target burst.
*   **Frozen Orb:** 1-minute cooldown. Essential for both AoE and single-target resource generation.
*   **Ice Block / Ice Cold:** 4-minute cooldown. Defensive immunity (Ice Block) or 70% damage reduction (Ice Cold). **Usage:** Your primary panic button.
*   **Time Warp:** 5-minute cooldown. 30% Haste.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases Icicle and Frozen Orb damage.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Highly valuable up to the "Shatter Cap" (33.34%), then loses some value but remains strong.
4.  **Haste:** Increases cast speed and resource generation.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Frost, reaching the **Shatter Cap** (33.34% Crit) is historically important, though in current patches Mastery often rivals it for top priority.
