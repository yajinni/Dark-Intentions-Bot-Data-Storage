# Enhancement Shaman Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Enhancement Shaman is a fast-paced melee damage dealer that channels elemental forces through dual-wielded weapons. The spec revolves around building and spending **Maelstrom Weapon (MW)** stacks on powerful spells while maintaining a priority-based melee rotation. In the current meta, the **Totemic** Hero Talent build is highly recommended for all content types.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Enhancement is about staying active and keeping your basic buffs up while reacting to your Maelstrom Weapon stacks.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Maelstrom Weapon (MW):** Build stacks through melee attacks; spend at 10 stacks on Lightning Bolt or Chain Lightning.
*   **Totemic Synergy:** (Totemic build) Your **Surging Totem** provides constant damage and MW generation.
*   **Thorim's Invocation:** Casting Lightning Bolt/Chain Lightning "primes" your melee strikes to discharge that spell.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Surging Totem:** Place on cooldown.
2.  **Lava Lash:** Use on cooldown (especially with **Hot Hand** procs).
3.  **Stormstrike:** Primary builder.
4.  **Lightning Bolt:** Spend 10 MW stacks (Single Target).
5.  **Chain Lightning:** Spend 10 MW stacks (AoE).
6.  **Crash Lightning:** Use for AoE and to empower your melee strikes.
7.  **Sundering:** Use on cooldown for massive physical damage.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target (Totemic Priority)
1.  **Voltaic Blaze:** Use if Flame Shock is missing.
2.  **Surging Totem:** Always maintain uptime.
3.  **Lava Lash:** Use with Hot Hand or Whirling Fire procs.
4.  **Sundering:** High priority burst.
5.  **Doom Winds:** Use for burst MW generation.
6.  **Lightning Bolt:** Spend 10 MW stacks.
7.  **Stormstrike:** Primary builder.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Sundering:** Massive AoE burst.
2.  **Primordial Storm:** Spend 10 MW stacks for heavy AoE damage.
3.  **Crash Lightning:** maintain active cleave.
4.  **Chain Lightning:** Spend 10 MW stacks.
5.  **Lava Lash:** Spread Flame Shock (if talented).

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Doom Winds:** 1-minute cooldown. Increases Windfury chance and generates 2 MW per second. **Usage:** Your primary 1-minute burst tool. Always pair with Surging Totem.
*   **Feral Spirit:** 2-minute cooldown. Summons two wolves that increase your elemental damage. **Usage:** Use on cooldown for sustained throughput.
*   **Ascendance:** 3-minute cooldown. Transforms you into a Wind Ascendant. **Usage:** Your "big" cooldown.
*   **Surging Totem:** 1-minute cooldown. The cornerstone of the Totemic build. **Usage:** Place at the start of every pull.
*   **Astral Shift:** 2-minute cooldown. 40% damage reduction. **Usage:** Primary defensive.

## Stat Priority

### Totemic Build (Recommended)
1.  **Agility**
2.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of all your elemental strikes and procs.
3.  **Haste:** Essential for attack speed and resource generation.
4.  **Critical Strike:** Increases chance for double damage.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

### Stormbringer Build
1.  **Agility**
2.  **Haste**
3.  **Mastery = Critical Strike**
4.  **Versatility**

**Note:** For Enhancement, **Mastery** is typically the most important secondary stat for the Totemic build, while **Haste** is favored for Stormbringer.
