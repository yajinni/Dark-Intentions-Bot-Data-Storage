# Augmentation Evoker Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Augmentation Evoker is a unique "Support DPS" spec that focuses on empowering allies while maintaining personal damage. The spec's power comes from maximizing the uptime of **Ebon Might**, which increases the primary stat of nearby allies. The gameplay involves a priority system of extending Ebon Might through Essence spenders and Empower spells, while providing targeted utility like **Prescience**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Augmentation is about keeping your core buffs active on yourself and your allies while ensuring you don't over-cap Essence.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Ebon Might:** Your most important task. Almost all other actions should support extending this buff.
*   **Prescience:** Maintain on two high-performing allies to increase their Crit chance.
*   **Essence:** Your primary resource. Build with Living Flame/Azure Strike and spend on **Eruption** to extend Ebon Might.
*   **Empower Spells:** **Fire Breath** and **Upheaval** provide burst damage and extend Ebon Might.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Ebon Might:** Cast if it has < 4 seconds remaining.
2.  **Prescience:** Maintain on two allies.
3.  **Breath of Eons:** Use on cooldown (with ally burst windows).
4.  **Fire Breath:** Use at Level 3 (during Ebon Might).
5.  **Upheaval:** Use at Level 1 (during Ebon Might).
6.  **Eruption:** Spend Essence to extend Ebon Might.
7.  **Living Flame:** Primary filler.
8.  **Azure Strike:** Filler for movement or AoE.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Ebon Might:** Maintain maximum uptime.
2.  **Breath of Eons:** Primary support burst.
3.  **Prescience:** maintain on 2 targets.
4.  **Tip the Scales:** Use with Fire Breath.
5.  **Fire Breath:** Level 3.
6.  **Upheaval:** Level 1.
7.  **Eruption:** Primary spender to extend Ebon Might.
8.  **Living Flame:** builder.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Ebon Might:** Keep active for group-wide stat buff.
2.  **Upheaval:** Massive AoE burst and extension.
3.  **Fire Breath:** Massive AoE damage.
4.  **Eruption:** Cleaves through targets.
5.  **Azure Strike:** Primary builder for 2+ targets.

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Breath of Eons:** 2-minute cooldown. Accumulates ally damage for a massive explosion. **Usage:** Your most powerful tool. Always coordinate with raid/party cooldowns (e.g., Bloodlust, PI).
*   **Ebon Might:** 30-second base cooldown. Increases primary stats. **Usage:** The lynchpin of the spec. Goal is 70%+ uptime.
*   **Tip the Scales:** 2-minute cooldown. Makes your next Empower spell instant and max level. **Usage:** Best used with Fire Breath during Ebon Might.
*   **Zephyr:** 2-minute cooldown. Reduces AoE damage taken for the group. **Usage:** Powerful defensive utility.
*   **Blistering Scales:** 30-second cooldown. Increases a target's armor and deals damage when hit. **Usage:** Keep on the tank.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Critical Strike:** High priority for **Chronowarden** (allows buffs to crit).
3.  **Haste:** High priority for **Scalecommander** (reduces cast times and GCD).
4.  **Mastery:** Increases the duration and power of your buffs.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Augmentation, **Crit** and **Haste** are typically the most important secondaries for maintaining rotational flow and maximizing support value.
