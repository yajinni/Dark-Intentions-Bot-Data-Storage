# Subtlety Rogue Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Subtlety Rogue is a high-burst melee DPS spec that revolves around its signature cooldown, **Shadow Dance**. The spec is defined by its reliance on stealth-only abilities and its complex "dance" windows where you must pool resources and then unleash explosive damage. The gameplay follows a strict priority system focused on maximizing the value of **Secret Technique** and maintaining constant pressure.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Subtlety is about pooling your Energy and Combo Points for your Shadow Dance windows.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Shadow Dance:** Allows the use of all Stealth-only abilities (like Shadowstrike) for a short duration.
*   **Shadow Blades:** A powerful cooldown that increases Combo Point generation and damage.
*   **Master of Shadows:** (If talented) Increases Energy regeneration during Stealth or Shadow Dance.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Shadow Dance:** Cast as soon as **Secret Technique** is available (ideally with 6+ CP).
2.  **Secret Technique:** Your most important finisher. Use at 6+ Combo Points during Shadow Dance.
3.  **Shadowstrike:** Primary builder during Shadow Dance or Stealth.
4.  **Eviscerate / Coup de Grace:** Primary finisher at 6+ Combo Points when Secret Technique is on CD.
5.  **Backstab:** Filler builder outside of Shadow Dance/Stealth.
6.  **Vanish:** Use offensively to re-enter Stealth and use Shadowstrike when Shadow Dance is on CD.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Shadow Blades:** Sync with Shadow Dance.
2.  **Symbols of Death:** Sync with Shadow Dance for Energy and Damage.
3.  **Shadow Dance:** Use when Secret Technique is ready.
4.  **Secret Technique:** Highest priority finisher during Dance.
5.  **Coup de Grace:** Spend 6+ CP.
6.  **Eviscerate:** Spend 6+ CP.
7.  **Shadowstrike:** Use to build CP during Dance/Stealth.
8.  **Backstab:** main builder outside of Dance.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Shuriken Storm:** Primary builder for 3+ targets.
2.  **Secret Technique:** High priority AoE burst during Dance.
3.  **Black Powder:** Primary AoE spender for 3+ targets at 6+ CP.
4.  **Shuriken Tornado:** (If talented) Use for massive burst AoE and CP generation.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Shadow Dance:** 1-minute recharge (2 charges). Allows use of Stealth abilities. **Usage:** The core of the spec. Always pool CP and energy before entering a Dance.
*   **Shadow Blades:** 3-minute cooldown. Increases CP generation and damage. **Usage:** Your "big" burst. Always sync with Shadow Dance.
*   **Symbols of Death:** 30-second cooldown. Increases damage by 10% and generates 40 Energy. **Usage:** Macro with Shadow Dance or use immediately before.
*   **Vanish:** 2-minute cooldown. **Usage:** Offensive cooldown to trigger stealth-related damage bonuses and cast Shadowstrike.
*   **Cloak of Shadows:** 2-minute cooldown. Magic immunity. **Usage:** Defensive.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Agility**
2.  **Haste:** Critical for reducing the cooldown of Secret Technique (via Disorienting Strikes) and for resource regeneration.
3.  **Critical Strike:** Increases throughput and proc frequency.
4.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of your finishers.
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Haste Breakpoints:**
*   **Trickster Hero Talent:** ~700 Haste.
*   **Deathstalker Hero Talent:** ~1100 Haste.

**Note:** For Subtlety, **Haste** and **Crit** are typically the most important secondaries for maintaining the flow of the rotation and maximizing burst.
