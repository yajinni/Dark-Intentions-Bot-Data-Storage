# Destruction Warlock Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Destruction Warlock is a burst-heavy ranged DPS spec that focuses on high-impact fire and shadowflame spells. The gameplay revolves around generating **Soul Shards** with **Incinerate** and **Conflagrate**, and then spending them on massive **Chaos Bolts** or **Rain of Fire**. The spec is defined by its strong 2-target cleave via **Havoc** and its explosive burst during **Summon Infernal**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Destruction is about keeping your target(s) burning and ensuring you don't over-cap Soul Shards while maximizing your Havoc windows.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Immolate:** Your primary DoT. Maintain 100% uptime to generate Soul Shard fragments.
*   **Havoc:** Duplicates your single-target spells onto a secondary target. Essential for 2-target cleave and shard generation.
*   **Backdraft:** Casting Conflagrate reduces the cast time of your next two Incinerates or Chaos Bolts.
*   **Soul Shards:** Your primary resource. Build with Incinerate/Conflagrate and spend on high-impact spells.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Immolate:** Maintain 100% uptime.
2.  **Havoc:** Use on a secondary target whenever available for 2-target cleave.
3.  **Summon Infernal:** Your primary burst cooldown. Use on pull and every 2-3 minutes.
4.  **Chaos Bolt:** Spend Soul Shards (don't cap at 5).
5.  **Conflagrate:** Use to generate Soul Shards and Backdraft stacks.
6.  **Incinerate:** Primary filler to generate Soul Shards.
7.  **Cataclysm:** Use to instantly apply Immolate to multiple targets.

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Summon Infernal:** Primary burst window.
2.  **Cataclysm:** Use to refresh Immolate.
3.  **Shadowburn:** Use if available (especially in execute).
4.  **Chaos Bolt:** Spend 5 Soul Shards immediately.
5.  **Immolate:** Maintain 100% uptime.
6.  **Conflagrate:** Spend charges to avoid capping and for Backdraft.
7.  **Incinerate:** main filler.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Havoc:** Maintain on a secondary target.
2.  **Summon Infernal:** Deals massive AoE damage and generates shards.
3.  **Rain of Fire:** Primary Soul Shard spender for 3+ targets.
4.  **Cataclysm:** Instantly apply Immolate to all targets.
5.  **Channel Demonfire:** (If talented) Use on cooldown after applying Immolate.
6.  **Conflagrate & Incinerate:** Use to build shards quickly via Havoc.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Summon Infernal:** 2-minute or 3-minute cooldown. Deals massive impact damage and pulses fire damage. **Usage:** Your "big" button. It also significantly increases your Soul Shard generation.
*   **Havoc:** 30-second cooldown. Marks a target for 12 seconds. **Usage:** The lynchpin of your cleave damage. Always use when a second target is present.
*   **Cataclysm:** 30-second cooldown. Massive AoE burst and DoT application. **Usage:** Use on cooldown for AoE and to save GCDs on Immolate.
*   **Dark Pact:** 1-minute cooldown. Sacrifices pet health for a massive shield. **Usage:** Primary defensive.
*   **Unending Resolve:** 3-minute cooldown. 40% damage reduction. **Usage:** Major defensive.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Critical Strike:** Critical for massive Chaos Bolt damage and shard generation.
3.  **Haste:** Critical for reducing cast times and increasing the frequency of Shard generation.
4.  **Mastery:** Increases damage by a random amount (always at least a minimum percentage).
5.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Destruction, **Crit** and **Haste** are typically the most important secondaries for maximizing their signature burst damage.
