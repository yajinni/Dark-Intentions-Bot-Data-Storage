# Demonology Warlock Knowledge Base

## Spec Summary
Demonology Warlock is a master of summoning and commanding a horde of demons. The gameplay revolves around generating **Soul Shards** with **Shadow Bolt** or **Demonbolt** and spending them on powerful summons like **Call Dreadstalkers** and **Hand of Gul'dan**. The spec's power peaks when you align all your active demons with your primary cooldown, **Summon Demonic Tyrant**.

---

## Easy Mode Summary
Demonology is about staying active and keeping your basic summons up while ensuring you don't over-cap Soul Shards.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Wild Imps:** Summoned by Hand of Gul'dan. They provide steady damage and can be sacrificed for **Implosion**.
*   **Demonic Core:** Procs from your imps expiring or dreadstalkers fading; makes your next **Demonbolt** instant and generate more shards.
*   **Soul Shards:** Your primary resource. Build with Shadow Bolt/Demonbolt and spend on high-impact summons.

### Simplified Rotation
1.  **Grimoire: Imp Lord:** Use on cooldown (your most powerful summon).
2.  **Call Dreadstalkers:** Use on cooldown (essential for damage and procs).
3.  **Hand of Gul'dan:** Spend 4-5 Soul Shards (don't cap at 5).
4.  **Demonbolt:** Use whenever you have **Demonic Core** procs.
5.  **Power Siphon:** Use to generate Demonic Core stacks if you have imps active.
6.  **Shadow Bolt:** Primary filler to generate Soul Shards.

---

## Rotation Priority

### Single-Target
1.  **Summon Demonic Tyrant:** Primary burst window. Use when Dreadstalkers and a large army of imps are active.
2.  **Grimoire: Imp Lord:** On cooldown.
3.  **Call Dreadstalkers:** Maintain maximum uptime.
4.  **Hand of Gul'dan:** Spend 5 Soul Shards.
5.  **Demonbolt:** Spend procs immediately (don't cap at 4 stacks).
6.  **Shadow Bolt:** main builder.

### Multi-Target (AoE)
1.  **Implosion:** Spend imps when you have 6+ active for massive AoE burst.
2.  **Hand of Gul'dan:** Use to generate imps for Implosion.
3.  **Call Dreadstalkers:** Deals significant cleave damage.
4.  **Demonbolt:** Use procs to build shards quickly.
5.  **Bilescourge Bombers:** (If talented) Use on cooldown for AoE burst.

---

## Major Cooldowns & Usage
*   **Summon Demonic Tyrant:** 1.5-minute or 2-minute cooldown. Extends all active demons by 15 seconds and increases their damage by 15%. **Usage:** The cornerstone of the spec. Always ensure your "big" demons (Dreadstalkers, Vilefiend, etc.) are out before casting Tyrant.
*   **Grimoire: Imp Lord:** 1-minute or 2-minute cooldown. Summons a powerful Felguard. **Usage:** Use on cooldown.
*   **Summon Doomguard:** 3-minute cooldown. **Usage:** Extra burst for long encounters.
*   **Unending Resolve:** 3-minute cooldown. 40% damage reduction. **Usage:** Primary defensive.
*   **Demonic Circle / Gateway:** Essential for mobility and positioning.

---

## Stat Priority
1.  **Intellect**
2.  **Critical Strike ≅ Haste:** These are often interchangeable for the top secondary spot depending on talent builds.
3.  **Mastery:** Increases the damage of all your summoned demons.
4.  **Versatility:** Flat damage boost and reduction.

**Note:** For Demonology, **Haste** is the engine that drives your shard generation, while **Mastery** ensures your army hits as hard as possible.
